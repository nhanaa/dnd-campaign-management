# Playstyle Tips — Bai Jian (Fighter)

## Action Economy Priority

### Round 1 (Closing Distance)
1. **Sudden Charge** (2 actions) — Stride ×2 + Strike to close gap
2. **Dueling Parry** (1 action) — raise AC immediately

### Standard Round (Level 2+)
1. **Snagging Strike** (1 action) — Strike + Off-Guard on hit. Always lead with this.
2. **Strike** (1 action) — benefits from Off-Guard if Snagging hit
3. **Dueling Parry** (1 action) — +2 AC. Non-negotiable until Dueling Dance at 12.

### Burst Round (Level 4+ with Sure Strike)
1. **Sure Strike** (1 action) — roll next attack twice
2. **Snagging Strike** (1 action) — almost guaranteed hit, Off-Guard applied
3. **Strike** (1 action) — benefits from Off-Guard

### Debuff Round
1. **Snagging Strike** (1 action) — Off-Guard
2. **Strike** (1 action)
3. **Demoralize** (1 action) — Frightened stacks with Off-Guard. -2 AC + -1 to everything.

## Key Tactical Notes

### Reactive Strike (AoO)
- Triggers on manipulate actions, move actions (leaving a square), or ranged attacks within reach
- This is your primary zone control. Enemies can't safely cast spells or move past you.
- On a crit, you disrupt manipulate actions (spell casting, item use)

### Snagging Strike
- Requires free hand — never pick up a second weapon or shield
- Off-Guard lasts until start of YOUR next turn — the entire party benefits
- If the enemy steps away from your hand's reach, Off-Guard drops BUT they provoke Reactive Strike. Lose-lose for them.

### Demoralize
- 1 action, Intimidation check vs target's Will DC
- Frightened 1 on success, Frightened 2 on crit
- Target is immune to YOUR Demoralize for 10 minutes after
- Frightened applies penalty to ALL checks and DCs — not just AC. This weakens enemy saves too.

### Dueling Parry
- Circumstance bonus — does NOT stack with Shield
- You MUST be holding only one one-handed weapon and nothing else
- Lasts until start of your next turn

### Dragonblood Fear Immunity
- Success → critical success on fear saves
- You are functionally immune to most Frightened effects
- Hellknights and devils LOVE fear effects. This is your hard counter.

## Breath of the Dragon (Level 7+)

- 15-ft cone (30-ft at level 9 with Formidable Breath), mental damage, Will save
- Recharges in 1d4 rounds — not spammable, use tactically
- Targets Will saves — set up with Frightened first (Dirge or Demoralize), then breathe
- At level 13+: failed save also inflicts Stupefied 1
- Best used against groups of enemies already affected by Dirge of Doom
- The breath is NOT fire — it's the crushing weight of Sovereign Dragon authority. Enemies buckle under imperial will.

### Breath Combo Sequence (Level 9+)
1. Dirge of Doom (enemies Frightened 1, -1 Will saves)
2. Next round: Breath of the Dragon (30-ft cone vs debuffed Will saves)
3. Enemies who fail: full damage + Stupefied 1 (level 13+)
4. Resume normal Snagging Strike → Strike rotation

## Party Synergy

- **Dragon Thaumaturge**: Your core partner. They Exploit Vulnerability for bonus damage, you make the target Off-Guard + Frightened so their Strikes land and crit. Courageous Anthem buffs their attacks further. Two dragon-blooded fighters working in tandem.
- **Goblin Alchemist**: Squishy, mediocre accuracy. Your AoO protects them, Courageous Anthem patches their attack bonus, Off-Guard helps their bombs hit.
- **Goblin Inventor (Danger Dan)**: The construct is your secondary frontline body. Between your AoO zone and Danger Dan's body-blocking, enemies have nowhere safe to go. Off-Guard benefits the construct's Strikes too.
- **Catfolk Witch (Medic)**: Your lifeline. Protect at all costs — stay within 30 ft. Your Frightened debuffs lower enemy saves, making the Witch's spells and hexes land more often. The Witch heals you, you make the Witch more effective. Symbiotic.

## Things to Remember

- Always keep one hand free. Your entire build depends on it.
- Demoralize different targets — each is immune to you for 10 min after.
- Sure Strike is best used on your FIRST attack (no MAP) to fish for crits.
- Courageous Anthem at level 8: cast round 1, Martial Performance extends it when you Strike. Don't recast every round.
- Dirge of Doom vs Courageous Anthem: Dirge for groups (debuff all enemies), Anthem for bosses (buff whole party).
- You have no low-light vision until level 11. Carry torches or use the Light cantrip from Bard Dedication.
- Breath of the Dragon recharges in 1d4 rounds. Don't waste it on a single weak enemy — save it for groups or when Frightened is already applied.
- Penalty types stack: Off-Guard (circumstance) + Frightened (status) + Stupefied (status, different effect) all apply simultaneously.
