# Playstyle Tips — Bai Jian (Fighter + Beastmaster)

## Action Economy Priority

### Round 1 (Closing Distance)
1. **Sudden Charge** (2 actions) — Stride x2 + Strike to close gap
2. **Dueling Parry** (1 action) — raise AC immediately
3. Hound: free Stride to catch up (Mature, no Command needed)

### Standard Round — NOT Flanking (Level 2+)
1. **Snagging Strike** (1 action) — Strike + Off-Guard on hit. Lead with this when no flanking.
2. **Strike** (1 action) — benefits from Off-Guard if Snagging hit
3. **Command Hound** or **Dueling Parry** (1 action) — offense vs defense choice

### Standard Round — Flanking (Level 2+)
Flanking already provides Off-Guard — skip Snagging Strike:
1. **Strike** (1 action) — already benefits from Off-Guard via flanking
2. **Strike** (1 action) — MAP -5, but Off-Guard helps
3. **Command Hound** or **Dueling Parry** (1 action)

### Lockdown Round (Level 8+)
1. **Slam Down / Crashing Slam** (2 actions) — Strike + Trip (auto-Trip at level 10)
2. **Advantageous Assault** (1 action, press) — bonus damage vs Prone target, deals damage even on miss
3. Hound: free Strike (benefits from target being Prone/Off-Guard)

### Hound Grab Round (Level 8+)
1. **Command Hound** (1 action) — hound Strides + jaws Strike → Lock Jaw if hit (auto-Grab)
2. **Strike** (1 action) — target is Off-Guard from Grab
3. **Advantageous Assault** (1 action, press) — bonus damage vs Grabbed target

### Glimpse Setup (Level 9+)
1. **Glimpse Vulnerability** (1 action) — weakness 2 on target for the rest of the fight
2. **Strike** (1 action) — benefits from weakness
3. **Dueling Parry** or **Command** (1 action)
*One-time setup. After this, just Strike normally with free weakness 2.*

### DHA Burst (All levels with DHA)
1. **Dual-Handed Assault** (1 action, flourish) — two-hand d10 + circumstance damage
2. **Strike** (1 action)
3. **Command Hound** or **Dueling Parry** (1 action)
*Use DHA for personal damage when Trip isn't needed or target is Trip-immune.*

### After Dueling Dance (Level 12+)
Parry is permanently free. All 3 actions are offensive:
1. **Crashing Slam** (2 actions) — auto-Prone
2. **Advantageous Assault** (1 action) — bonus damage vs Prone
3. **Command Hound** — free since Parry is automatic
*OR: Strike + Strike + Command for 4 attacks across two MAP tracks.*

## Key Tactical Notes

### Reactive Strike (AoO)
- Triggers on manipulate actions, move actions (leaving a square), or ranged attacks within reach
- Primary zone control. Enemies can't safely cast spells or move past you.
- On a crit, you disrupt manipulate actions (spell casting, item use)
- **Prone enemies who Stand provoke Reactive Strike** — free Strike when they try to recover from Crashing Slam

### Snagging Strike
- Requires free hand — never pick up a second weapon or shield
- Off-Guard lasts until start of YOUR next turn — the entire party benefits
- **When flanking or Moob has Off-Guard covered**: skip Snagging, use Slam Down or DHA instead
- Still useful for ranged allies (Moob's bombs, Panacea's spells) who don't benefit from flanking

### Advantageous Assault (Level 8+)
- Press trait — designed as your 2nd or 3rd action after an initial Strike
- Works vs **Grabbed** (hound Lock Jaw), **Prone** (Crashing Slam), or **Restrained**
- Circumstance bonus to damage = weapon damage dice (+2 at Striking, +3 at Greater Striking)
- **Deals damage even on failure** — guaranteed chip damage equal to weapon dice count
- Best paired with Crashing Slam: Trip (2 actions) → Advantageous Assault (1 action) = full offensive turn with guaranteed damage

### Crashing Slam (Level 10+)
- Auto-Trip on hit. No Athletics check. 100% reliable Prone.
- Prone = Off-Guard to melee + must spend action to Stand + provokes Reactive Strike when Standing
- Your **only** Prone source — Crashing Slam is non-negotiable every fight with Trip-eligible targets

### Glimpse Vulnerability (Level 9+)
- 1 action (manipulate — provokes Reactive Strike from adjacent enemies)
- Target gains weakness 2 to your weapon Strikes. Lasts until you Glimpse a different target.
- One-time setup. Don't re-Glimpse the same target.
- Only affects YOUR Strikes, not Ren's or allies'
- Best used turn 1 or 2 against a boss/priority target, then forget about it and Strike normally

### Dueling Parry
- Circumstance bonus — does NOT stack with Shield
- You MUST be holding only one one-handed weapon and nothing else
- Lasts until start of your next turn
- **Pre-level 12**: choosing between Parry and Command Hound is the core tension
- **Post-level 12 (Dueling Dance)**: Parry is free. Always Command.

### Dragonblood Fear Immunity
- Success → critical success on fear saves (Dragonblood heritage)
- Frightened condition reduced by 1 (Bravery)
- Effectively immune to most fear effects
- Hellknights and devils LOVE fear effects. This is your hard counter.

## Boarhound Tactics

### Hound Action Economy
- **Without Command**: 1 free action (Strike or Stride). No Support.
- **With Command (1 of your actions)**: 2 hound actions. Strike + Strike, Stride + Strike, Strike + Lock Jaw, Support + Stride.
- **Support is usually worse** than Strike + Strike. Only use when hound needs to Stride anyway.
- **Hound has separate MAP** — its Strikes don't affect your MAP and vice versa.

### Lock Jaw Combo (Level 8+)
1. Command Hound → hound jaws Strike (if hit) → Lock Jaw (auto-Grab)
2. Target is Grabbed: Off-Guard + immobilized
3. Your turn: Advantageous Assault for bonus damage vs Grabbed target
4. Target must spend action to Escape — burns their action economy

### Scouting (Level 10+ with Beastmaster Bond)
- Telepathic link within 100 ft
- Send hound ahead. It detects fiends/undead at 40 ft precise scent.
- Hound telepathically reports what it finds without returning.
- Daily prep: choose fiend or undead as designated foe for the day.
- In guerrilla operations: use Ren to sweep buildings, corridors, ambush sites before the party enters.

### Keeping the Hound Alive
- Nimble Boarhound has decent AC (+Dex focus) but medium HP
- Don't leave Ren exposed against enemies that can focus it down
- Panacea can heal Ren (it's an animal, Heal spell works)
- Battle Medicine works on Ren if you're adjacent
- Position Ren to threaten flanking but not be the closest target

## Breath of the Dragon (Level 7+)

- 15-ft cone (30-ft at level 13 with Formidable Breath), mental damage, Will save
- Recharges in 1d4 rounds — not spammable, use tactically
- Targets Will saves — set up with Demoralize first if possible (Frightened penalizes Will)
- At level 15+: failed save also inflicts Stupefied 1
- The breath is NOT fire — it's the crushing weight of Sovereign Dragon authority

## Party Synergy

- **Cyrathul (Thaumaturge)**: Core frontline partner. Both exploit vulnerabilities (yours via Glimpse, his via Exploit). Your Prone + his Strikes = crits.
- **Moob (Alchemist)**: Covers Off-Guard independently via bombs. Your AoO zone protects him. Accept his elixirs/mutagens.
- **Fang + Wing (Inventor)**: Secondary frontline unit. Enemies between you, Wing, and Ren have nowhere safe.
- **Panacea (Witch Medic)**: Your lifeline. She can heal both you and Ren. Your Prone/Grab debuffs lower enemy saves for her spells.

## Armor Plan

- **Levels 1-6**: Lamellar Breastplate (+4 AC, DEX cap +1). Best AC available.
- **Level 7+**: Consider Chain Shirt (+2 AC, DEX cap +3) if Dex is boosted, or stay in breastplate.
- **AC at level 1**: 18 (10 + 3 prof + 1 DEX + 4 armor). With Dueling Parry at Lv2: 20.

## Things to Remember

- Always keep one hand free. Your entire build depends on it.
- Demoralize different targets — each is immune to you for 10 min after.
- Jian's deadly d8 makes crits hit HARD. Fighter accuracy = crit fishing.
- Advantageous Assault deals damage even on failure — always worth using vs Prone/Grabbed targets.
- You have no low-light vision until level 11 (Draconic Sight). Carry torches.
- Breath of the Dragon recharges in 1d4 rounds. Save it for groups or debuffed targets.
- Penalty types stack: Off-Guard (circumstance) + Frightened (status) + Prone (circumstance to attacks) all apply simultaneously.
- **Flanking**: With 3+ melee bodies (you, Cyrathul, Fang+Wing, hound), flanking should be available most rounds.
- **Jian (katana stats)**: 1d6 S, deadly d8, two-hand d10, versatile P. Lower base die than longsword but deadly d8 adds significant crit damage. The right choice for a crit-fishing Fighter.
- **Command an Animal has the concentrate trait** — you cannot Command Ren while raging, confused, or under effects that block concentrate actions.
