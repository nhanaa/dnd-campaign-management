# Build Progression — Bai Jian

> ⚠️ **Level 9 Multitalented pick pending DM approval**. This build has three contingency plans for the Ancestry Paragon slot at level 9 (Exemplar Plan A, Guardian Plan B, or Canny Acumen Plan C). See "Contingency Plans" at the bottom. The level 1-8 core is identical across all three plans, so nothing blocks Session 1.

## Concept

Tian Human (Dragonblood, Sovereign Dragon) **Dueling Fighter** with a jian (katana base, reflavored). One-handed grip + free hand for Dueling Parry chain. **Marshal (Dread path) Free Archetype** provides party damage aura and reactive Prone generation. **Dual-Handed Assault + Slam Down → Crashing Slam** gives Bai Jian two flourish modes: personal damage nuke (DHA, 1 action) and party debuff (Slam Down → Prone, 2 actions). **Sovereign Dragon** ancestry provides mental resistance, fear immunity, and Breath of the Dragon AoE. The dizi (bamboo flute) remains as an heirloom RP prop — Bai Jian plays it in quiet moments in memory of Bai Feng, his missing brother.

## Build Philosophy

- **Dueling style** = one-handed jian (1d6 S, deadly d8, two-hand d10, versatile P, Sword group) + free hand. Dueling Parry for AC, Snagging Strike for Off-Guard on contact, Slam Down/Crashing Slam for Prone, Dread Marshal crit rider for passive Frightened generation. Four debuff axes active at once by level 10.
- **Marshal (Dread path)** = 15-ft emanation aura. Dread Marshal Stance grants allies **+1 status damage scaling with weapon damage dice** (+2 at Striking, +3 at Greater Striking, +4 at Major Striking). Crits auto-apply Frightened 1, feeding Shatter-Defenses-like chains. Enter stance round 1, free for the rest of combat. CHA 14 → 18 scales Intimidation-based stance checks and Steel Yourself/Rallying feats.
- **Dual-Handed Assault at level 4** = jian's two-hand d10 trait + circumstance damage equal to weapon damage dice. Best-in-slot personal damage feat because of the specific weapon choice. 1-action flourish, alternative to Slam Down per turn.
- **Slam Down at level 6** = 2-action flourish (Strike + Athletics Trip). Delayed from level 4 to get DHA online earlier. Slam Down enables the Crashing Slam chain.
- **Crashing Slam at level 10** = auto-Trip on hit, upgrades Slam Down's reliability to 100%. Party-wide Off-Guard via Prone.
- **Topple Foe (Marshal) at level 10** = reactive Prone source. When ally makes a melee Strike against a creature both of you are adjacent to, reactively attempt a Trip. Stacks with Crashing Slam's active Prone for dual Prone generation (your turn + ally turns).
- **Dueling Riposte at level 8** = reaction Strike on enemy crit-fail against Dueling Parry. Debuff-stacked enemies (Frightened 1+ from Dread Marshal, occasional Prone from Slam Down) widen the crit-fail window by ~50%, making Riposte fire ~2-3 times per combat rather than the base ~1.
- **Dueling Dance at level 12** = stance that makes Dueling Parry permanent. Frees up 1 action per turn for the rest of the campaign.
- **Dragonblood (Sovereign)** = fear immunity (crit-success on fear saves), Dragon Lore (free Diplomacy + Intimidation + Dragon Lore), Draconic Resistance (mental damage resistance), Breath of the Dragon (mental AoE via Will save).
- **Party role** = frontline debuffer/buffer alongside Dragon Thaumaturge. Dread Marshal + Prone + Frightened + Off-Guard = 4 overlapping debuff axes covering the party's attacks. Three melee bodies (Bai Jian, Thaumaturge, Construct) enable frequent flanking and trigger Topple Foe reliably.

## Combat Loops

### Levels 1–3 (Snagging Strike era, pre-archetype unlocks)

Core rotation:
- **Standard**: Snagging Strike → Strike → Dueling Parry
- **Aggressive**: Snagging Strike → Strike → Demoralize
- **Opener**: Sudden Charge (Stride ×2 + Strike) → Dueling Parry

*Note: Marshal Dedication at level 2 grants a 15-ft fear-save aura passively, but Dread Marshal Stance isn't unlocked until level 4.*

### Levels 4–5 (DHA online, Dread Marshal active)

**Round 1 setup**: Enter Dread Marshal Stance (Intimidation, auto-pass easy DC of level) → Snagging Strike → Dueling Parry
**Damage mode**: DHA (Strike at MAP 0 with jian's two-hand d10 + circumstance damage) → Strike → Dueling Parry
**Party debuff mode**: Snagging Strike (Off-Guard on hit) → Strike → Demoralize (for Shatter Defenses chain)
**Opener**: Sudden Charge → Dueling Parry (round 1 closer)

*Dread Marshal crits automatically apply Frightened 1 to targets — allies' crits fuel the fear engine without Bai Jian spending actions.*

### Levels 6–7 (Slam Down online)

**Round 1 setup**: Enter Dread Marshal Stance → Sudden Charge → (Parry on round 2)
**Slam Down mode** (party debuff priority): Slam Down (Strike + Trip, 2 actions) → Dueling Parry. Prone applied ~80-90% reliability with Assurance Athletics. Party gets -2 AC vs target.
**DHA mode** (personal damage priority): DHA → Strike → Dueling Parry. Higher personal damage, no Prone. Best for when you don't need debuffs or target is Trip-immune.
**Pick which flourish based on the encounter**: Party-heavy target = Slam Down. Solo target or Trip-immune = DHA.

*Shatter-Defenses-style Off-Guard chain now runs: Dread Marshal crit rider applies Frightened → your next Strike exploits Frightened-weakened saves → Off-Guard from Prone compounds.*

### Levels 8–9 (Dueling Riposte online)

**Round 1 setup**: Enter Dread Marshal Stance → Slam Down → Dueling Parry
**Standard**: Slam Down (or DHA) → Strike → Dueling Parry (always have Parry up for Riposte trigger)
**Riposte expected triggers**: 1-2 per combat, more against debuffed enemies. Each trigger = free reaction Strike at MAP 0 vs enemy who just crit-failed.
**Dread Marshal + Frightened 1 widens crit-fail window** by ~5% → Riposte fires more often than baseline.

*Reactive Strike + Dueling Riposte can both fire in the same round (different triggers), giving you up to 2 reactions worth of free Strikes per turn.*

### Levels 10–11 (Crashing Slam + Topple Foe)

**Primary**: Crashing Slam (Slam Down upgraded — auto-Trip on hit, 2 actions) → Strike → Dueling Parry
**Combo setup**: Demoralize (Frightened 1) → Crashing Slam (auto-Prone + Off-Guard + spirit damage) → Dueling Parry
**Standing provokes Reactive Strike** — if the enemy Stands to escape Prone, AoO triggers a free Strike. They only have 2 actions left.
**Topple Foe reactive Prone**: When ally (Dragon Thaumaturge, Construct) makes a melee Strike against a creature you're both adjacent to, you reactively Trip. Secondary Prone source beyond your own turn.

*Two Prone axes running simultaneously: your Crashing Slam on your turn + Topple Foe on ally turns. Enemy always has to spend actions standing up, which provokes Reactive Strike, which is another free Strike for Bai Jian.*

### Level 12 (Dueling Dance — Parry permanently free)

**Dueling Dance** makes Dueling Parry permanently active — you have 3 free offensive actions per turn now.

**Max damage turn**: Crashing Slam → Strike → Strike (3 full offensive actions, Parry is automatic).
**Full debuff turn**: Demoralize → Crashing Slam → Strike. Enemy is Frightened + Prone + Off-Guard.
**Coordinated Charge turn** (Marshal): Stride + Strike against priority target. On hit, all allies within 60 ft can reactively Stride toward the target. Mass engage on round 1 of boss fights.
**Dueling Riposte + Reactive Strike + Topple Foe** all available as reactions, stacked across enemy turns.

### Debuff Chain Priority
- **Levels 1–5**: Snagging Strike (Off-Guard on hit)
- **Levels 6–9**: Slam Down Trip (~65-80% reliable with Assurance Athletics at level 6) + Snagging Strike fallback
- **Level 10+**: Crashing Slam (auto-Prone on hit) as primary, Snagging Strike as backup, Topple Foe as reactive secondary

### Combat Flexibility (Level 9+)
Fighter's Combat Flexibility class feature lets you "rent" a 1st-8th level Fighter feat per day. Use this to swap in:
- **Blind-Fight** on days with invisible/concealed enemies
- **Lunge** on days with reach enemies
- **Combat Grab** on days with spellcasters to hold
- **Felling Strike** on days with flying enemies
- **Brutish Shove** on days requiring positioning

### The Breath + Fear + Crashing Slam Combo (Level 10+)

Round 1: Dread Marshal Stance → Sudden Charge

**Dex is capped at +1 by breastplate** — boosting Dex above 12 provides no AC benefit (only marginal Reflex save / initiative improvements). Drop Dex from the boost cycles entirely and put those slots into **Wis** for Will save and Perception scaling.

### Save Profile at Level 10-11
- **Fort**: Expert (master at 9 via Juggernaut) + Con +4 + level 10 = +20 at 10, +22 at 11 with master
- **Reflex**: Expert + Dex +1 + level 10 = +17
- **Will**: Expert (via Bravery at 3) + Wis +2 + level 10 = +18 → +20 at level 11 with Canny Acumen

Will save is now genuinely defended — patched from trained (+14) to expert with Wis boost (+20). The Wis-for-Dex swap gives +2 Will and +2 Perception vs -2 Reflex (capped by armor anyway).

## Contingency Plans (Level 9 Multitalented Slot)

The level 9 Ancestry Paragon slot uses Multitalented to grant a 2nd-level multiclass dedication for free. Three options depending on DM rulings:

### Plan A: Exemplar Dedication [RARE — War of Immortals]
**Pending DM approval.** Grants one ikon; recommended pick is **Gleaming Blade**:
- Immanence: +2 spirit damage per weapon damage die on every jian Strike
- Transcendence (Flowing Spirit Strike): 2-action nova combining two Strikes into one spirit damage packet, weakness/resistance applied once
- Shift Immanence is free on initiative, once per round otherwise
- Later archetype feats: Exemplar Resiliency (lvl 10), Basic Glory → Vow of Mortal Defiance (lvl 12, +1d6 spirit vs unholy/holy targets), Advanced Glory → Flow of War (lvl 14, 4-action turn 1/hour), Advanced Glory → Additional Ikon (lvl 16, if DM allows RAW interpretation)
- Trade-off: Rare content, requires DM to read the War of Immortals Exemplar rules

### Plan B: Guardian Dedication [Remaster, Battlecry!]
**Safer fallback.** Grants the **Taunt** action:
- 30-ft tag: taunted enemy attacking allies instead of you takes -1 circumstance attack + Off-Guard
- Later archetype feats: Guardian Resiliency (lvl 10), Guardian's Intercept (lvl 12, reaction to take physical damage for an ally), Basic Defender → Long-Distance Taunt (lvl 14), Advanced Defender (lvl 16)
- Trade-off: Less offensive than Exemplar, but no Rare content barrier and the Guardian path is entirely Remaster-legal

### Plan C: General Training → Canny Acumen (Will) [No Dedication]
**If both Exemplar and Guardian are declined** or if Bai Jian wants to drop the second-archetype architecture entirely:
- Take Canny Acumen (Will) via General Training ancestry feat
- Use level 11 general feat slot for something else (Toughness already taken, so maybe Incredible Initiative or Fast Recovery)
- Frees up Free Archetype slots 10-16 for more Marshal feats instead of Exemplar/Guardian feats
