# Build Progression — Bai Jian

## Concept

Tian Human (Dragonblood, Sovereign Dragon) **Dueling Fighter** with a jian (katana base, reflavored). One-handed grip + free hand for Dueling Parry chain. **Beastmaster Free Archetype** provides an **Isgeri Boarhound** companion — a hunting hound bred to track fiends and undead, with precise scent and Lock Jaw grab. **Thaumaturge Dedication** (via Multitalented at level 9) adds Glimpse Vulnerability for personal damage via weakness application. **Dual-Handed Assault + Slam Down → Crashing Slam** gives Bai Jian two flourish modes: personal damage nuke (DHA, 1 action) and trip lockdown (Crashing Slam → Advantageous Assault, 2 actions). **Sovereign Dragon** ancestry provides mental resistance, fear immunity, and Breath of the Dragon AoE. The dizi (bamboo flute) remains as an heirloom RP prop — Bai Jian plays it in quiet moments in memory of Bai Feng, his missing brother.

## Build Philosophy

- **Dueling style** = one-handed jian (1d6 S, deadly d8, two-hand d10, versatile P, Sword group) + free hand. Dueling Parry for AC, Snagging Strike for Off-Guard on contact, Crashing Slam for auto-Prone, Advantageous Assault for bonus damage against locked-down targets. Crit fishing with Fighter accuracy + deadly d8.
- **Beastmaster (Isgeri Boarhound)** = Nimble companion with precise fiend/undead scent (40 ft, daily designation). Lock Jaw auto-Grab on successful jaws Strike. Telepathic scouting via Beastmaster Bond (level 10). The hound acts independently via Mature's free 1-action Strike/Stride, or gets 2 actions when Commanded (costs 1 of Bai Jian's actions — better than a MAP -10 Strike). Nature's Precision adds +1d4 precision damage (+2d4 specialized) on the hound's finesse jaws vs Off-Guard targets.
- **Thaumaturge (Weapon implement)** = Glimpse Vulnerability (1 action, manipulate) applies weakness 2 to your weapon Strikes against a target. One-time setup per target, lasts the whole fight. Jian = implement, no hand conflict. At level 12, Resolute patches Will saves to master.
- **Dual-Handed Assault at level 4** = jian's two-hand d10 trait + circumstance damage equal to weapon damage dice. Best-in-slot personal damage feat because of the specific weapon choice. 1-action flourish.
- **Slam Down at level 6** = 2-action flourish (Strike + Athletics Trip). Delayed from level 4 to get DHA online earlier. Slam Down enables the Crashing Slam chain.
- **Advantageous Assault at level 8** = 1-action press. Strike vs Grabbed/Prone/Restrained target with circumstance bonus to damage equal to weapon dice count (+2 Striking, +3 Greater Striking). Deals damage even on failure. Direct synergy with Crashing Slam (Prone) and the hound's Lock Jaw (Grabbed).
- **Crashing Slam at level 10** = auto-Trip on hit, upgrades Slam Down's reliability to 100%. Party-wide Off-Guard via Prone.
- **Dueling Dance at level 12** = stance that makes Dueling Parry permanent. Frees up 1 action per turn for the rest of the campaign. The build's biggest power spike — permanent Parry means Bai Jian can always Command the hound without sacrificing defense.
- **Dragonblood (Sovereign)** = fear immunity (crit-success on fear saves), Dragon Lore (free Diplomacy + Intimidation + Dragon Lore), Draconic Resistance (mental damage resistance), Breath of the Dragon (mental AoE via Will save).
- **Party role** = frontline striker/debuffer with a scouting companion. Crashing Slam Prone + hound Lock Jaw Grab + Off-Guard from flanking/Moob's bombs = targets are locked down and debuffed. The hound provides fiend/undead detection and telepathic intel for the guerrilla campaign.

## Combat Loops

### Levels 1-3 (Snagging Strike era, pre-companion maturity)

Core rotation:
- **Standard**: Snagging Strike → Strike → Dueling Parry
- **Aggressive**: Snagging Strike → Strike → Command Hound (hound Strides or Strikes)
- **Opener**: Sudden Charge (Stride x2 + Strike) → Dueling Parry

*Note: Hound is young at levels 1-3. Must Command every round for it to act. At level 4, Mature grants the hound a free 1-action Strike/Stride without Commanding.*

### Levels 4-5 (DHA online, Mature hound)

**Round 1**: Sudden Charge → Dueling Parry. Hound free Strides to catch up.
**Damage mode**: DHA (two-hand d10 + circumstance damage) → Strike → Dueling Parry. Hound free Strikes.
**Command mode**: Strike → Strike → Command Hound (Stride + Strike or Strike + Strike). Better than MAP -10 third Strike.
**Defensive mode**: Strike → Strike → Dueling Parry. Hound free Strikes independently.

### Levels 6-7 (Slam Down online, Breath of the Dragon)

**Slam Down mode** (trip priority): Slam Down (Strike + Trip, 2 actions) → Dueling Parry or Command. Hound free Strikes if not Commanded.
**DHA mode** (personal damage priority): DHA → Strike → Dueling Parry. Hound free Strikes.
**Pick which flourish based on the encounter**: Trip-eligible target = Slam Down. Trip-immune or personal damage needed = DHA.

### Levels 8-9 (Advantageous Assault online, Thaumaturge Dedication, Combat Flexibility)

**Lockdown combo**: Slam Down (Strike + Trip) → Advantageous Assault (press, bonus damage vs Prone). Hound free Strikes or Strides.
**Hound grab combo**: Command Hound (hound Stride + jaws Strike → Lock Jaw if hit) → Strike → Advantageous Assault (press, bonus damage vs Grabbed target).
**Glimpse setup**: Glimpse Vulnerability (1 action, weakness 2 on target) → Strike → Strike or Dueling Parry. One-time setup, then free bonus damage all fight.
**Full lockdown**: Glimpse Vulnerability → Slam Down (Prone) → hound free Strikes. Next round: Advantageous Assault (vs Prone, with weakness 2 active) → Strike → Command/Parry.

### Levels 10-11 (Crashing Slam + Beastmaster Bond)

**Primary**: Crashing Slam (auto-Prone on hit, 2 actions) → Advantageous Assault (press, bonus damage vs Prone) or Command Hound. Hound free Strikes.
**Full lockdown**: Crashing Slam (Prone) → Command Hound (hound jaws + Lock Jaw → Grabbed + Prone). Target is Off-Guard + immobile + action-taxed.
**Scouting**: Send hound ahead with Beastmaster Bond telepathy + precise fiend/undead scent 40 ft. Receive intel without the hound returning.
**Standing provokes Reactive Strike**: Enemy Stands from Prone → Reactive Strike triggers free Strike. They only have 2 actions left.

### Level 12+ (Dueling Dance — Parry permanently free)

**Dueling Dance** makes Dueling Parry permanently active — you have 3 free offensive actions per turn.

**Max lockdown turn**: Crashing Slam (auto-Prone) → Advantageous Assault (bonus damage vs Prone) → Command Hound (Strike + Lock Jaw). All with permanent +2 AC.
**Glimpse + assault**: Glimpse Vulnerability → Crashing Slam → Advantageous Assault. Target has weakness 2, is Prone, takes bonus damage.
**Full Command turn**: Strike → Strike → Command Hound (2 actions). 4 attacks across two MAP tracks. Permanent Parry.
**DHA burst**: DHA (two-hand d10 nuke) → Strike → Command Hound. Personal damage + hound damage.

### Combat Flexibility (Level 9+)
Fighter's Combat Flexibility class feature lets you "rent" a 1st-8th level Fighter feat per day. Use this to swap in:
- **Blind-Fight** on days with invisible/concealed enemies
- **Felling Strike** on days with flying enemies
- **Lunge** on days with reach enemies
- **Combat Grab** on days with spellcasters to hold
- **Brutish Shove** on days requiring positioning
- **Dueling Riposte** on days when you expect to be targeted heavily (fewer melee allies present)

### Isgeri Boarhound Details

- **Name**: Ren (刃) — "Blade edge." A working blade to complement Bai Jian's jian. The hunter and his cutting edge.
- **Source**: Pathfinder #222: Hellbreakers (the AP Kaazz is running)
- **Size**: Small → Medium (Mature) — stays Medium with Nimble
- **Attacks**: Jaws 1d8 piercing (finesse) → 2d8 at Mature
- **Speed**: 40 ft
- **Senses**: Foe scent (precise, 40 ft vs designated fiend/undead), scent (imprecise, 40 ft)
- **Special**: During daily prep, designate fiend or undead as the hound's foe. Precise scent detects that creature type at 40 ft — through walls, darkness, invisibility.
- **Support Benefit**: +1 circumstance bonus to your next Strike vs a foe in the hound's reach. If designated foe, additional 1d6 piercing (2d6 nimble/savage). Note: Support costs 1 of the hound's 2 Commanded actions, and the hound can only Stride (not Strike) after Supporting. Usually better to have the hound Strike + Strike or Strike + Lock Jaw instead.
- **Advanced Maneuver — Lock Jaw**: After a successful jaws Strike, target is automatically Grabbed (Off-Guard + immobilized, no Athletics check). Unlocked at Incredible (level 8).
- **Nimble Progression**: +2 Dex, +1 Str/Con/Wis, Expert Acrobatics, +2 flat damage, magical attacks. Finesse jaws use Dex for attacks — Nimble boosts both accuracy and AC.

### Action Economy with the Hound

**Support rules (important):**
- Support is 1 of the hound's 2 Commanded actions
- After Supporting, the hound can ONLY use basic move actions (Stride) — NOT Strike
- If the hound already took a non-move action, it can't Support
- The free Mature action (Strike or Stride) CANNOT be used for Support
- Support is usually worse than Strike + Strike or Strike + Lock Jaw. Use it only when the hound needs to Stride into position anyway.

**When NOT Commanding (hound acts free):**
- Hound gets 1 free action: Strike (if adjacent to enemy) or Stride (if not)
- Realistically the hound Strides ~half the time and Strikes ~half the time
- Bai Jian uses all 3 actions for himself (Strike + Strike + Dueling Parry, or flourish combos)

**When Commanding (costs 1 of your actions):**
- Hound gets 2 actions: Stride + Strike, Strike + Strike, Strike + Lock Jaw, etc.
- Better than your MAP -10 third Strike. The hound has its own MAP track.
- Trade-off: you lose Dueling Parry or one of your own Strikes

**After Dueling Dance (level 12):**
- Parry is permanently free. You can ALWAYS Command without sacrificing defense.
- Full turn: Strike + Strike + Command → hound Strike + Strike/Lock Jaw, with permanent +2 AC.

## Thaumaturge Dedication (Level 9 via Multitalented, Ancestry Paragon)

- **Implement**: Weapon (jian). No hand conflict — the jian IS the implement.
- **Glimpse Vulnerability**: 1 action (manipulate — provokes Reactive Strike from adjacent enemies). Select a creature → it gains **weakness 2** to your unarmed and weapon Strikes. Lasts until you Glimpse a different target. One-time setup per fight.
- **Level 12 (Free Archetype)**: **Resolute** — Will saves increase to master. Prereq: expert Will (Fighter Bravery at level 3 ✓).
- **Spellcasting**: Trained in thaumaturge spell attack/DC (keyed on Charisma). Relevant if you take spellcasting feats later.
- **Esoterica**: You gain esoterica, allowing esoterica actions from Thaumaturge feats if taken later.
