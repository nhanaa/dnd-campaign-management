## Appearance

A tall, lean swordsman. Warm tan skin with a faint golden undertone. Black hair pulled back in a practical half-knot. Deep amber-gold eyes with a draconic gleam that most people can't quite place. No horns, no scales — the dragon blood is internal, only surfacing in moments of intensity. Wears a lacquered steel lamellar breastplate over dark traveling robes with silk cord ties. A straight jian on his back. Everything about him says "long road."

## Backstory

Bai Jian comes from a family of hunters in Lingshen, Tian Xia, specializing in hunting down the region's most dangerous creatures: oni, yaoguai, evil spirits, and worse. He carries Sovereign Dragon blood, ancient imperial magic that runs in the family.

He had an older brother, Bai Feng. The best of them, a master of the sword and a renowned hunter across their homeland. Bai Feng taught him not only how to wield a blade, but how to think like a hunter. Five years ago, a strange creature appeared, wreaking havoc across a town in Lingshen. The two brothers took on the mission to hunt it down, but in the process, Bai Jian suffered a severe injury, and his brother vanished.

The creature was unlike anything he had ever seen: infernal, feral, and scorched. He had to find his brother. So he set out on a journey, learned what the creature truly was, and traced its origins to Isger. There, he learned of the Hellbreakers, rebels fighting the same infernal forces that took his sibling.

He joined up. The cause is real, and he knows what tyranny looks like from the history of his own home. But he'd be lying if he said this was just about Isger. He still holds onto the hope that one day, he might reunite with his brother.

## Personality

- **Attitude**: Quiet and measured. Speaks when he has something worth saying. Observes first, acts decisively. Not cold, just controlled. Warms up slowly, but once you've earned his trust, he's ride-or-die.
- **Beliefs**: A blade drawn carelessly is worse than no blade at all. Tyranny is the same everywhere. Family isn't just blood, it's who you choose to fight beside.
- **Edicts**: Finish what you start. Protect those who can't protect themselves. Never stop searching. Honor the people who taught you by becoming better than they were.
- **Anathema**: Abandoning someone who depends on you. Making bargains with fiends. Cruelty toward the powerless. Giving up on someone before you have proof they're gone.
- **Likes**: A well-maintained weapon, quiet mornings, tea, people who say what they mean, high ground with a clear view.
- **Dislikes**: Unnecessary cruelty, people who talk about honor but don't live it, wasted potential, the cold (Isger is nothing like Lingshen), being pitied.
