## Party Composition Notes

- **Frontline**: Bai Jian (Fighter) — the ONLY dedicated melee frontliner
- **Healing**: Catfolk Witch (Medic dedication)
- **Damage**: Leshy Kineticist (fire/wood blasts), Goblin Inventor (construct vehicle)
- **Face/Social**: Bai Jian (Diplomacy, Intimidation, CHA 14, Performance via Bard)
- **Buffs**: Bai Jian (Courageous Anthem at level 8 — +1 attack/damage/fear saves party-wide)
- **Debuffs**: Bai Jian (Demoralize, Snagging Strike Off-Guard, Dirge of Doom at level 10)
- **Divine**: None. No dedicated divine caster.
- **Knowledge**: Bai Jian (Dragon Lore, Religion, Survival), Kineticist (Nature likely), Witch (Occultism likely)

## Party Gaps

- No dedicated healer beyond Witch/Medic
- No divine caster for anti-fiend spells (Bai Jian covers this through sheer martial power + fear immunity)
- Only one frontliner — Bai Jian needs to hold the line alone
- The construct vehicle (Danger Dan) may act as a secondary body on the field

## Party Synergy

### Bai Jian + Catfolk Witch (Medic)
- Protect the healer at all costs. Stay within 30 ft when possible.
- Witch likely has debuffs/hexes — Bai Jian's Off-Guard + Frightened reduce enemy saves for the Witch's spells.

### Bai Jian + Leshy Kineticist
- Kineticist is likely a ranged blaster. Bai Jian holds the line so the Kineticist can blast safely.
- Reactive Strike punishes anything trying to get past Bai Jian to the Kineticist.
- Courageous Anthem buffs the Kineticist's attack rolls.

### Bai Jian + Goblin Inventor (Danger Dan)
- The construct vehicle could serve as a secondary frontline body.
- If Danger Dan charges in, enemies are caught between Bai Jian's AoO zone and the construct.
- Snagging Strike Off-Guard benefits the Inventor's attacks.

### Fear Economy
- Bai Jian is the fear engine: Demoralize + Dirge of Doom + Shatter Defenses
- The whole party benefits from enemies being Frightened (penalty to everything) and Off-Guard (-2 AC)
- Bai Jian is immune to fear himself (Dragonblood + Bravery), so enemy fear effects are one-directional
