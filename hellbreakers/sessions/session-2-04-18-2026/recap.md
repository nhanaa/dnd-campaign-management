## Session Summary

Picking up from the warehouse cliffhanger, the party made quick work of the Chelaxian loyalists who had captured Gertraud Middenhelm. After interrogating the sole survivor and sending him limping back with a warning, they rescued Gertraud, collected loot from the warehouse, and escorted her back to the Hellbreakers HQ at Saringallow.

The following day, Rhokrag briefed them on their next mission: intercept a Chelaxian cargo ship on the North Kansira River and seize supplies for the cause — but minimize killing. The party hatched a fake-prisoner ruse, with Moob and a disguised Cyrathul pretending to turn in "captured Hellbreaker" Bai Jian, while Fang and Panacea hid on the cliffs above. The deception got them on board, but the stealth team blew their cover when Wing knocked a boulder into the river.

What followed was a brutal Extreme-difficulty ship boarding against Captain Julenus Aedin, his animated armor bodyguard, and hardened swabbies. The animated armor resisted all physical damage types and Fang went down twice — once to the armor's glaive, once to a swabby's relentless fists. Moob's tenderizer grenades cracked the armor's resistance, Cyrathul bit the captain's neck clean through, and Bai Jian's grapple-reposition-shove sent the armor into the river. When the armor hit the water, its failsafe detonated — setting the entire ship ablaze. In the ensuing chaos, the party fought fires with water bombs, secured supplies, rescued trapped crew members, and barely escaped the sinking ship. Both goblins nearly drowned in the escape; Moob sacrificed HP to push Fang above water.

- The party camped at a rocky outcropping overlooking a river bend — deep channel on one side, shallow on the other, ships forced close to the cliff

### Act 7 — Ship Combat (Extreme Encounter)
- See **Combat Encounters** below for round-by-round detail
- Captain Julenus Aedin and his animated armor bodyguard proved devastating
- **128 XP awarded** (Extreme difficulty)

### Act 8 — The Burning Ship (Skill Challenge)
- The animated armor's failsafe detonated, setting the ship on fire from below
- **Moob**: Water bombs to fight fires (2 successes), looted captain's coin purse, diplomacy to evacuate remaining crew
- **Cyrathul**: Esoteric Lore to identify and secure valuable supplies
- **Bai Jian**: Athletics to rescue trapped crew members (lifted a collapsed wall, broke down a door), secured supplies, told rescued sailors to spread the word that "the Hellbreakers are not all bad"
- **Panacea**: Crit athletics to leap from the cliff onto the burning deck (triple somersault), healed Fang, secured supplies via Thievery
- **Fang**: Crafting check to push Wing off the ship into the water for later salvage
- **+70 bonus XP** (40 for crew rescue, 30 for supplies)

### Act 9 — The Drowning
- The ship sank. Bai Jian and Cyrathul swam to shore easily
- Moob, Fang, and Panacea struggled — goblins can't swim, cat hates water
- Panacea made it on her second attempt
- Fang kept rolling 4s; Moob voluntarily took 5 damage (critical failure) to push Fang above water
- Bai Jian threw rope from shore (hero-pointed the athletics check) — both goblins grabbed on and were pulled to safety

## Combat Encounters

### Encounter 1 — Warehouse Fight (2 rounds, 88 XP)

**Enemies**: 3 Chelaxian Loyalists — two with crossbows, one leader (shouted "Fragilia!")

**Round 1**:
- Panacea attempted Deception ("Oh hello, we just fell through the door") — failed. Moved forward, hit a loyalist with Needle Darts
- Moob strode twice and threw a Tallow Bomb — persistent fire damage + Goblin Burnit splash
- Loyalist 1 fired crossbow at Moob, reloaded, fired again
- Loyalist 2 stabbed at Moob, stepped, reloaded
- Leader shouted "Fragilia!" and attacked Bai Jian — Bai Jian dodged
- Cyrathul placed Mirror Cloak image for flanking, Exploited Vulnerability (failed Esoterica), struck the leader hard
- Bai Jian critical Snagging Strike through the leader's face (25 damage, killing blow) — the flanking off-guard from Cyrathul's Mirror turned the hit into a crit. "You dare draw your weapon against me"
- Bai Jian Sudden Charged Loyalist 2, another critical hit (19 damage, killing blow) — "Cut off one of his arm and kicked him in the face"
- Loyalist 3 surrendered, threw down crossbow, fell to his knees

**Round 2 (mop-up)**:
- Fang (on Wing) flew in, gave Wing 3 actions — Wing strode twice and grabbed the dropped crossbow in his jaws
- Combat ended via surrender acceptance

### Encounter 2 — Night's Prize Ship Boarding (Extreme, 128 XP + 70 bonus)

**Enemies**: 2 Night's Prize Swabbies (fists), Captain Julenus Aedin (dagger), Animated Armor (glaive, all-physical-damage resistance, failsafe explosive)

**Pre-combat**: Ruse partially succeeded — Moob, Cyrathul (disguised), and Bai Jian (fake prisoner) boarded. Wing knocked a boulder revealing the ambush team.

**Round 1**:
- Panacea: Needle Darts into the sails (shredding them)
- Swabby 1: Two punches at Bai Jian — one connected hard
- Swabby 2: Stepped toward Moob, two swings — both missed (over Moob's head)
- Moob: Ran through Swabby 2's legs, strode to stern, kicked the anchor winch free (failed first Athletics, succeeded second) — ship began drifting
- Cyrathul: Dismissed disguise, unfurled wings, critically Demoralized the captain (Frightened 2), struck Swabby 1 with sword pommel (non-lethal)
- Bai Jian: Snagging Strike on Swabby 1 (non-lethal, off-guard), second strike knocked him out
- Fang (on Wing): Rode onto the ship (70 ft movement), non-lethal wheel attack on Swabby 2, hero-pointed the hit. Goblin gauntlet strike (crit, "pitiful little goblin damage")

**Round 2** (Captain and Armor emerge):
- Captain Aedin emerged from below deck with the animated armor: "What the hell do you think you're doing on my boat?"
- Animated Armor: Two glaive strikes on Fang — **Fang went down** (first down of the campaign)
- Captain: Dagger stab at Moob (one hit, one miss)
- Panacea: Moved closer on rocks, 2-action Heal on Fang at range — brought him back
- Swabby 2: Stood up, punched Wing twice — surprisingly hard hits ("Consecutive serious punches")
- Moob: Critical Tenderizer Grenade on animated armor (11 acid + splash, reduced physical resistance, off-guard to bite attacks), Parry stance with Wallop Stick, attempted non-lethal strike on captain (miss)
- Cyrathul: Strode to armor area. Attempted to bite the animated armor twice (off-guard from Tenderizer) — missed both, even after spending a hero point. "I tried. That's my turn."
- Bai Jian: Flanked animated armor, struck it with piercing — minimal damage due to resistance. Moved away.
- Fang: Dismounted Wing (prone). Pressed the red button — **Exploded Wing** (2 actions). Animated armor critically succeeded the Reflex save (zero damage). Fang took self-damage. "This is going horribly wrong in the best way"

**Round 3**:
- Animated Armor: Three strikes at Cyrathul (one hit, two misses)
- Captain: First attack brought **Moob down**. DM redirected second attack to Cyrathul (retconned targeting a downed character)
- Panacea: Moved on rocks, 2-action Heal on Moob at range — brought him back
- Swabby 2: Attacked Fang/Wing area — brutal hits. **Fang went down a second time** (now Wounded 2)
- Moob: Stood up, Quick Alchemy Tenderizer Grenade on captain (hit — made captain off-guard to bites), Parry stance
- Cyrathul: Exploit Vulnerability on captain (failed, hero-pointed, succeeded — Personal Antithesis). Bit captain with jaws (off-guard from Tenderizer) — hit. Second bite — hit. **DM: "How do you want to do this?"** Killing blow — chomped straight through the captain's neck. 
- Bai Jian: Killed the remaining swabby ("Pierce him through the stomach" — lethal, no more non-lethal). Moved to animated armor, Grapple — success.
- Fang: Dying recovery check — **natural 20** ("How about that?"). Stable at 0 HP.

**Round 4**:
- Animated Armor: Tried to escape grapple three times — all failed
- Panacea: Needle Darts on armor (hero-pointed, still resisted). Cast Guidance on Moob.
- Moob: Quick Alchemy bomb on armor (resisted). Create a Diversion — "Hey shiny rust bucket, look what I'm doing to your master!" (poured acid on the captain's corpse) — failed. Second attempt with words — succeeded.
- Cyrathul: Attempted Demoralize on armor (no mind — wasted). Exploit Vulnerability on armor (crit fail — still got Personal Antithesis). Longsword strike — miss.
- Bai Jian: **Reposition** armor toward the edge → **Step** → **Shove** it overboard. "Goodbye, frickin' armor." Splash. Boom. **Failsafe detonated** — ship caught fire from below.

**Post-combat skill challenge** (see Act 8 above): 6 rounds of fire-fighting, supply-securing, crew-rescuing as the ship burned and sank.

### Things Bai Jian knows that the party does not
- Nothing secret this session. Everything was shared.
- The animated armor's failsafe was unexpected. A merchant ship shouldn't have that kind of enchantment. Someone spent real money protecting that cargo — or that captain.

### What Bai Jian is thinking about
- **The armor's resistance**. The jian couldn't cut it. Even piercing barely scratched it. **Cyrathul** killed the captain with Exploit Vulnerability + bite combo after Moob's Tenderizer — that's the playbook. But for constructs with no weakness, the party has no good answer without the alchemist's grenades.
- **Non-lethal is expensive**. The -2 circumstance penalty nearly cost the party against the swabbies. **Rhokrag wants minimal killing**, but the mission escalated fast. The captain and armor forced lethal response.
- **Moob and Fang**. Moob pushed Fang above water at cost to himself. That bond is real. Keep them close in formation — if one goes down, the other will take risks.
- **Panacea's healing range**. She had to spend actions repositioning twice to reach Fang with Heal. Only two Heal spell slots. The party needs to stay tighter or accept that ranged healing is limited.
- **Bai Jian told the sailors to spread the word**. Rhokrag wanted hearts and minds. Rescuing crew from a fire we partially caused is complicated — but they saw us save them. That has to count for something.
