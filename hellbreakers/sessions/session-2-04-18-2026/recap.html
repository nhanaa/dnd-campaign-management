Session Summary

Picking up from the warehouse cliffhanger, the party made quick work of the Chelaxian loyalists who had captured Gertraud Middenhelm. After interrogating the sole survivor and sending him limping back with a warning, they rescued Gertraud, collected loot from the warehouse, and escorted her back to the Hellbreakers HQ at Saringallow.

The following day, Rhokrag briefed them on their next mission: intercept a Chelaxian cargo ship on the North Kansira River and seize supplies for the cause — but minimize killing. The party hatched a fake-prisoner ruse, with Moob and a disguised Cyrathul pretending to turn in "captured Hellbreaker" Bai Jian, while Fang and Panacea hid on the cliffs above. The deception got them on board, but the stealth team blew their cover when Wing knocked a boulder into the river.

What followed was a brutal Extreme-difficulty ship boarding against Captain Julenus Aedin, his animated armor bodyguard, and hardened swabbies. The animated armor resisted all physical damage types and Fang went down twice — once to the armor's glaive, once to a swabby's relentless fists. Moob's tenderizer grenades cracked the armor's resistance, Cyrathul bit the captain's neck clean through, and Bai Jian's grapple-reposition-shove sent the armor into the river. When the armor hit the water, its failsafe detonated — setting the entire ship ablaze. In the ensuing chaos, the party fought fires with water bombs, secured supplies, rescued trapped crew members, and barely escaped the sinking ship. Both goblins nearly drowned in the escape; Moob sacrificed HP to push Fang above water.

Hearts and Minds

  • Mission: Intercept a Chelaxian cargo ship at a river bottleneck, two days' travel upriver.
  • Rhokrag stressed minimal killing — "We must refrain from killing as much as possible... In the coming weeks and months, we're going to need the people of Isger on our side."
  • Moob immediately flagged a problem: "I actually might need to get a new formula if we're going to have to do non-lethal, because every bomb I can make is very lethal."

Act V · Exploration · The Ambush Plan

The Prisoner Ruse

  • The party camped at a rocky outcropping overlooking a river bend — deep channel on one side, shallow on the other, ships forced close to the cliff.
  • Bai Jian devised the plan: Moob and Cyrathul (disguised as a human via masquerade scarf) present "captured Hellbreaker" Bai Jian to flag down the ship. Fang and Panacea hide on the cliff for ambush support.
  • Cyrathul: "We could pretend to be opportunist loyalists and accuse them of being rebels." Bai Jian: "What if we pretend we actually caught a Hellbreaker? Like a fake hostage situation?"
  • Panacea cast Guidance on Moob before the performance. Bai Jian hero-pointed his Performance check to sell the prisoner act.
Act VI · Roleplay · Boarding the Night's Prize

Countrymen!

  • The ship — red hull, black accents, Chelaxian flag, spiky gunwales — dropped anchor and extended a boarding plank.
  • Moob talked them aboard: "Countrymen! I got a damn Hellbreaker. Can you help? You got manacles below deck?"
  • Bai Jian played the struggling prisoner: "Hey, release me, you scum." Cyrathul (disguised): "I take what I can get."
  • The ruse worked — until Wing knocked a boulder off the cliff, revealing Panacea and Fang. "What's happening here? The rebels!"
  • Panacea: "There goes the element of surprise."
Act VII · Combat · Ship Combat (Extreme)

The Night's Prize

Extreme-difficulty ship boarding against Captain Julenus Aedin, his animated armor bodyguard (all-physical-damage resistance + failsafe explosive), and two hardened swabbies. Fang went down twice. Cyrathul bit the captain to death. Bai Jian shoved the armor overboard.

See Combat Encounters below for round-by-round detail. +128 XP (Extreme).

Act VIII · Mystery · The Burning Ship

Failsafe

  • The animated armor's failsafe detonated when it hit the water, setting the ship ablaze from below.
  • Moob: Water bombs to fight fires (2 successes), looted the captain's coin purse, used Diplomacy to evacuate remaining crew — "We didn't want to do this. Live to see another day."
  • Cyrathul: Esoteric Lore to identify and secure valuable supplies from the cargo.
  • Bai Jian: Athletics to rescue trapped crew members — lifted a collapsed wall, broke down a door. Secured supplies. Shouted to the rescued sailors: "Go spread the words that the Hellbreakers is not all bad."
  • Panacea: Crit athletics to leap from the cliff onto the burning deck — "You watch this cat lady take two steps back and perfectly backflips, does a triple somersault in the air and lands on the deck." Treated Fang's wounds. Secured supplies via Thievery.
  • Fang: Crafting check to push Wing's wreckage off the ship into the water for later salvage.
  • +70 bonus XP (40 crew rescue + 30 supplies).
Act IX · Combat · The Drowning

Grab Onto My Rope

  • The ship sank. Bai Jian and Cyrathul swam to shore easily.
  • Moob, Fang, and Panacea struggled — goblins can't swim, cat hates water.
  • Panacea made it on her second attempt: "I'm hissing and really pissed off about being wet, by the way."
  • Fang kept rolling 4s. Moob voluntarily took 5 damage (critical failure) to push Fang above water: "I purposely will take a critical failure to bump up to help Fang because he's my child, basically."
  • Bai Jian threw rope from shore (hero-pointed the athletics check). Both goblins grabbed on and were pulled to safety.
  • The Night's Prize crew watched from shore. "I hope your friend doesn't die. You all saved us."

Round 1 — Swabbies Neutralized

  • Panacea: Needle Darts into the sails — shredded them.
  • Swabby 1: Two punches at Bai Jian — one connected hard.
  • Swabby 2: Stepped toward Moob, two swings — both sailed over his head.
  • Moob: Tumbled Through Swabby 2's legs, strode to the stern, kicked the anchor winch free with a double-foot kick. Ship began drifting.
  • Cyrathul: Dismissed disguise, unfurled dragon wings. Critically Demoralized the captain (Frightened 2) — "Surrender now or you will all die." Struck Swabby 1 with sword pommel (non-lethal).
  • Bai Jian: Snagging Strike on Swabby 1 (non-lethal, off-guard), second strike knocked him out cold.
  • Fang (on Wing): Rode onto the ship (70 ft movement), non-lethal wheel attack on Swabby 2 (hero-pointed). Goblin gauntlet crit — "pitiful little goblin damage." Swabby begged for mercy.

Round 2 — Captain & Armor Emerge

  • Captain Aedin emerged with the animated armor: "What the hell do you think you're doing on my boat?"
  • Animated Armor: Two glaive strikes on Fang — Fang went down (first down of the campaign).
  • Captain: Dagger stab at Moob (one hit, one miss).
  • Panacea: Moved closer on the rocks, 2-action Heal on Fang at range — brought him back.
  • Swabby 2: Stood up, punched Wing twice — "Consecutive serious punches."
  • Moob: Critical Tenderizer Grenade on animated armor (11 acid + splash) — reduced its physical resistance, made it off-guard to bite attacks. Parry stance. Attempted non-lethal strike on captain (miss).
  • Cyrathul: Strode to armor area. Attempted to bite the armor twice (off-guard from Tenderizer) — missed both, even after spending a hero point. "I tried. That's my turn."
  • Bai Jian: Flanked the armor, struck with piercing — minimal damage. "Your sword pokes into the armor and doesn't really do much damage." Moved away.
  • Fang: Dismounted Wing (prone). Pressed the red button — Exploded Wing (2 actions). Armor critically succeeded the Reflex save (zero damage). Fang took self-damage. "This is going horribly wrong in the best way."

Round 3 — The Turning Point

  • Animated Armor: Three strikes at Cyrathul (one hit, two misses).
  • Captain: First attack brought Moob down. DM redirected second attack to Cyrathul (retconned targeting a downed character).
  • Panacea: Moved on the rocks, 2-action Heal on Moob at range — brought him back.
  • Swabby 2: Attacked Fang/Wing area — brutal hits. Fang went down a second time (now Wounded 2).
  • Moob: Stood up, Quick Alchemy Tenderizer Grenade on captain (hit — made captain off-guard to bites). Parry stance.
  • Cyrathul: Exploit Vulnerability on captain (failed, hero-pointed, succeeded — Personal Antithesis). Bit captain with jaws — hit. Second bite — hit. DM: "How do you want to do this?" "He's nice and tasty. I'll just go straight for the neck." Killing blow on Captain Aedin.
  • Bai Jian: Killed the remaining swabby — "Pierce him through the stomach." Lethal, no more non-lethal. Moved to animated armor, Grapple — success.
  • Fang: Dying recovery check — natural 20. "How about that?" Stable at 0 HP.

Round 4 — Goodbye, Frickin' Armor

  • Animated Armor: Tried to escape grapple three times — all failed.
  • Panacea: Needle Darts on armor (hero-pointed, still resisted). Cast Guidance on Moob.
  • Moob: Quick Alchemy bomb on armor (resisted). Create a Diversion — "Hey shiny rust bucket, look what I'm doing to your master!" (poured acid on the captain's corpse) — failed. Second attempt with words — succeeded.
  • Cyrathul: Attempted Demoralize on armor (no mind — wasted). Exploit Vulnerability (crit fail — still got Personal Antithesis). Longsword strike — miss.
  • Bai Jian: Reposition armor toward the edge → Step → Shove it overboard. "Goodbye, frickin' armor." Splash. Boom. Failsafe detonated — ship caught fire from below.

+128 XP (Extreme) + 70 bonus XP (40 crew rescue + 30 supplies secured).

Private notes, observations, and things to think about. Pax's notes only — not shared with the rest of the party.

What Bai Jian did this session

  • Crit-killed two loyalists in the warehouse fight. One through the face, one lost an arm.
  • Untied Gertraud first while the party dealt with the prisoner. Priorities.
  • Left the jian at Hagrath's forge overnight for the +1 potency rune transfer. Paid 3.5gp.
  • Volunteered as the fake prisoner. Sold the performance with a hero point.
  • Killed the remaining swabby with a lethal thrust — no more non-lethal after Fang went down twice.
  • Grappled the animated armor, then Reposition → Step → Shove it overboard. The move that won the fight.
  • Rescued trapped crew from the burning ship — lifted a collapsed wall, broke down a door.
  • Threw the rope that saved both goblins from drowning.

Things Bai Jian knows that the party does not

  • Nothing secret this session. Everything was shared.
  • The animated armor's failsafe was unexpected. A merchant ship shouldn't have that kind of enchantment. Someone spent real money protecting that cargo — or that captain.

What Bai Jian is thinking about

  • The armor's resistance. The jian couldn't cut it. Even piercing barely scratched it. Cyrathul killed the captain with Exploit Vulnerability + bite combo after Moob's Tenderizer — that's the playbook. But for constructs with no weakness, the party has no good answer without the alchemist's grenades.
  • Non-lethal is expensive. The -2 circumstance penalty nearly cost the party against the swabbies. Rhokrag wants minimal killing, but the mission escalated fast. The captain and armor forced lethal response.
  • Moob and Fang. Moob pushed Fang above water at cost to himself. That bond is real. Keep them close in formation — if one goes down, the other will take risks.
  • Panacea's healing range. She had to spend actions repositioning twice to reach Fang with Heal. Only two Heal spell slots. The party needs to stay tighter or accept that ranged healing is limited.
  • Bai Jian told the sailors to spread the word. Rhokrag wanted hearts and minds. Rescuing crew from a fire we partially caused is complicated — but they saw us save them. That has to count for something.
