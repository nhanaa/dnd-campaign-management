## Session Summary

Picking up on the shore of the North Kansira River after the Night's Prize sinking, the party dealt with the grateful ship crew, salvaged Wing from the riverbed, and fought off river snakes disturbed by the wreck. Moob played the charming goblin diplomat — tipping the boatswain, handing out elixirs, and asking the crew to spread good stories about the Hellbreakers in taverns. After healing up, distributing loot, and a two-day journey back to Saringallow, the party learned that Rhokrag had gone missing while investigating a rumored Hellknight regrouping at the Waterspout Inn.

Armed with healing potions from Marie and two new bomb recipes Moob reverse-engineered on the road, the party traveled two more days to the riverside inn — only to find it occupied by Chelaxian forces, with tents, a docked warship flying Chelaxian colors, and armed soldiers. After waiting until nightfall, an attempted negotiation went south fast, and the party tore through three Chelaxian soldiers in the camp before discovering worse inside: Marines on the ship, a Chelaxian navy captain, and a devil that could fly and spit webs. Bai Jian spotted Rhokrag — bruised, beaten, and tied to a chair in the ship's cabin — before leaping from the crow's nest to skewer the devil mid-air with a hero-pointed critical hit. The session ended on a cliffhanger: the ship captain and a crow's nest Marine still standing, sails on fire from Fang's explosions, and Rhokrag still bound.

## Key Events

### Act 1 — Shore Aftermath (Roleplay / Social)
- The party came ashore after the Night's Prize sinking, alongside the surviving crew
- **Moob** stepped up as spokesman — asked for their new "captain," got the story on the dead crew (Frank, the animated armor's victim, was apparently respected)
- The boatswain handed over a **Boatswain's Favorite** (alchemical item) as thanks; Moob immediately pocketed it to reverse-engineer
- Moob paid a different sailor a gold coin and silvers to fetch a cart from a nearby farmstead, then told him: *"Whenever you're in the pub, speak about Moob, the goblin bomber hellbreaker who is loose with his coin and has a charming smile"*
- **Fang** asked a sailor to help dive for Wing's wreckage and handed him soap and a bedroll as gifts — pure goblin generosity
- **Panacea** administered first aid, treating wounds across the party
- **Bai Jian** interrogated a swabby about the captain and the animated armor — learned Aedin bought it after the Battle of Breachill out of fear, and nobody tracked him

### Act 2 — River Snakes (Combat)
- Ten minutes after the wreck, the water started bubbling — two **gliding river snakes** emerged from the disturbed shipwreck nest
- Snake 1 burst out at Bai Jian with jaws wide; missed twice on aquatic ambush
- **Moob** hero-pointed a mud bomb throw into a critical hit, killing Snake 1 outright: *"Have a mud cake"*
- Snake 2 lunged at **Panacea**, bit and poisoned her
- Panacea successfully treated her own poison, moved away, and attempted **Trade Life for Death** — the snake critically succeeded the save and shrugged it off
- **Fang** activated Overdrive (failed, but got +1 fire), then bit the snake with dragon teeth
- **Cyrathul** placed his Mirror Cloak image, Exploited Vulnerability, and struck the snake with his longsword — killing blow. *"I'll just kind of appear next to him and slash its head off."*
- See **Combat Encounters** for round-by-round detail

### Act 3 — Wing Salvage & Loot (Exploration / Loot)
- A sailor dove and roped Wing's wreckage; the party hauled it ashore
- **Moob** gave Fang rust scrub for Wing: *"I got that specifically for your go-kart"*
- **Fang** repaired Wing over the rest hour (multiple crafting checks, healed 10 HP)
- Loot distributed from the Night's Prize salvage: **Ring of Discretion** → Bai Jian, **Savior Spike talisman** → Bai Jian's jian, **Fine Compass** → sold, various small items → party stash
- **Panacea** treated wounds on the goblins — critical success on Moob after treating Fang first ("It's because it's your karma points for putting your son first")
- **Moob** and **Fang** sang a goblin sailing song that had nothing to do with sailing, because goblins don't get on ships

### Act 4 — Return to Saringallow (Faction / Social)
- Two-day journey back to HQ; Moob reverse-engineered the **Boatswain's Favorite** and **Atrophy Bomb** recipes during travel
- At HQ, **Marie** and **Zara** greeted them; **Ethran Howell** alerted the party that **Rhokrag** was missing
- Rhokrag had gone to investigate rumored Hellknight regrouping at the **Waterspout Inn**, a riverside tavern halfway between Saringallow and Logos
- He had a non-league contact there with intelligence on Hellknight forces re-entering Isger after Breachill
- He was supposed to return yesterday and never showed up
- **Bai Jian** immediately volunteered the party to investigate: *"I don't think it's a good idea to just let him be if he's captured"*
- **Moob** gave Zara a chicken-scratch recipe for **goblin pickle juice bread**: *"Trust me, you'll have goblins rushing up your counter for pickle juice bread. It's a family recipe."*
- Marie provided **2 healing potions** for the road
- Moob asked Panacea if calling her "the cat" was racist — she didn't mind. Moob then launched into a philosophical discussion with Fang about racial terminology, concluding: *"Humans are more touchy about it than most."*

### Act 5 — The Road to Waterspout Inn (Exploration)
- Sold surplus gear (fine compass, work clothes, dagger) for 2gp each; donated ship charts and writing set to the cause
- Moob gave Ethran the fancy writing set as an office gift
- Two-day travel; uneventful — passing merchants and farmers with fall harvest
- On arrival: Chelaxian two-masted ship docked behind the inn, Chelaxian tents and banner by the river, no civilian activity
- Party observed from afar — no one entering or leaving the inn, unusual for mid-afternoon
- Decided to wait until nightfall; Moob injected himself with a **Quicksilver Mutagen** for stealth
- Approached at dusk; Cyrathul scouted ahead and spotted three soldiers around a campfire

### Act 6 — The Camp Fight (Combat)
- Moob attempted diplomacy: *"We just wanted to stay the night. Can't we at least eat our cake?"*
- Soldiers responded: *"Get the fuck out of here. This is Chelaxian area right now."*
- Initiative rolled; combat began
- **Fang** gave Wing three actions — strode in and wheel-slammed a soldier dead in one hit, then activated Overdrive afterward for the minute-long damage buff, and backed off taunting
- **Bai Jian** Sudden Charged the guisarme-wielding soldier, hero-pointed a crit (14 damage), then finished him with a second strike: *"We wanted to talk, and look what you've done."*
- **Cyrathul** emerged from the darkness to strike the last soldier: *"A dragon coming from the darkness"*
- The last soldier crit-tripped Cyrathul with his guisarme, then used **Punish the Fallen** — 8 total damage while Cyrathul was prone
- **Panacea** cast a 2-action **Heal** at range on Cyrathul, keeping him in the fight
- **Fang** drove Wing to the ship gangplank, Tokyo-drifted into the last camp soldier (non-lethal knockout), then tried to remove the gangplank (failed — too well-secured)
- See **Combat Encounters** for round-by-round detail

### Act 7 — The Waterspout Inn Ship Fight (Combat — Ongoing)
- Initiative continued as more enemies emerged from the ship
- **Bai Jian** Demoralized a Marine (Frightened 1), positioning at the ship's edge
- **Moob** threw an **Enchanted Star Grenade** (Runic Weapon from Panacea) — critical hit, 14 fire damage + cross-shaped splash. The Demoralized Marine caught the crit.
- A Marine charged down the gangplank with daggers; another climbed to the crow's nest screaming *"Wake up down there! We're under attack!"*
- **Fang** dismounted, sent Wing to kill the dagger Marine, then attacked the ship's mast (9 damage) and pressed the red button — **Explode innovation** set the sail on fire (wood weakness to fire bypassed hardness)
- **Bai Jian** climbed the crow's nest and struck the Marine with Snagging Strike (5 damage off-guard)
- **Cyrathul** strode to the ship and Exploited Vulnerability on a **devil** — weakness 3 to holy
- A **flying devil** burst from the ship cabin, webbed Bai Jian to the crow's nest and Cyrathul to the deck
- A **Chelaxian navy captain** (woman) kicked open the door: *"What the heck is going on on my ship?"*
- The crow's nest Marine attacked webbed Bai Jian — missed all 3 dagger strikes (stormtrooper)
- **Bai Jian** escaped the webbing, climbed down the crow's nest along the mast, and **leaped from the rigging onto the devil with his sword drawn** — hero-pointed into a **critical Snagging Strike** (21 damage + 2d6 falling damage to devil). **Killed the devil**, slamming into the deck on top of it — DM ruled no fall damage to Bai Jian (devil cushioned the landing). *"I slash at his body, then grab him by the neck and push him down into the ground with me."*
- **Cyrathul** — still webbed to the deck — projected his Mirror Cloak to act at range: Exploited Vulnerability on the captain (failed), then hero-pointed a longsword strike via mirror (hit)
- **Panacea** cast **Needle Darts** at the captain (hit), then sustained **Forbidding Ward** on Moob
- **Moob** threw a fire bomb at Cyrathul's webbed area — 1 fire splash destroyed the web, finally freeing Cyrathul. Parried with wallop stick.
- **Session ended on cliffhanger**: Ship captain and crow's nest Marine still standing. Sails on fire. Rhokrag visible — beaten, bruised, tied to a chair.
