Session Summary

Picking up on the shore of the North Kansira River after the Night's Prize sinking, the party dealt with the grateful ship crew, salvaged Wing from the riverbed, and fought off river snakes disturbed by the wreck. Moob played the charming goblin diplomat — tipping sailors, handing out elixirs, and asking the crew to spread good stories about the Hellbreakers in taverns. After healing up, distributing loot, and a two-day journey back to Saringallow, the party learned that Rhokrag had gone missing while investigating a rumored Hellknight regrouping at the Waterspout Inn.

Armed with healing potions from Marie and two new bomb recipes Moob reverse-engineered on the road, the party traveled two more days to the riverside inn — only to find it occupied by Chelaxian forces, with tents, a docked warship flying Chelaxian colors, and armed soldiers. After waiting until nightfall, an attempted negotiation went south fast, and the party tore through three Chelaxian soldiers in the camp before discovering worse inside: Marines on the ship, a Chelaxian navy captain, and a devil that could fly and spit webs.

Bai Jian spotted Rhokrag — bruised, beaten, and tied to a chair in the ship's cabin — before leaping from the rigging to skewer the devil with a hero-pointed critical hit. The session ended on a cliffhanger: the ship captain and a crow's nest Marine still standing, sails on fire from Fang's explosions, and Rhokrag still bound.

Timeline

Act I · Roleplay · Shore Aftermath

The Grateful Crew

  • The party came ashore after the Night's Prize sinking alongside the surviving crew. Moob stepped up as spokesman — asked for their new "captain," got the story on the dead crew.
  • The boatswain handed over a Boatswain's Favorite (alchemical item) as thanks. Moob pocketed it to reverse-engineer.
  • Moob paid a different sailor gold and silvers to fetch a cart from a nearby farmstead: "Whenever you're in the pub, speak about Moob, the goblin bomber hellbreaker who is loose with his coin and has a charming smile."
  • Fang asked a sailor to help dive for Wing's wreckage, then handed him soap and a bedroll as gifts — pure goblin generosity.
  • Panacea administered first aid, treating wounds across the party.
  • Bai Jian interrogated a swabby about the captain and animated armor — learned Aedin bought it after the Battle of Breachill out of fear, and nobody tracked him.
Act II · Combat · River Snakes

Disturbed Nest

  • Ten minutes after the wreck, the water started bubbling — two gliding river snakes emerged from the disturbed shipwreck nest.
  • Snake 1 burst out at Bai Jian with jaws wide; missed twice on aquatic ambush. Bai Jian and Cyrathul had delayed until it surfaced.
  • Cyrathul placed his Mirror Cloak image and struck with longsword (solid damage). Bai Jian strode to the snake — Snagging Strike (miss), second strike (miss). "Not a great start."
  • Moob hero-pointed a mud bomb throw into a critical hit, killing Snake 1 outright: "Have a mud cake."
  • Snake 2 lunged at Panacea, bit and poisoned her. She treated her own poison successfully, moved away, and attempted Trade Life for Death — snake critically succeeded the save.
  • Fang activated Overdrive (failed, +1 fire only), then bit the snake with dragon teeth. Cyrathul finished it with Mirror + Exploit Vulnerability + longsword: "I'll just appear next to him and slash its head off."
Act III · Exploration · Wing Salvage & Loot

Pulling Wing From the River

  • A sailor dove and roped Wing's wreckage; the party hauled it ashore. Moob gave Fang rust scrub: "I got that specifically for your go-kart."
  • Fang repaired Wing over the rest hour (multiple crafting checks, healed 10 HP). Also discovered tools and supplies stashed inside Wing — crossbow, top-shelf wine.
  • Loot distributed: Ring of Discretion to Bai Jian, Savior Spike talisman to Bai Jian's jian, Fine Compass to party stash (sold later).
  • Panacea treated wounds on the goblins — critical success on Moob after treating Fang first. "It's because it's your karma points for putting your son first."
  • Moob and Fang sang a goblin sailing song that had nothing to do with sailing, because goblins don't get on ships. "Boats, Buddha water."
Act IV · Faction · Return to Saringallow

Rhokrag is Missing

  • Two-day journey back to HQ. Moob reverse-engineered the Boatswain's Favorite and Atrophy Bomb recipes during travel.
  • At HQ, Marie and Zara greeted them. Ethran Howell alerted the party: Rhokrag was missing.
  • Rhokrag had gone to investigate rumored Hellknight regrouping at the Waterspout Inn, a riverside tavern halfway between Saringallow and Logos. He had a non-league contact there.
  • He was supposed to return yesterday and never showed up. Bai Jian immediately volunteered: "I don't think it's a good idea to just let him be if he's captured."
  • Moob gave Zara a chicken-scratch recipe for goblin pickle juice bread: "Trust me, you'll have goblins rushing up your counter. It's a family recipe."
  • Marie provided 2 healing potions. Moob asked Panacea if calling her "the cat" was racist — she didn't mind. Moob and Fang then had a philosophical discussion about racial terminology: "Humans are more touchy about it than most."
Act V · Exploration · The Road to Waterspout Inn

Chelaxian Occupied

  • Sold surplus gear for 2gp each; donated ship charts and writing set to the cause. Moob gave Ethran the fancy writing set as an office gift.
  • Two-day travel; uneventful — passing merchants and farmers with fall harvest.
  • On arrival: Chelaxian two-masted ship docked behind the inn, tents and banner by the river, no civilian activity. No one entering or leaving — unusual for mid-afternoon.
  • Decided to wait until nightfall. Moob injected himself with a Quicksilver Mutagen for stealth. "You see my green skin kind of turn pearlescent white."
  • Approached at dusk. Cyrathul scouted and spotted three soldiers around a campfire. One spotted him back: "Oza!" Cyrathul: "It's probably best that the dragon is the one who comes out."
Act VI · Combat · The Camp Fight

We Wanted to Talk

  • Moob attempted diplomacy: "We just wanted to stay the night. Can't we at least eat our cake?" Soldiers: "Get the fuck out of here. This is Chelaxian area right now."
  • Fang drove Wing into a soldier, wheel-slammed him dead in one hit, then activated Overdrive and backed off taunting. "I wasn't expecting him to flop over dead just like that."
  • Bai Jian Sudden Charged the guisarme soldier — hero-pointed a critical hit (14 damage), then finished him with a second strike: "We wanted to talk, and look what you've done."
  • Cyrathul emerged from the darkness to strike the last soldier. "A dragon coming from the darkness." The soldier crit-tripped Cyrathul with his guisarme, then Punish the Fallen (8 total damage).
  • Panacea cast a 2-action Heal at range on Cyrathul, keeping him in the fight.
  • Fang drove Wing to the gangplank, Tokyo-drifted into the last soldier for a non-lethal knockout. Tried to remove the gangplank — failed (too well-secured).
Act VII · Combat · The Ship Fight (Ongoing)

Webbing, Fire, and a Devil

  • Initiative continued. Bai Jian Demoralized a Marine (Frightened 1). Panacea hero-pointed Battle Medicine on Moob and cast Runic Weapon on his star grenade.
  • Moob threw the enchanted Star Grenade — critical hit, 14 fire damage + cross-shaped splash. The Demoralized Marine caught the crit. Ship deck caught fire.
  • A dagger Marine charged down the gangplank. Another climbed to the crow's nest screaming: "Wake up down there! We're under attack!"
  • Fang dismounted and sent Wing to kill the frightened Marine (Demoralize enabled the hit at +0: "the frightened did it").
  • Bai Jian climbed the crow's nest, Snagging Strike on the Marine (5 damage, off-guard). Spotted Rhokrag tied to a chair: "I saw him! He's captured over there!"
  • Cyrathul Exploited Vulnerability on the devil — weakness 3 to holy. The devil flew from the cabin, webbed Bai Jian to the crow's nest and Cyrathul to the deck.
  • A Chelaxian navy captain kicked open the door: "What the heck is going on on my ship?" Fired at Bai Jian — missed. The crow's nest Marine attacked webbed Bai Jian — missed all 3 strikes.
  • Fang hero-pointed Wing's attack on the mast (9 damage), then Exploded Wing — 5 fire damage, wood weakness bypassed hardness, sails caught fire. "Timber!"
  • Bai Jian escaped the webbing, climbed down the mast, and leaped from the rigging onto the devil — hero-pointed into a critical Snagging Strike (21 damage + 2d6 falling damage). Killed the devil. "I slash at his body, then grab him by the neck and push him down into the ground with me."
  • Cyrathul — still webbed to the deck — projected his Mirror Cloak to act at range: hero-pointed a longsword strike on the captain via mirror (hit). Panacea hit the captain with Needle Darts. Moob burned the web off Cyrathul with 1 fire splash, finally freeing him.
