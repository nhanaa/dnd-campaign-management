## Session Summary

Session 5 picked up exactly where the cliffhanger left it: the Vault Guardian rising in the Stillwind Lab, Silas standing on a glowing tile he never noticed. The DM retconned a small mercy for Avon's active Detect Magic — the tile lit up just before Silas stepped on it — but Silas vanished anyway in a flash of arcane circle. The party was four, and then five: a blonde-haired Barbarian named **Loren** burst through the doors mid-combat, professional, taciturn, and in a hurry to get paid. Mist's Heat Metal cooked the construct from the inside while Avon's Spiritual Weapon battered it from outside, and Loren ended it with a savage greataxe to the neck. Two arcane turrets came with the construct, and the party traded reactive 10-foot pulses for steady acid punches and crossbow bolts until both pillars went silent.

After the dust settled, Mist used Mage Hand to investigate three pedestal items: a small bag of beans (later identified as a magical Bag of Beans, seven beans inside), a heavy chest holding 28 gold and two healing potions, and a mysterious orb with mist swirling inside its glass. When Avon channeled an Identify on the orb and Mist touched it at the same time, **both characters disappeared** — pulled into a frozen tableau of a battle and a ritual, every figure mid-action and silent, every spell suppressed. Avon memorized the scene; together they discovered they could *transpose* the figures by shoving them, and shoving the kneeling ritual victim hard enough flung them back to reality. The orb went dark.

Loren escorted the party to the **Crowned Pheasant**, an upscale tavern in the Noble District where **Evrin** waited in a black velvet tuxedo. He took the prism into a metal-lined box, paid them in their existing agreement, and pitched a long-term contract framed by a startlingly principled speech: *"We don't shy away from dirty jobs, but I'd like to lift the bottom of this city up, not just the rich."* Mist's **Persuasion 25** earned a *"I like your style, kid"* and pried a **signing bonus** out of Evrin — but only for the long-term contract, not the original prism deal (Evrin: *"I would not pay you a copper more than what we agreed"*). Akasha shut down renegotiating the *prism* deal with *"a deal is a deal,"* but Mist's separate gambit landed: he agreed to the long-term contract and explicitly cashed in the promised bonus. The party left with a code phrase (*"purchase a seagull"*) and a free suite for the night. Mist privately warned Avon to keep the orb-vision a secret from Loren and Evrin — the party would investigate the frozen tableau themselves first.

## Key Events

### Act I — Cliffhanger Resolution & Initiative
- DM retconned a Detect Magic mercy: Avon's active ritual revealed a glowing tile under Silas a split-second before he stepped on it, but the party couldn't stop him in time
- A magic circle flared, Silas vanished, the circle went inert — and then the construct turned: *"Prepare to be annihilated."*
- Mist gave a clean session-4 recap (gained DM Inspiration); Talya pushed him to do it: *"Let's start with the recap."*
- Construct described: large metal frame, encased arcane crystal in chest, helmet-capped, arcane striations arcing across its body, full of ranged options — *"clearly a metal one this time. Useless... let's go."*

### Act II — The Vault Guardian (Combat)
- See **Combat Encounters** section for the full round-by-round breakdown
- Loren burst through the doors at minute ~30 of the session (Round 1 still in progress), an obvious cameo arrival ("I forgot to hit join voice" — out-of-character apology by the player)

### Act III — The Three Pedestals
- **Mage Hand inspection** by Mist (cautious — one HP loss away from down):
  - **Whisper Prism** (center pedestal) — emits a cacophony of indecipherable whispers within ~10 feet. Mist dropped it and ran when the whispers started.
  - **Bag of Beans** (left pillar, small leather bag) — Mage Hand could lift it. History 18: Mist recognized it as a magical bag where dumping the beans causes them to explode, but planting them one by one and watering them produces effects. Seven beans inside.
  - **Heavy chest** (left of pillar, on floor) — too heavy for Mage Hand. Talya investigated; chest unlocked. **28 gold + 2 healing potions.**
  - **Mysterious orb** (right pillar) — swirling mist inside a glass sphere, in a fitted holder
- Avon completed his Detect Magic ritual: Whisper Prism = Divination, Bag of Beans = Evocation/Conjuration mix, Orb = Conjuration

### Act IV — The Frozen Tableau (Mist & Avon Disappear)
- Avon used **Channel Divinity (Preserve Knowledge)** to cast **Identify** on the orb without the 100-gp pearl component
- Mist touched the orb at the same time out of curiosity
- **Both vanished.** Loren and Akasha both dove for the Whisper Prism on the floor — Akasha won the simultaneous DEX check by a hair and grabbed it. Whispers seemed to focus on her but stayed indecipherable.
- Mist and Avon found themselves in **two frozen scenes** sharing one space:
  - **Outdoor battle** — a fighter leaping mid-air with a sword aimed at a defenseless target; another figure with archer's arrows headed for their back
  - **Sheared-in-half building** — three robed figures conducting a ritual; one kneeling in prayer/chant; another raising a morning star to bring down on the kneeler's skull; a stone pedestal with two objects on top and a third (a red crystal in ornate gold housing) fallen on the floor next to it
- Everything frozen, dead silent, no breeze
- **Spells didn't work** — Mist's Mage Hand fizzled like a lighter clicking; Avon's Light spell did nothing
- Avon pinched his cheek (took 1 damage) — *not* a dream
- Avon (Insight 19) committed every face, weapon, and architectural detail to memory for later research
- Avon poked the kneeling ritual victim with the blunt end of his spear — the body **transposed** (slid as if a chess piece). A harder shove vacuumed both Mist and Avon back to reality.
- They reappeared in the lab next to the orb — now an empty crystal, no swirl. Time passage outside was minimal.

### Act V — Loot Split & Departure
- Akasha rolled Insight 22 on Loren — fully truthful, no deception
- Loren refused a treasure cut (already compensated by Evrin)
- 7 gold each, healing potions distributed (Avon: *"as a cleric, I probably shouldn't need potions"* — gave one to Talya)
- Mist pocketed the Bag of Beans, decided to plant them later
- Party walked out of the Stillwind Lab without incident, sun getting low

### Act VI — The Crowned Pheasant (Noble District)
- People in the streets gave the party friendly waves and thanks — word of their **Wardstone Festival defense** (Session 1) was spreading citywide. Their hideout actions remained unknown publicly.
- Mist had never been to the Noble District before — buskers aren't welcome here
- Tavern: stone floors, stained glass, very upscale — *the Crowned Pheasant*
- **Evrin** waited at a private table in a black velvet tuxedo — speaking with practiced upscale diction that surprised the party (none of them had spoken to him directly before; that had been Silas's role)
- Loren delivered a professional debrief: prism retrieved, vision witnessed, Silas missing
- Evrin's reaction to Silas's absence: deeply concerned, hiding it (Mist + Loren both rolled Insight 17/21)
- Akasha pulled the prism out — surrounding tables started hearing the whispers; one woman started crying. Evrin: *"put it away, put it away."*
- Akasha asked Evrin directly *"why is it so important?"* — answer: *"What the ultimate destiny for this item is, I don't know. I just know that I needed to acquire it to get paid."*
- **Evrin's pitch — long-term contract:** *"I follow the same code as your compatriot [Silas]. When he left our organization, I almost left with him. I want to be like him. We don't shy away from dirty jobs... but I'd like to turn my organization into something seen as legitimate. Lift the bottom up, not just the rich."*
- **Mist Persuasion 25** trying for a signing bonus → *"I like your style, kid"*
- **Akasha** shut down renegotiating the *original prism deal*: *"The deal was for information that was already provided. So the time for renegotiations, I believe, has passed. Because a deal is a deal."*
- **Evrin parsed the two threads cleanly**: *"I absolutely would not pay you a copper more than what we agreed. However, what your friend is suggesting is that we enter a more long-term agreement, and in that case a signing bonus, I'm sure, would be more than acceptable."*
- Mist (Insight 6 — moved by Evrin's reformer speech given his own backstory) agreed to long-term work, then explicitly **cashed in the signing bonus**: *"Alright, but remember the signing bonus I think you promised."* Evrin smiled, asked the rest if they were in.
- **Talya** agreed; **Akasha** extended *alliance* but declined formal membership; Avon stayed silent
- **Code phrase to summon Evrin:** leave a message with the barkeep asking to *"purchase a seagull"*
- Akasha placed the prism in its bag, then into Evrin's metal-lined box — Evrin took it, bowed, left

### Act VII — Aftermath
- Loren lingered, ate a small pie, answered Mist's questions about Evrin honestly: *"I would not call dirty jobs noble. He goes about his dealings with high decorum and is honest, if not as a person himself."* Pays fairly, protects his own.
- Loren's primary work is security — once protected the traveling musician **Sylvia Plath** (Mist knows of her, with a flicker of jealousy)
- Mist privately to Avon: keep the orb-vision secret from Loren and Evrin — *"I'd rather we find out ourselves first."*
