Session Summary

Session 5 picked up exactly where the cliffhanger left it: the Vault Guardian rising in the Stillwind Lab, Silas standing on a glowing tile he never noticed. The DM retconned a small mercy for Avon's active Detect Magic — the tile lit up just before Silas stepped on it — but Silas vanished anyway in a flash of arcane circle. The party was four, and then five: a blonde-haired Barbarian named Loren burst through the doors mid-combat, professional, taciturn, and in a hurry to get paid. Mist's Heat Metal cooked the construct from the inside while Avon's Spiritual Weapon battered it from outside, and Loren ended it with a savage greataxe to the neck. Two arcane turrets came with the construct, and the party traded reactive 10-foot pulses for steady acid punches and crossbow bolts until both pillars went silent.

After the dust settled, Mist used Mage Hand to investigate three pedestal items: a small Bag of Beans (later identified as magical, seven beans inside), a heavy chest holding 28 gold and two healing potions, and a mysterious orb with mist swirling inside its glass. When Avon channeled an Identify on the orb and Mist touched it at the same time, both characters disappeared — pulled into a frozen tableau of a battle and a ritual, every figure mid-action and silent, every spell suppressed. Avon memorized the scene; together they discovered they could transpose the figures by shoving them, and shoving the kneeling ritual victim hard enough flung them back to reality. The orb went dark.

Loren escorted the party to the Crowned Pheasant, an upscale tavern in the Noble District where Evrin waited in a black velvet tuxedo. He took the prism into a metal-lined box, paid them in their existing agreement, and pitched a long-term contract framed by a startlingly principled speech: “We don't shy away from dirty jobs, but I'd like to lift the bottom of this city up, not just the rich.” Mist's Persuasion 25 earned an “I like your style, kid” and pried a signing bonus out of Evrin — but only for the long-term contract, not the original prism deal (Evrin: “I would not pay you a copper more than what we agreed”). Akasha shut down renegotiating the prism deal with “a deal is a deal,” but Mist's separate gambit landed: he agreed to the long-term contract and explicitly cashed in the promised bonus. The party left with a code phrase (“purchase a seagull”) and a free suite for the night. Mist privately warned Avon to keep the orb-vision a secret from Loren and Evrin — the party would investigate the frozen tableau themselves first.

Act IV: The Frozen Tableau (Mist & Avon Disappear)
Puzzle / Lore
  • Avon used Channel Divinity (Preserve Knowledge) to cast Identify on the orb without the 100-gp pearl. Mist touched the orb at the same time out of curiosity.
  • Both vanished. Loren and Akasha simultaneously dove for the Whisper Prism on the floor — Akasha won the simultaneous DEX check and grabbed it. The whispers seemed to focus, trying to be heard, but stayed indecipherable.
  • Mist and Avon found themselves in two frozen scenes sharing one space: an outdoor battle (a fighter mid-leap with sword, an archer's arrows headed for a victim's back) and a sheared-in-half building with three robed figures conducting a ritual
  • Inside the ritual: a kneeling figure praying; a second figure with a morning star raised over their head; a stone pedestal with two objects, with a third (a red crystal in ornate gold housing) fallen on the floor next to it
  • Everything frozen, dead silent — no breeze. Mist's Mage Hand fizzled like a lighter clicking. Avon's Light spell did nothing. No magic in this place.
  • Avon pinched his cheek (1 damage) — not a dream. Insight 19: committed every face, weapon, and architectural detail to memory for later research. Everything was foreign — no cultural or architectural match either character recognized.
  • Avon poked the kneeling ritual victim with the blunt end of his spear — the body transposed sideways. A harder shove vacuumed both Mist and Avon back to reality.
  • They reappeared next to the orb — now an empty crystal sphere, the swirling mist gone. Time passage outside was minimal.

The Vault Guardian & Two Arcane Turrets — Stillwind Lab Vault
5 Rounds

Enemies: 1 Vault Guardian (large metal arcane construct, encased crystal core, helmet-capped) + 2 Arcane Turrets (pillar-mounted, reactive 10-ft arcane pulse on hit — DC 13 DEX, 10 dmg)

Initiative (Foundry tracker): Avon 21 → Mist 19 → Vault Guardian 17 → Akasha 14 → Top Turret 12 → Talya 10 → Lower Turret 10 → Loren Lyster 10. Akasha goes BEFORE Talya every round.
