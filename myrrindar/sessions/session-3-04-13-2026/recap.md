## Session Summary

Picking up from last session's cliffhanger, the party found themselves deep inside a secret hideout beneath Skybreeze, having followed Elias Whitmore and his green-robed escort through a hidden door. With Garrett already tied up and Blitt approaching down the corridor, the session opened with tension — and quickly escalated into the party's first real combat encounter.

After dispatching the hideout's defenders in a close-quarters brawl through the lounge, the party interrogated the organization's blue-robed lieutenant. He revealed that his group — the **Vale Tempest** — exists to protect the Wardstones by suppressing what they call "lies" about something beneath Skybreeze. Their tool: enchanted amulets that control the minds of key citizens, keeping them aligned with the "truth" that the Wardstones simply protect against the Arcane Storm. When Silas stealthily removed Elias's amulet, the researcher began questioning everything — only to collapse in agony, suggesting the enchantment's hold runs deeper than simple persuasion.

Akasha's daring reconnaissance as a mouse revealed an amulet assembly room staffed by a fancy-robed woman (likely a leader), and a journal exposed five enchanted targets throughout Skybreeze. Facing a moral crossroads, the party voted to eliminate the witnesses, free Elias (amulet intact), and withdraw to regroup — leveling up to 3 as the day ended.

## Key Events

### Act 1: The Hideout Confrontation (00:00–24:00)

- Session opened with the DM's recap of Session 2: discovering the hideout, lifting Elias's amulet, following the green robes through the secret door
- **Blitt** (Dragonborn, Vale Tempest) discovered Avon in the corridor — Avon attempted to bluff his way through as an investigator
- Silas backed the story, claiming Elias sent them; Blitt was unconvinced
- **Mist** flew in from outside, bluffing that guards were already notified — rolled high on Deception, convincing the green robes to cooperate temporarily
- All five party members were corralled into the lounge; Blitt tried to close the door, but Silas jammed his foot in
- A **Blue Robe leader** emerged from a side room, called "Breach!" — initiative rolled

### Act 2: Lounge Fight (24:00–74:00)

- **Close-quarters combat** in a cramped lounge with two hallway chokepoints
- Talya critted on Blitt with an unarmed strike, knocking him unconscious against the wall
- **Mist cast Dissonant Whispers** on the Blue Robe leader — 12 damage, forced him to flee down the hallway (triggering an opportunity attack from Avon)
- Mist gave **Bardic Inspiration** to Talya
- Akasha clubbed Avarian with a crit, used stunt points for Knock Prone, then followed up with a sickle hit
- Avon moved into the front line by shoving Mist out of the way, tanking two opportunity attacks (both missed)
- Silas knocked out Avarian with a shortsword pommel strike (non-lethal)
- The Blue Robe returned but whiffed his attack on Silas — his staff snapped on the doorframe
- One Green Robe surrendered after Mist's intimidation
- **Akasha shot the Blue Robe's knee** with her shortbow, then intimidated him into surrendering (using Heroic Inspiration to reroll the Intimidation check)
- **Final tally**: 2 unconscious (Blitt, Avarian), 2 surrendered (Blue Robe, hallway Green Robe), 1 restrained (Garrett)

### Act 3: Interrogation of the Vale Tempest (74:00–122:00)

- Silas freed Elias from the chair while others questioned the Blue Robe leader
- The Blue Robe claimed the Vale Tempest **opposes** the Wardstone sabotage and protects the city
- Said Elias is "sick" — the amulet keeps him "sane" by suppressing "madness" (believing lies about the Wardstones)
- Avon pressed hard: "Who told you it's a lie?" — the leader claimed not to know his own organization's top leadership
- Mist recognized the Blue Robe's symbol from Skybreeze street stories (History check with Guidance: 14) — a secretive group of Wardstone protectors
- Mist bluffed that he already knew the organization's name (Deception roll: 27 with Guidance) — the leader cracked and revealed: **"We're the Vale Tempest"**
- **Amulet experiment**: Silas stealthily lifted the amulet off Elias while the party distracted the blue robe
  - Without the amulet, Elias began questioning the Wardstone narrative: *"I don't think the Wardstones are protecting us... the research doesn't lie"*
  - He mentioned hearing tales of something beneath the city, but collapsed in agony before he could elaborate
  - The Blue Robe **backhanded Elias** when he started talking, then demanded the amulet be returned
  - Medicine checks failed across the board — the party couldn't determine what was happening to Elias
  - Amulet replaced — Elias immediately calmed and reverted: *"Of course the Wardstones protect us"*
- One Green Robe (the one who was crawling toward the assembly room to sound the alarm) was killed by Akasha's arrow

### Act 4: Mouse Reconnaissance & Discovery (122:00–158:00)

- Party explored the hideout: brewery (unused), dining room (Mist looted 6 gold of silverware), kitchen (Akasha took provisions)
- Discovered a door to a room with **three more Vale Tempest members**: a Green Robe (note-taker), a Blue Robe (assembling amulets), and a **Fancy Robe woman** (enchanting them) — likely a higher-ranking leader
- **Akasha used Wild Shape** to become a mouse and infiltrate the room
  - Rolled a 21 on Stealth — scurried under the door unseen
  - Observed the amulet assembly process: Blue Robe carves physical runes, Fancy Robe enchants them
  - Overheard the Fancy Robe say they need to make Elias's amulet **stronger** and that a recent breakthrough enables this
  - Read the note-taker's journal from the shelf (Perception 15) — confirmed the breakthrough
  - Escaped unseen after three successful stealth checks
- **Silas found a journal** that listed enchanted targets:
  - **Elias Whitmore** — head wind researcher (dedicated page)
  - **Rufus Dawnstrider** — dock foreman ("moderate success, reapplication recommended")
  - **Valo, Regina, Bildren** — city guards ("limited success, low awareness, stronger amulets recommended")
- Mist recognized **Rufus** as someone he knows from the streets — a cool guy with influence over the docks

### Act 5: The Moral Crossroads (158:00–197:00)

- Party debated three options: leave quietly, confront the Fancy Robe, or eliminate witnesses
- Silas argued for killing the incapacitated members and leaving — "These witnesses need to go. No one knows it's us."
- Avon wanted to confront the Fancy Robe but acknowledged the risk
- Akasha: "I will not kill them, but I will not stop anybody who would want to"
- Mist sided with Silas — didn't want a target on the party's back
- **Final vote**: Kill the incapacitated, free Elias, leave without confronting
- Silas dispatched the prisoners (including Blitt, Avarian, Garrett, and the Blue Robe lieutenant)
- Elias was escorted out with his amulet still on — he stumbled off into the city
- The party vacated the hideout, shut the secret door, and retreated to an inn
- **Level up to 3** upon completing a long rest
