Session Summary

The session opened with a level-up to 4 after the long rest in the hag’s cottage from Session 7. Mist looted the hag’s body, finding a Jar of Eyes (Divination), a Dream Catcher (Abjuration), and a silver spoon engraved with dates — one matching the funeral of an Iliora elder 60 years ago. Avon theorized the eyes cause nightmares and the dreamcatcher prevents them. Jaethal performed a Find Familiar ritual using Avon’s Sevra incense, which burned silver instead of orange — summoning an iridescent fairy dragon with an unexplained silvery hue.

With the hag dealt, Akasha led the party to her hometown of Iliora, built across the massive world tree Idranthusin. The tree felt diminished. Calith, Akasha’s older brother, greeted her with grim news: patrols going missing, strange laughter and roars at night, no animal sounds, fear growing daily. “Idranthusin is weeping.” Mist and Jaethal busked a duet through the city gates — Performance 19 with Druidcraft flower scent — drawing a swaying crowd and lifting spirits the city desperately needed.

At the Elder’s Hall, Thalindor confirmed Deepbloom had passed through with a guide — Liriel, Akasha’s childhood friend. Pindle, an aspiring elf adventurer-researcher, volunteered to guide them east. In the blighted forest, they found massive claw marks, uprooted trees, and hag effigies. Avon’s Augury returned Woe. Five “toads” revealed themselves as quasits — demon lizards that poison on hit. The party dispatched four across three rounds. Jaethal put the last one to Sleep with a nat 1 Wis save, and Akasha tied it up with Jaethal’s manacles — “Now that he’s sleeping, I don’t feel right attacking it.”

Round 1 — Setup and First Blood

  • Jaethal Taunting Misty Step → Q4 & Q5 fail Wis DC 15 (disadvantage on non-Jaethal attacks). Faerie Fire → Q2 & Q3 fail Dex (advantage on attacks vs them). Avon also fails (friendly fire)
  • Quasit 2 claws Avon (advantage from Faerie Fire, dirty 20), 4 slashing + Avon poisoned
  • Akasha Guiding Bolt on Q2 — 15 radiant. Blows its leg off
  • Mist Cloud of Daggers on Q4 (6 damage). Bardic Inspiration to Talya. Flies 40ft back, 15ft up
  • Avon (poisoned) Mind Spike on Q2 — Wis save 12, fails. 16 psychic. Head explodes. Q2 dead
  • Quasit 4 (taunted) swings at Talya — disadvantage reroll drops 23 to 15, misses
  • Talya two unarmed strikes on Q4 — both miss (low rolls)
  • Quasit 1 attacks Pindle — hits. Pindle wounded

Round 2 — Talya’s Rampage

  • Quasit 3 swings at Akasha — 14, misses. Akasha’s poison wears off
  • Jaethal Eldritch Blast on Q3 (advantage from Faerie Fire) — hits, but rolls 1 on d10 damage twice. Cursed dice
  • Akasha Ice Knife on Q3 — misses even with Faerie Fire advantage
  • Quasit 5 attacks Avon (advantage from Faerie Fire on Avon) — two 10s (15 total), misses
  • Mist True Strike crossbow on Q3 (Faerie Fire advantage) — hits, 7 damage. First live use of the new cantrip
  • Avon (poisoned) Sacred Flame on Q3 — Dex save 8, fails. 8 radiant (max d8). Q3 bloodied
  • Quasit 4 claws Talya — 23 hits, 5 slashing + poisoned. Cloud of Daggers triggers at end of turn — 14 slashing, Q4 shredded
  • Talya redirects to bloodied Q3 — two unarmed strikes: 9 + 10 damage. “Punching its head into the dirt, making a bloody soup.” Q3 dead
  • Quasit 1 attacks Pindle — rolls a 2, misses. Pindle accidentally parries while flailing in panic

Round 3 — Cleanup and Capture

  • Jaethal Eldritch Blast on Q1 (25 to hit!) — damage roll pitiful again. Refreshing Misty Step to Pindle — 9 temp HP
  • Akasha Guiding Bolt on Q5 — hits, instantly bloodies it
  • Quasit 5 scampers to Talya — 23 hits, 6 slashing + poisoned
  • Mist moves Cloud of Daggers to Q5 — 8 damage. “So, I’m just gonna die, you evil, ugly monster.”
  • Avon Sacred Flame on Q5 — Dex save 7, fails. 4 radiant. Q5 vaporized in golden light
  • Quasit 1 attacks Pindle — 9 damage, burns through all temp HP from Jaethal’s Refreshing Step
  • Talya uses Bardic Inspiration on acid punch at Q1 — hits, acid eats through its belly. Second punch also connects
  • Jaethal casts Sleep — Wis DC 15, Q1 rolls nat 1. Falls asleep snoring. “Nice bedtime story.”
