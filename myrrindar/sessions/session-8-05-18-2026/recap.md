## Session Summary

The session opened with a level-up to 4 (long rest from the S7 hag fight) and a careful investigation of the hag's cottage. Mist looted the hag's body, finding a **Jar of Eyes** (Divination), a **Dream Catcher** (Abjuration), and a **silver spoon engraved with dates** — one matching the funeral of an Iliora elder 60 years ago. Avon theorized the eyes cause nightmares and the dreamcatcher prevents them. Meanwhile, Jaethal drew Talya a sketch of her S7 hag-stomp and then performed a **Find Familiar** ritual using Avon's incense from Sevra. The incense burned **silver** — unusual — and summoned a **fairy dragon** familiar with an unexplained silvery hue: iridescent blue with violet butterfly wings, cat-sized, and capable of speech.

With the hag dealt, Akasha's pull to the east had faded, but the forest's unnatural silence remained. The party traveled northeast to **Iliora**, where Akasha's hometown spread across the massive **world tree Idranthusin** — bridges between branches, houses built into the bark, lightning bugs in the canopy. But the tree felt *diminished*. **Calith**, Akasha's older brother and patrol guard, greeted her with a hug and grim news: patrols going missing, strange laughter and roars in the forest at night, no animal sounds, fear growing daily. *"Idranthusin is weeping."* Mist and Jaethal busked a duet through the city gates — Performance 19 with Druidcraft flower scent — drawing a swaying crowd and lifting spirits the city desperately needed. Mist earned 4 gold.

At the Elder's Hall, **Thalindor** confirmed **Thalren Deepbloom** had passed through hours earlier with a guide — **Liriel**, Akasha's childhood friend. A new NPC, **Pindle** — a short, spectacled elf researcher and aspiring adventurer — volunteered to guide the party east. In the forest, they found uprooted trees, massive claw marks, and more hag effigies. Avon's **Augury** returned **Woe** — a straight fight would end badly. Five "toads" approached the party after Mist's botched stealth check (he bumped into Avon's shield). Akasha tried Speak with Animals — no response. Then they revealed themselves: **quasits** — small demon lizards that poison on hit. The party dispatched four across three rounds. Jaethal put the last one to **Sleep** with a nat 1 Wisdom save, and Akasha tied it up with Jaethal's manacles — *"Now that he's sleeping, I don't feel right attacking it."* The session ended with the party standing over a bound, snoring demon in the blighted forest east of Iliora.

#### Round 1

- **Quasit 3**: Rakes Akasha (15 vs AC 15, ties = hits), 7 slashing + poisoned. Wisdom save DC 10 for all party — everyone passes
- **Jaethal**: Bonus Action **Taunting Misty Step** (30ft away) — Quasits 4 and 5 fail Wis DC 15, disadvantage on non-Jaethal attacks. Action: **Faerie Fire** — catches the whole group in the 20ft cube. Quasits 2 and 3 fail Dex save (advantage on attacks against them). Avon also fails Dex save (friendly fire — advantage on attacks against Avon). Flies 15ft up
- **Quasit 2**: Attacks Avon (advantage from Faerie Fire, dirty 20), 4 slashing + Avon poisoned
- **Akasha**: **Guiding Bolt** on Quasit 2 — hits, **15 radiant damage**. Blows its leg off. Wreathed in golden/cyan light (next attack has advantage)
- **Quasit 5**: Attacks Talya — rolls 10, misses
- **Mist**: **Cloud of Daggers** on Quasit 4 — 6 slashing damage (no save). Bonus Action: Bardic Inspiration (d6) to Talya. Flies 40ft back, 15ft up
- **Avon** (poisoned): **Mind Spike** on Quasit 2 — Wis save 12, fails DC 13. **16 psychic damage** — *head explodes*. Quasit 2 dead
- **Quasit 4** (taunted): Attacks Talya with disadvantage — initial 23 retconned to 15 (taunted reroll), misses
- **Talya**: Two unarmed strikes on Quasit 4 — both miss (low rolls). Holds Bardic Inspiration
- **Quasit 1**: Attacks Pindle — hits, deals damage. Pindle wounded and bleeding

#### Round 2

- **Quasit 3**: Attacks Akasha — 14 to hit, misses (AC 15). Akasha's poison wears off
- **Jaethal**: Eldritch Blast at Quasit 3 (advantage from Faerie Fire) — hits, but rolls **1 on d10 damage** twice in a row. Cursed dice
- **Akasha**: **Ice Knife** at Quasit 3 (advantage from Faerie Fire) — misses even with advantage
- **Quasit 5**: Attacks Avon (advantage from Faerie Fire on Avon) — rolls two 10s (15), misses Avon's AC
- **Mist**: **True Strike crossbow** on Quasit 3 (advantage from Faerie Fire) — hits, **7 piercing/radiant damage**
- **Avon** (poisoned): **Sacred Flame** on Quasit 3 — Dex save 8, fails DC 13. **8 radiant damage** (max d8!). Quasit 3 bloodied. Ends turn adjacent to Quasit 5
- **Quasit 4**: Attacks Talya — 23 to hit, **5 slashing + poisoned**. Cloud of Daggers triggers at end of turn — **14 slashing, kills Quasit 4**: *"the daggers seem to start churning faster and it shreds it into small little demon lizard pieces"*
- **Talya**: Redirected to Quasit 3 (the bloodied, fairy-fired one). Two unarmed strikes — first hits for **9 damage**, second hits for **10 damage**. **Kills Quasit 3**. *"I'm just going to pummel it until it's more of a pulp than whatever it was."* DM: *"You punch its head into the dirt and you're making a bloody soup in the divot."*
- **Quasit 1**: Attacks Pindle — rolled a 2, misses. Pindle accidentally parries while swatting his arms in panic

#### Round 3

- **Jaethal**: Eldritch Blast at Quasit 1 — hits (25!), but damage roll again pitiful. **Refreshing Misty Step** to Pindle — 9 temp HP
- **Akasha**: **Guiding Bolt** on Quasit 5 — hits, **instantly bloodies it**
- **Quasit 5**: Scampers to Talya — 23 to hit, 6 slashing + poisoned
- **Mist**: Moves **Cloud of Daggers** to Quasit 5 — **8 slashing damage**. *"So, I'm just gonna die, you evil, ugly monster."*
- **Avon**: **Sacred Flame** on Quasit 5 — Dex save 7, fails. 4 radiant. **Quasit 5 vaporized** — *"this golden ray of light just encompasses it and it sizzles and vaporizes"*
- **Quasit 1**: Attacks Pindle — 9 damage, burns through all temp HP from Jaethal's Refreshing Step
- **Talya**: Uses Bardic Inspiration on her punch at Quasit 1 — acid fist hits, burns away the flesh of its belly. Second punch also connects
- **Jaethal**: *"I'm too close to hit him with an Eldritch Blast, but we could do funnier things."* Casts **Sleep** — Wis DC 15, quasit rolls **nat 1**. Falls asleep snoring. *"We're going to try to tell him a nice bedtime story and cast Sleep."*

Jaethal also ties rope around its feet (DC 13 Str to escape). Session ends with the party standing over a bound, sleeping demon.
