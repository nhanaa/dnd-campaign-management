## Session Summary

After a long rest in Evrin's pre-paid suite, the party realized something embarrassing: in five sessions of adventuring together, **they had never actually told each other their backstories**. Breakfast at the Crowned Pheasant became a slow, careful introduction round — Avon's Bahamut vision out of the village of Byron, Akasha's Whispering Canopy and the blight on the mother tree, Mist's fairy settlement and the kidnapping he survived, Talya's shipwreck and the monastery that took her in. Mist, guarded as ever, kept it short: *"I grew up in a fairy settlement, and then I got kidnapped, and then..."* Avon and Akasha gently offered to help track his captors. The session-wide tonal shift started here.

The day's actual goal was research. **Akasha's quest** — find "Deep Bloom" in Skybreeze — became the through-line. A street encounter with **Carrie-Anne** (Avon's bashful Bahamut-temple admirer) revealed the name in full: **Thalren Deepbloom**, an herbalist in the Craftsman's District. The party first detoured to the **Wizard's Tower library**, a self-organizing archive run by a magical owl pedestal. Akasha's Deep Bloom query produced *The Ins and Outs of Herbs by Thalren Deepbloom*. Avon's Wardstone-history query produced a sanitized children's book — and a **second owl that turned around mid-flight** with a darker, embossed volume the library refused to deliver. A halfling librarian named **Tibbles Briarthorne** materialized to test them with a "go ahead, take any book" trap; Mist baited him into a **Nat-20-on-Insight monologue with Guidance** that confirmed the Wardstone story was performative theatre.

The Craftsman's District turned dramatic fast. **Thalren Deepbloom** answered the door playing a deliberate cover-act — and dropped it the second they were inside. Akasha's blight, on the *world tree* in Iliora, alarmed him visibly: *"if the World Tree is fully infected, it will spread across the land through the roots... it'll reach far and wide underground."* He pressed a small, peach-pit-sized green glowing **seed of renewal** into Akasha's hand, told her its purpose would reveal itself in time, and bolted east toward Iliora — looking over his shoulder as he went.

Mist flew up and immediately spotted a **green-robed Vale Tempest tail** sneaking after Thalren. The party gave chase across rooftops and alleyways — Talya backflipping off roofs into hay bales, Akasha tripping over her own feet, Mist as bird's-eye spotter — and Mist landed his **Suggestion gambit** on the tail with an Unsettling-Words rider, freezing the agent mid-pursuit. After a small back-and-forth (Talya stumbling 40 feet ahead, Akasha climbing a roof to eavesdrop and getting kicked off), Avon ended up **alone in the alley with the agent and the agent's amulet** — agreeing to wear it in exchange for the truth. He bypassed the 100-gp pearl on Identify via Channel Divinity (Preserve Knowledge), confirmed the amulet was a save-based Charm Person, cast Bless on himself, Mist, and Talya before the others stepped back, and put the amulet on once alone.

What the agent told him was the load-bearing reveal of the campaign: **the Wardstones do not keep a storm at bay**. *"They keep it beneath. They keep it buried."* Massive, not from this world, even the agent's own brain "couldn't process what it is." The Vale Tempest are *keepers of the truth* — they suppress it because public panic would empty Skybreeze. The agent also revealed a **magic-vs-violence schism inside the Vale Tempest** (he's of the magic camp), confirmed his organization did **not** attack the Wardstone at the festival (so Jensen is someone else), and gave Avon their hierarchy: **Initiate** (green) → **Veilbearer** (blue) → **Channeler** (white). The fancy-robe woman from Session 3 was a Channeler named **Lady Avesna**.

The amulet's Charm tried to take Avon — he saved on a 14 CHA — and the agent panicked, threw a Nat-20 punch at Avon's face for 7 damage, and tried to flee. Mist's Suggestion held him on a failed save. After a beat of clarity (*"They will kill me — they always know"*), Avon hard-cast Identify and pinpointed a tracking crystal sewn into the agent's robe. Off came the robe. Off came everything except boxers and shoes. The agent introduced himself: ***"I know you didn't ask, but my name's Rowan."*** Mist offered him asylum-by-utility — disguise him, smuggle him out — and he took the deal. The party bought him a hooded set of civilian clothes for 1 gp and headed to the **Temple of Bahamut** to hide him. Akasha's closing line: *"Yay! Why is it my pet? It's our pet."*

## Key Events

### Act I — The Recap & The Long Rest
- Mist opened with the Session 5 recap (Stillwind Lab → vault construct → Loren the cameo barbarian → frozen tableau → Whisper Prism to Evrin → signing bonus → Mist + Avon keeping the orb-vision secret)

- Long rest in the pre-paid suite at the Crowned Pheasant; everyone wakes refreshed

### Act II — Backstory Swap Over Breakfast
- Avon proposed introductions: *"Should we like introduce each other? Because we haven't done it."* Akasha agreed: breakfast around the table.
- **Avon** — Born in **Byron**, a coastal fishing village far from Skybreeze. Was a guard. Trained on spears, carries 10 javelins (*"I only have seven now"*). A wandering sage introduced him to the wider world of Myrrindar. Halfway to Skybreeze, in **Sevra**, he intervened in a fight on the road, lost badly, and the local clerics took him in — that's when he became a **Cleric of Bahamut**. A vision from Bahamut a month ago told him to come to Skybreeze: *"there seems to be some sort of danger."*
- **Akasha** — Raised in the **Whispering Canopy**, an ancient forest in northwestern Myrrindar where *"the trees hum with the memories of the earth."* Heard the forest's heartbeat as a child — the gift that brought her into the **Circle of the Stars** as their only apprentice. Her quiet life was broken when *"a mysterious creeping blight began to wither the oldest oaks from the inside out."* Tasked by the elders to find the source. Carries a staff carved from a fallen branch of the **mother tree**. Came to Skybreeze on the elders' guidance.
- **Mist** — *"I'm Mist. I grew up in a fairy settlement, and then I got kidnapped, and then..."* Played guarded and visibly tender. Now in Skybreeze trying to make money — performed a couple of notes on his lyre to lighten the mood, but stayed reserved. Avon and Akasha offered to help track the kidnappers; Mist quietly: *"I'd rather not want them to kidnap me again." — "You've got us now."*
- **Talya** — *"I got swept off my ship, washed up near a monastery, and decided it seemed like a pretty good time to go explore the land for a change."* Plus a beat about *"swearing like a sailor the whole time."* Old crew unaccounted for: *"I don't know where they wound up. I got washed off. Don't know if they made it, don't know if it sank."*
- DM (Seerosaur) prompted everyone: *"Why did you decide to continue with this group?"*
  - **Akasha**: *"They seem like a good group. I would be able to find the answers I seek because of this group."*
  - **Mist**: *"For Mist, it's basically a combination of — they seem capable, and following them seems to generate quite a bit of income... and also Mist is just bored. So he wants to have some fun."*
  - **Avon**: *"I see an opportunity to do good with people. It's always nicer to be with a group of like-minded people. If I'm to represent Bahamut, then it should be for good."*
  - **Talya**: *"Better to go with things in numbers, you know. Seems like a capable bunch."*
- Mist, dryly, on the group's track record: *"Yeah, we haven't made a disaster yet, maybe."*

### Act III — Failed Evrin Summon & Picking the Day's Lead
- Mist used the agreed code phrase at the barkeep — barkeep winked: *"Master Evrin will be in touch."* Evrin had already left the night before. Mist pouted, flew back to the table.
- Open leads discussed: the amulet targets (Rufus Dawnstrider, Valo, Regina, Bildren); the orb-vision; the Wardstones; a separate Bahamut-temple invite for Avon (Carrie-Anne)
- **Akasha revealed her own quest** for the first time to the party: *"I'm here looking for Deep Bloom — what I was guided here to find. I don't know if it's a plant, a person, or a potion."*
- Group consensus: hit the **Wizard's Tower library** (best magic/history collection); also keep eyes out for amulets in the streets
- Mist Perception 21 on the walk through the Noble District — no visible amulets up here. *"I guess they haven't reached the noble or wealthy people as much."*

### Act IV — The Carrie-Anne Encounter
- Avon Perception 10 — heard *"Sir Avon! Sir Avon!"* in the street and turned around to find **Carrie-Anne**, the Bahamut priestess, polite-bowing and red-faced
- Carrie-Anne: *"All the clerics are so excited to meet you. I told them you were here in Skybreeze."* Avon hedged: he had to finish a library trip first
- Avon casually asked about Deep Bloom — Carrie-Anne went through her mental Rolodex and produced the full name: ***Thalren Deepbloom*** *— "a very skilled herbalist, best in the city."* Shop in the Craftsman's District
- Akasha picked up the answer to Carrie-Anne's *"what do you want with him?"* — Carrie-Anne's face flickered with *"slight disappointment that you answered and not Avon"*
- Akasha (after she left): *"I think somebody has a fan, Avon."*

### Act V — The Wizard's Tower Library
- Wizard's Tower foyer: purple-and-gold banners, statues, a self-opening main door operated by two robed guards
- The library is open-archway, no librarian, no checkout — wizards and initiates studying at long tables. A wooden podium with carved wing-feathers reads **START HERE**
- Akasha pressed *"start"* — nothing. Voice query: ***"Information on Deep Bloom"*** — pedestal glowed purple, an owl materialized from the wood and flew off into the stacks. Returned 2 minutes later with the book and melted back into the pedestal
- Book: ***The Ins and Outs of Herbs by Thalren Deepbloom*** — a green-and-gold-trim encyclopedia of herbs (no recipes, just listings)
- Akasha: *"Well, that is super cool, and I want one for myself."*
- Akasha refused to walk off with it: *"I'm not going to take it. I'm not going to steal from wizards. That's just asking for them to hate me."*
- Avon's second query: history of the **Wardstones** — *two* owls came back. The first delivered ***The Day the Storm Was Sundered*** (a children's-grade history retelling the official storm narrative). The second owl approached with a **dark, leather-bound, embossed book**, stopped mid-air, **turned around**, and flew it back into the stacks
- Avon Insight (with Guidance): the storm book reads as story, not factual record. He flagged the second-owl turnaround as suspicious
- **Akasha rolled the d4 for Guidance** — got a clear read: information here is **being suppressed**. *"There is probably information here that people don't want released."*

### Act VI — Tibbles Briarthorne and the Nat-20 Trap
- Halfling **Tibbles Briarthorne** waddled over in distinguished robes: *"Names Tibbles Briarthorne. I always enjoy anyone following the pursuit of knowledge."*
- Avon asked the rules — Briarthorne offered them a "free" book to walk out with: *"Why don't you call a book and you can go ahead and leave with it. Any book you want."* Akasha's gut: *"I feel like that's a trap. I feel like that's a trap."*
- Mist Insight 12 (with Avon's Guidance): *"He's being a little mischievous."*
- Mist tested it: Mage-Handed *The Day the Storm Was Sundered* through the doorway — at the threshold the book teleported back to the pedestal. Briarthorne: *"It's like I said, you can't take him out!"*
- **Mist's needling probe** — *"Imagine if, like, the Wardstone got destroyed, surely nothing bad's gonna happen, right?"* Briarthorne ramped into a wagging-finger speech about the storm coming back, ships wrecked, 87-years-ago disaster — *"super red in the face... admonishing you"*
- Mist Insight Nat 20 (with Avon's Guidance) → DM: *"a dirty 20, a filthy 20."* Result: *"He's putting it on pretty thick."* Confirmed Briarthorne is performative, not a true believer
- Mist pushed harder — Briarthorne admitted he arrived in Skybreeze **20 years AFTER the storm**, so he wasn't there for the actual event. Mist Insight again (with Guidance): the timeline is plausible (halflings live to ~150) — he's not lying about *that*
- Briarthorne tried to redirect by walking to the podium himself and querying ***"the true story of the Wardstones"*** — owl returned with another sanitized "true story" book, virtually identical to the first children's-grade one. He handed it over: *"see, this is good reading right here."*
- Meanwhile **Mist quietly approached the pedestal** out of Briarthorne's earshot and whispered his own query: ***"Vale Tempest."*** A second owl flew off
- A minute later, the **dark embossed book approached again, stopped mid-air, turned around**, and flew back into the stacks — Mist's Vale Tempest query, refused by the library. Briarthorne went tense: *"What did you ask it to bring?"* Mist deflected ("a magical beast"); Briarthorne threatened to ban them by pulling pedestal records
- An older elf wizard passed by: *"Oh, Briarthorne, you've made such a great system here."* Briarthorne immediately: *"I don't know what she's talking about."* Flagged as a lie
- Briarthorne pivoted: *"I think library's closing. It's library inspection time. Everybody out."* Pushed them out — and an opaque force field sealed the doorway behind them, with him on the inside
- Avon's takeaway: Briarthorne **made the system, runs the suppression, and is not an honest witness**

### Act VII — Thalren Deepbloom
- Craftsman's District ~30-minute walk west. The shop: stone walls and a wooden roof, vines and flowers creeping up the walls and onto the roof. Akasha knocked
- Crash inside, scrambling, running footsteps — door swings open. *"Yes, what can I do for you?"*
- Akasha introduced herself: *"I'm Akasha. I come from the Whispering Canopy."* Thalren visibly clocked the name: *"Oh, you're a long way from home."* He eyed the rest of the party warily — *"there's a lot of you"*
- Akasha mentioned the **blight** — Thalren's whole posture changed. *"Come in, come in, come in, all of you, hurry, hurry."* Door shut. He breathed: *"Hopefully I dropped the act with the rest of you. It is tiring. Not everybody is so trustworthy around here."*
- Real Thalren: tattered robes, white undershirt, dirt-stained slacks (gardener's clothes), scattered papers, packing fast
- Akasha: blight has reached the **mother tree** in Iliora. Thalren: *"Then we have little time."*
- Thalren, alarmed: *"If the World Tree is fully infected by anything, it will spread across the land through the roots. It won't stay in Iliora. It'll reach far and wide underground. It will all be infected."*
- For a blight to affect a world tree, *"it has to be a very powerful magic."* Other blights he's seen were natural — this one isn't
- Thalren handed Akasha a small (peach-pit-sized) **green glowing seed** from a locked box: ***"Take this. It will guide you in your travels... It's the seed of renewal. Its purpose will reveal itself to you in time."***
- He confirmed he was being **watched** ("they're keeping tabs on me") — declined to identify *"they"* — packed and moved fast
- *"I must venture to Iliora myself."* Stepped outside, dropped the act mid-conversation as he hit the street, jogged east

### Act VIII — Spotting the Vale Tempest Tail
- Mist flew up to look around — Perception (advantage, flight) → **21**. Spotted a **green-robed figure** skulking through alleys, clearly tailing Thalren, clearly not aware of the party
- Whispered to the party: *"We're being followed by the green person."*
- Group decision: follow Thalren far enough to keep him safe to the city limits

### Act IX — The Rooftop Chase
- Athletics/Acrobatics check 1 — *"Talya backflipping off of rooftops into hay bales"* (DM); Mist airborne as spotter (no roll); Avon 14 (ungraceful but keeping up); Akasha low → with d4 Guidance → keeping pace, *"kind of tripping over things"*
- Mist: *"Parkour!"*
- Avon: ***"No man left behind."***
- Round 2 of checks — Avon 18 (gaining ground), Akasha 16 (also better), Talya still way out front *"right on his heels"*
- Mist (overhead) saw the Vale Tempest tail also start sprinting — Thalren felt himself being followed and was now booking it east
- Range to the tail: an alley over. Mist could see him from above; the others could not
- DM noted: **Talya was 40 feet ahead of the rest of the group** when the party stopped to deal with the tail

### Act X — Mist's Suggestion + Talya's Stumble
- Mist: *"I have suggestion."* Spent the 2nd-level slot. Concentration spell, up to 8 hours
- **Unsettling Words rider** — burned a Bardic Inspiration use, rolled the d6 → −6 to the agent's next save
- Suggestion: ***"Stop right there and prepare to answer any question you're being asked truthfully."***
- Save: agent rolled an 11 even *without* the d6 → with -6, **5**, fail. Stopped dead in the alley
- Meanwhile **Talya rolled Acrobatics 10** — stumbled over a wheelbarrow, looked back, realized her friends weren't there. Circled back 40 feet to rejoin the party
- Group reunited in a slightly larger alley, started the interrogation

### Act XI — The Alley Interrogation (Group Questioning)
- Mist, opening: *"Why are you following the guy?"* — Agent: *"We had to make sure he wasn't a risk."*
- Iterative questioning (agent has to answer truthfully under Suggestion):
  - Risk to what? *"Skybreeze."* Why? *"He's one of the ones that knows. The truth of what's below."*
  - *"What is below?"* — *"If I tell you, you'll be a risk, and you'll need to be contained."*
  - Mist landed it: *"You mean — like an amulet?"* Agent's gaze snapped over: *"What do you know of amulets?"*
- Agent reached into his robe, produced an amulet: ***"Put this on and I will tell you everything."***
- Avon used **Channel Divinity (Preserve Knowledge)** to cast **Identify** without the 100-gp pearl. Reading: *"This item remotely casts Charm Person. Active only when worn around the neck. There is a save."*
- While Avon read — Mist kept questioning the still-Suggested agent:
  - **Vale Tempest organizational purpose**: *"To keep everyone safe. To keep Skybreeze safe."*
  - Knows **Jensen**? *"I don't know who that is."* (Confirms Jensen is *not* Vale Tempest — he's a separate faction)
  - Akasha described the **Fancy Robe woman** from Session 3 — agent: *"That's one of our Channelers."* Generic role descriptions: *"Channelers oversee Veilbearers; Veilbearers corral the Initiates."* (Specific names and color mappings came later, in Act XII.)

### Act XII — The Amulet Reveal (Avon Alone)
- Plan: Avon wears the amulet alone, others stay out of the alley
- **Avon cast Bless** on himself, Mist, and Talya (1 minute, concentration) BEFORE the others stepped back — using the pre-departure huddle to stack the deck for the upcoming Charm save
- Akasha rolled Athletics 18 to climb a roof to eavesdrop; Mist tried to fly up — agent saw him → Mist Persuasion/Deception 20 → *"Oh good thinking, we don't want anyone else hearing this"*
- Akasha's leg twitched on the rooftop, kicked a stone off — agent: *"I said alone."* She had to fully leave. Mist also forced fully out of earshot
- **Avon, alone with the agent, in the alley.** Put the amulet on
- Agent's reveal — the load-bearing disclosures:
  - ***"What is not true is that the Wardstones keep a storm at bay."***
  - ***"They keep it beneath. They keep it buried."***
  - What is "it"? *"I don't really know what it is. My brain can't process what it is. They say it's massive. They say it's not even from this world."*
  - **Vale Tempest = "keepers of the truth."** Suppress the truth so panic doesn't empty Skybreeze
  - Hideouts? Yes, more than the one in Session 3. Won't disclose locations. Suggested Avon return to the original hideout to "join us"
  - **Hierarchy**: Initiates (green robes) → **Veilbearers** (blue) → **Channelers** (white). *"They're very particular about their colors."*
  - The Channeler who ran the Session-3 hideout: ***Lady Avesna*** (spelled out E-V-E-S-N-A). Per Rowan, she *"is not the violent type"* — neither were the others who died there. Doesn't know who has replaced them since the raid
  - Agent reports to **Veilbearer Mikos** — refused to give location. *"You can ask as nice as you want. I'm going to pick my life over your niceness."*
  - **Internal Vale Tempest split**: ***"Some of them are crazy. They'll kill anyone who they think is a threat. That's not the way it's supposed to be. We're supposed to keep the secret through magic, not violence."***
  - **Vale Tempest did NOT attack the festival Wardstone**: *"That definitely wasn't us. That's counterproductive. We're trying to keep the Wardstones active."* (So Jensen's faction is something else entirely.)
  - The agent had been tailing Thalren **for weeks**, determining whether he needed an amulet. His finding: *"He is not a threat."*

### Act XIII — Charm Resist & Failed Containment
- Agent pulled a stone, spoke into it quietly. Tingle from the amulet → **Avon CHA save 14 → resists**. Avon faked compliance briefly, then took the amulet off
- Agent panicked: *"It didn't work! Please put it back on!"*
- Agent threw a punch at Avon's face — **NAT 20** — 7 damage. *"I'm sorry, I'm sorry — I just—"* Then turned and tried to bolt
- Avon attempted a tackle (athletics, opposed) — agent had disadvantage (back turned) but his higher-of-two beat Avon's roll. He worms free
- Agent ran a few steps, stopped — Mist's Suggestion still active. **DM gave him another Wisdom save**: 9 + 2 = **11**, vs Mist's spell save 13 → **fails**. Agent comes to a begrudging stop, turns back
- Mist persuasion: *"We're not trying to harm you. We haven't even antagonized you yet. We might even become friends."*

### Act XIV — Tracking, the Robe, and Rowan
- Agent: *"They will kill me. They always know everything we do. I don't know how."*
- Avon hard-cast **Identify** (no ritual time available) → arcane pulse → **the crystal sewn into the agent's robe lights up**. The robe is the tracker
- Mist: *"Just take it off."* Agent strips robe, hood, eventually everything except boxers and shoes — *"I gotta get out of here, I gotta get out of here"* — then freezes again
- Avon offered his oversized dragonborn robes — *"He looks like a child in his dad's clothes"* (DM)
- Mist offered him a deal: come with us, help where you can, we'll smuggle you out. Agent agreed: *"I don't know how much help I can be. I can't really do anything. But I'll do my best."*
- Agent: ***"I know you didn't ask, but my name's Rowan."***
- Mist released the Suggestion concentration
- Avon committed the amulet's shape to memory ("for tracking later") and tossed it onto the rooftop
- DM stat-line check: Rowan is +0 to everything, ~15-20 HP

### Act XV — Heading to the Temple of Bahamut
- Plan: take Rowan to the **Temple of Bahamut** to hide him (Carrie-Anne's invite finally getting cashed in)
- Stopped at a market clothier — bought Rowan a hooded set of civilian clothes for **1 gp**
- Akasha (closing line): ***"Yay! Why is it my pet? It's our pet."***
- Mist (privately, last word of the session): *"At least he can be a shield for us... If you know what I mean."*
