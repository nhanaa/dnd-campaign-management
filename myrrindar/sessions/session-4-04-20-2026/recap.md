## Session Summary

The party woke up the morning after their assault on the Vale Tempest hideout and gathered at a tavern near the docks to plan their next move. With heat on them from the killings, they decided to lay low and pursue the Whisper Prism — an artifact they owe Evrin as repayment for a debt. Avon had a surprising encounter with Carrie-Anne, a priestess of Bahamut who recognized him by reputation and invited him to the local temple. Meanwhile, Silas confronted a mysterious figure watching the party — an agent of Evrin's, reminding them that "everyone doesn't like to wait."

After researching the Stillwind Lab at the library — where Mist sweet-talked a scholar into revealing details about the lab's construct defenses while Silas pocketed children's picture books — the party traveled northwest to the ancient arcane facility. Inside, they fought through crystal-headed constructs and wall-mounted turrets in a tense combat, then navigated trapped hallways deeper into the lab. A lightning trap at the final door punished the party for not checking for traps, and when they finally reached the vault chamber, Silas's attempt to sneak in triggered a massive 10-foot guardian construct: "Prepare to be annihilated."

## Key Events

### Act I — The Morning After (Tavern)
- Party reconvened at a tavern near the docks to plan their next move
- Agreed to pursue the **Whisper Prism** for Evrin instead of investigating the amulet targets — too much heat from the Vale Tempest killings
- **Avon** met **Carrie-Anne**, a priestess of Bahamut who recognized him by reputation from Sevra and invited him to visit the Skybreeze temple
- **Silas** spotted a suspicious figure watching the group, confronted him — the man delivered a message from Evrin: "Everyone doesn't like to wait. We're watching."
- Silas revealed he used to be on the same crew as Evrin — Silas was the top member, Evrin was third

### Act II — Library Research
- Party went to the Skybreeze library to research the Stillwind Lab
- **Mist** charmed a scholar using Silver Tongue (rolled an 8 on the d20, treated as 10 by the feat, total 17) to learn: the lab was an old construct-making facility, abandoned 87 years ago during the Arcane Storm, still has active defenses and possibly treasure
- **Avon** located a map showing the lab's exact position among the northwest ruins
- **Silas** searched for information on the Whisper Prism (failed) but successfully stole three children's picture books, including *The Timid Owlbear*
- Party learned constructs are likely resistant to physical damage — need magic or elemental attacks

### Act III — Preparation & Travel
- **Mist** haggled for grease at a general store — played a beautiful song (performance check) that reminded the shopkeeper of home, scored two bottles for the price of one
- **Avon** distributed oil from his supply of ten bottles
- Party discussed elemental options: Mist's Heat Metal, Avon's Acid Breath Weapon, Talya's Elemental Strikes
- Traveled a couple hours northwest to the Stillwind Lab — an unassuming stone structure with a locked entrance

### Act IV — Entering the Stillwind Lab
- **Silas** picked the front door lock (rolled 19, one-handed without even looking)
- Inside: a blue-torch-lit stone room with three 4-foot crystal-headed constructs facing the far wall
- Silas scouted stealthily, used **Minor Illusion** (a glowing orb) to test the constructs — they reacted, walking toward it in eerie unison
- Silas reported back; party decided to enter as a group
- **Avon's** armor clanked on the doorframe — all three constructs activated: "Confirm identity."
- Avon name-dropped **Elias Whitmore** — wrong answer. "INTRUDER! INTRUDER!" with high-pitched screeching

### Act V — Combat: Crystal Constructs & Turrets
- See detailed **Combat Encounters** section below

### Act VI — Deeper into the Lab
- **Silas** picked an arcane-locked door (used Mist's **Bardic Inspiration** to push past DC 15)
- Hallway fire trap triggered — **Silas** dodged with a handstand forward (DEX save 16). **Avon** joked about looking back at the group with finger guns.
- Storage room with an **Evocation trap** detected by Avon's Detect Magic — party carefully avoided it
- Room lined with six deactivated turrets (dim crystals) — Silas tested with Minor Illusion (a splitting image of Mist). No reaction.
- **Akasha** solved an arcane rune door — but it was trapped! **15 lightning damage** to everyone in the hallway. Silas and Talya saved (7 damage), Mist, Avon, and Akasha took the full 15.

### Act VII — The Vault & Cliffhanger
- Vault chamber revealed: two stone pillars, three pedestals with objects, and a massive **10-foot guardian construct** with faintly pulsing purple crystal
- Party took a **short rest** in the previous room to heal up (hit dice)
- Avon cast Detect Magic — only the construct glowed
- **Silas** attempted to sneak in for a surprise attack, kicked a rock — the construct rose to its full height: **"Prepare to be annihilated."**

## Combat Encounters

### Crystal Constructs & Turrets — Stillwind Lab Entrance

**Enemies**: 3 crystal-headed constructs (4 ft., floating/walking, melee, piercing resistance), 2 arcane turrets (wall-mounted, ranged arcane bolt)
**Duration**: 5 rounds (~62 minutes real time)
**Trigger**: Avon's failed stealth + incorrect identity response ("Elias Whitmore" was not the password)

**Round 1:**
- **Turret 1** (appeared from wall) fired Arcane Bolt at Avon — 15 to hit, missed (Avon AC 18). Bolt deflected off chestplate.
- **Avon** moved to center of room, threw javelin at turret 1 — missed (hit the wall).
- **Akasha** shot an arrow at turret 1 — connected but low damage, just a scratch on the crystal.
- **Turret 2** appeared from the opposite wall, targeted Avon — 23 to hit, hit for **6 force damage**.
- **Silas** used Steady Aim + shortbow on turret 1 — **critical hit** (double nat 20s)! 20 total damage (arrow into the arcane bolt barrel, turret nearly exploded but still barely functional).
- **Construct A** floated to Avon, slammed him for **7 bludgeoning damage**. Avon down to 12 HP.
- **Talya** activated **Elemental Attunement (Acid)** for the first time — first swing at turret 2 missed (7 to hit), second swing hit for **7 acid damage**. Turret corroding.
- **Mist** used **Vicious Mockery** on turret 1 (failed WIS save with a 5) for 3 psychic damage and disadvantage on its next attack. Bonus action: **Healing Word** on Avon for **8 HP** (back to 20).
- **Constructs B & C** entered combat at the tail end (rolled natural 1s for initiative). Construct B attacked Avon — 16 to hit, missed (AC 18). Construct C's attack was abandoned by DM.

**Round 2:**
- **Avon** used **Acid Breath Weapon** (15-ft cone) targeting construct A and turret 2. Both failed the DC 12 DEX save (construct A rolled 1, turret 2 rolled 9). **Turret 2 destroyed** — fell off the wall. Construct A sizzling and corroding from acid.
- **Akasha** moved closer, club + sickle combo on a construct — club hit for **5 damage**, sickle hit for **2 damage**. Total 7, but "I would not call it bloodied."
- **Turret 1** targeted Avon at **disadvantage** (from Vicious Mockery) — rolled a 2, shot into the ceiling.
- **Silas** used Cunning Action to disengage, moved away, shot at turret 1 — missed (purple flashes blinded him). No Steady Aim since he moved.
- **Construct A** swung at Avon — 16 to hit, missed (AC 18 with shield making the difference).
- **Talya** Elemental Strikes (acid) on construct B — first swing missed (11), second hit for **4 acid damage**. First hit landed on construct B.
- **Mist** conserved spell slots, fired crossbow at turret 1 for **10 damage**. DM initially applied piercing resistance, then corrected — turret takes full force, now "bloodied." Gave **Bardic Inspiration** to Silas.
- **Constructs B & C** attacked Akasha (missed) and Talya (15 to hit, **8 damage**).

**Round 3:**
- **Avon** attempted a melee strike on construct A — missed.
- **Akasha** club + sickle combo again — club hit but minimal damage ("scrapes it with no visible damage"), sickle missed.
- **Turret 1** fired at Avon — total 13, missed.
- **Silas** Steady Aim + shortbow at turret 1 with sneak attack — hit. Damage shattered it. **Turret 1 destroyed.**
- **Construct A** hit Avon for **6 damage** (down to 14 HP).
- **Talya** Elemental Strikes (acid) on construct B — first hit for **7 acid damage** ("almost bloodied"), second hit punched a hole right through its chest with acid.
- **Mist** crossbow-shot construct B right in the exposed crystal — **killed it!** "Take that, you stupid construct!"
- **Construct C** swung at Akasha — missed.

**Round 4:**
- **Avon** cast **Cure Wounds** on himself (healed to 20 HP).
- **Akasha** switched to **Starry Wisp** for the first time in combat — "Can I do the starry thingy?" 15 to hit, hit for radiant damage. Construct C now "bloodied."
- **Silas** Steady Aim + shortbow on construct C with sneak attack — 7 base + 8 sneak attack = **15 total damage**. Arrow hit the crystal in its chest, it shattered out. **Construct C destroyed!**
- Only construct A remains.
- **Construct A** hit Avon for **4 damage** (23 to hit).
- **Talya** air-punched construct A from range with Elemental Strikes (acid) — hit for **9 damage**, bloodied it. Second swing missed.
- **Mist** crossbow shot for 9 damage — DM applied **piercing resistance** this time, reduced damage.

**Round 5:**
- **Avon** hit construct A with spear for **10 damage** — nearly finished it. Construct jittering with ~1 HP.
- **Akasha** used **Starry Wisp** — **critical hit!** Maximum 8 radiant damage on 1 HP remaining. **Construct A destroyed.** Earned 4 stunt points, saved for +2 AC carry-over.

**Results**: All 3 constructs and 2 turrets destroyed in 5 rounds. Avon tanked the majority of hits (AC 18 with shield). Constructs resist piercing damage — arrows and crossbow bolts did reduced damage. No party members downed.

**Kill Credit:**
- Turret 2: **Avon** (Acid Breath Weapon)
- Turret 1: **Silas** (Steady Aim + Sneak Attack)
- Construct B: **Mist** (crossbow)
- Construct C: **Silas** (Steady Aim + Sneak Attack)
- Construct A: **Akasha** (Starry Wisp critical hit)
