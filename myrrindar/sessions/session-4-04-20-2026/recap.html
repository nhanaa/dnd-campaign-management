Session Summary

The party woke up the morning after their assault on the Vale Tempest hideout and gathered at a tavern near the docks to plan their next move. With heat on them from the killings, they decided to lay low and pursue the Whisper Prism — an artifact they owe Evrin as repayment for a debt. Avon had a surprising encounter with Carrie-Anne, a priestess of Bahamut who recognized him by reputation and invited him to the local temple. Meanwhile, Silas confronted a mysterious figure watching the party — an agent of Evrin's, reminding them that “everyone doesn't like to wait.”

After researching the Stillwind Lab at the library — where Mist sweet-talked a scholar into revealing details about the lab's construct defenses while Silas pocketed children's picture books — the party traveled northwest to the ancient arcane facility. Inside, they fought through crystal-headed constructs and wall-mounted turrets in a tense five-round combat, then navigated trapped hallways deeper into the lab. A lightning trap at the final door punished the party for not checking for traps, and when they finally reached the vault chamber, Silas's attempt to sneak in triggered a massive 10-foot guardian construct: “Prepare to be annihilated.”

Combat Encounter

Crystal Constructs & Turrets — Stillwind Lab
5 Rounds

Enemies: 3 crystal-headed constructs (4 ft., floating, melee, piercing resist) + 2 arcane turrets (wall-mounted, ranged)

Initiative: Turret 1 → Avon → Akasha → Turret 2 → Silas → Construct A → Talya → Mist → Constructs B & C

Trigger: Avon's failed stealth + incorrect identity response (“Elias Whitmore” was not the password)

Round 1
  • Turret 1 — Arcane Bolt at Avon — 15 to hit, miss (AC 18). Bolt deflected off chestplate.
  • Avon — Moved to center, threw javelin at turret 1 — miss (hit the wall).
  • Akasha — Arrow at turret 1 — hit but low damage, “just a scratch on the crystal.”
  • Turret 2 — Emerged from opposite wall. Arcane Bolt at Avon — 23 to hit, 6 force damage.
  • Silas — Steady Aim + shortbow at turret 1 — CRIT (double nat 20s)! 20 damage. Arrow into the arcane bolt barrel, turret nearly exploded.
  • Construct A — Floated to Avon, slam — 21 to hit, 7 bludgeoning damage. Avon at 12 HP.
  • Talya — Activated Elemental Attunement (Acid). First swing miss (7). Second hit turret 2 for 7 acid damage — corroding.
  • Mist — Vicious Mockery on turret 1 (failed WIS save, rolled 5) — 3 psychic damage + disadvantage. Bonus: Healing Word on Avon for 8 HP.
  • Constructs B & C — Entered late (rolled nat 1s). B attacked Avon — 16, miss (AC 18). C's attack abandoned by DM.
Round 2
  • Avon — Acid Breath Weapon (15-ft cone) at construct A + turret 2. Both failed DC 12 DEX (construct rolled 1, turret rolled 9). Turret 2 DESTROYED — fell off wall. Construct A corroding.
  • Akasha — Club + sickle combo on a construct — club hit for 5, sickle hit for 2. Total 7 damage.
  • Turret 1 — Arcane Bolt at Avon with disadvantage (Vicious Mockery) — rolled a 2. Shot into the ceiling.
  • Silas — Cunning Action disengage, moved, shortbow at turret 1 — miss. Purple flashes blinded him.
  • Construct A — Swung at Avon — 16 to hit, miss (AC 18, shield making the difference).
  • Talya — Elemental Strikes (acid) on construct B — first miss (11), second hit for 4 acid.
  • Mist — Crossbow at turret 1 for 10 damage (DM initially applied resistance, then corrected — full force). Turret now bloodied. Bonus: Bardic Inspiration to Silas.
  • Constructs B & C — B at Akasha, miss. C hit Talya for 8 damage.
Round 3
  • Avon — Melee strike on construct A — miss.
  • Akasha — Club + sickle again — club hit but minimal damage, sickle miss.
  • Turret 1 — Arcane Bolt at Avon — total 13, miss.
  • Silas — Steady Aim + shortbow + sneak attack at turret 1 — hit. Turret 1 DESTROYED.
  • Construct A — Hit Avon for 6 damage. Avon at 14 HP.
  • Talya — Acid punches on construct B — first hit for 7 acid, second hit punched a hole through its chest.
  • Mist — Crossbow at construct B — 15 to hit, 10 damage, bolt hit the exposed crystal. Construct B DESTROYED. “Take that, you stupid construct!”
  • Construct C — Swung at Akasha — miss.
Round 4
  • Avon — Cure Wounds on himself — healed to 20 HP.
  • Akasha — Starry Wisp (first time in combat) — 15 to hit, radiant damage. Construct C now bloodied. “Can I do the starry thingy?”
  • Silas — Steady Aim + shortbow + sneak attack — 15 total damage. Crystal shattered. Construct C DESTROYED.
  • Construct A — Hit Avon for 4 damage.
  • Talya — Air-punched construct A from range — hit for 9 acid, construct bloodied. Second swing miss.
  • Mist — Crossbow at construct A — 22 to hit, 9 damage, but piercing resistance reduced it.
Round 5
  • Avon — Spear attack on construct A — hit for 10 damage. Construct jittering with ~1 HP.
  • Akasha — Starry Wisp — CRIT! Max 8 radiant on 1 HP. Construct A DESTROYED. Earned 4 stunt points → +2 AC carry-over.
Result: All 3 constructs and 2 turrets destroyed in 5 rounds. Avon tanked the majority of hits (AC 18 with shield). Constructs resist piercing — magic and acid proved far more effective. No party members downed.

Kill credit: Turret 2 — Avon (Acid Breath) • Turret 1 — Silas (Sneak Attack) • Construct B — Mist (Crossbow) • Construct C — Silas (Sneak Attack) • Construct A — Akasha (Starry Wisp Crit)

  • Should we return the Whisper Prism to Evrin immediately, or is there leverage in holding it?
  • Avon's temple visit — what do the Bahamut clerics know about him? Could be a sidequest hook.
