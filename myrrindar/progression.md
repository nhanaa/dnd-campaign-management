## Current: Level 2

## Planned Subclass: College of Eloquence (Tasha's Cauldron of Everything)

*DM (Seerosaur) allows Tasha's and most other supplements alongside 2024 PHB base class.*

## Level-by-Level Plan (Tier 2 Focus: Levels 3–10)

### Level 3 — Eloquence Online

**New Features:**
- **Silver Tongue**: Deception/Persuasion rolls of 9 or lower treated as 10. Minimum result: 17.
- **Unsettling Words** (BA): Spend 1 Bardic Inspiration → target subtracts die from next save. Pairs with any ally's save spell.
- **Fairy Magic**: *Faerie fire* (1/long rest, free, CHA-based). AoE advantage for allies, guarantees Silas's Sneak Attack.

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Hold Person** — Paralyzed = auto-crit within 5 ft. Talya and Silas shred the target. Use Unsettling Words first to tank their save.
- Swap Charm Person → **Blindness/Deafness** — Non-concentration debuff. Blinded = disadvantage on their attacks + advantage on attacks against them. Stack on top of any concentration spell.

| Spell | Lvl | Conc? | Role |
|-------|-----|-------|------|
| Bane | 1st | Yes | AoE debuff (-d4 attacks/saves) |
| Tasha's Hideous Laughter | 1st | Yes | Single-target incapacitate |
| Dissonant Whispers | 1st | No | Damage + forced movement → provokes OAs |
| Healing Word | 1st | No | Emergency BA pickup |
| Hold Person | 2nd | Yes | Paralysis (auto-crits for melee allies) |
| Blindness/Deafness | 2nd | **No** | Blinded debuff, stacks with concentration |

### Level 4 — War Caster

**Feat: War Caster**
- Advantage on concentration saves (~80% pass rate on DC 10 vs 55% without)
- Vicious Mockery as opportunity attack — enemy disengages and eats disadvantage
- Somatic components with hands full (hold lyre and cast)

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Suggestion** — "I suggest you put down your weapon and leave." Removes a creature from a fight entirely. Also dominates social encounters. Concentration, but War Caster now protects it.
- New cantrip available (3rd Bard cantrip). Consider: **Minor Illusion** (creative distraction/deception tool) or **Mind Sliver** (INT save, d6 psychic + subtract d4 from next save — another debuff layer).

---

### Level 5 — The Power Spike

**New Features:**
- **Font of Inspiration**: Bardic Inspiration recharges on **Short Rest**. Game-changing — Unsettling Words is now practically free every fight.
- **Bardic Inspiration die**: d6 → d8. Bigger debuff on Unsettling Words, bigger buff on inspiration.
- **Fairy Magic**: *Enlarge/reduce* (1/long rest, free, CHA-based). Shrink an enemy (-d4 damage, disadvantage on STR) or enlarge Talya (+d4 damage).
- **3rd-level spell slots unlock.**

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Hypnotic Pattern** — The best AoE control spell in tier 2. 30-ft cube, WIS save or *incapacitated* and speed 0. Ends entire encounters. Use Unsettling Words on the scariest enemy first.
- Swap consideration: Tasha's Hideous Laughter → **Slow** (if you want). Tasha's and Hold Person overlap as single-target concentration shutdowns. **Slow** hits up to 6 creatures: -2 AC, halved speed, no reactions, can only use Action OR Bonus Action. Creatures save individually each turn, so it stays sticky. *However*, Tasha's is still great for lower-slot use against one big target — your call.

---

### Level 6 — Eloquence Peak

**New Features:**
- **Unfailing Inspiration**: When a creature uses your Bardic Inspiration die and fails the roll, **they keep the die**. Your inspiration is never wasted. Combined with Font of Inspiration (short rest recharge), you're handing these out constantly.
- **Universal Speech**: Speak to any creature regardless of language (PB uses/long rest). Amazing for social encounters in an unfamiliar world.

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Fear** (if you kept Tasha's) or **Slow** (if you swapped Tasha's out at 5). Both are elite AoE debuffs.
  - Fear: 30-ft cone, WIS save or frightened + forced dash away. Shuts down an advancing group.
  - Slow: 6 targets in 120 ft, WIS save. More surgical targeting, brutal debuff, individual saves to end.

---

### Level 7 — 4th-Level Spells

**New Features:**
- **Countercharm** (base Bard feature): Action to give advantage on saves vs. frightened/charmed to allies within 30 ft. Situational but free.

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Polymorph** — The most versatile 4th-level spell.
  - Offensive: WIS save or become a turtle. Use Unsettling Words first. Target removed from fight.
  - Defensive: Turn a dying ally into a Giant Ape (157 HP). Emergency panic button.
  - Creative: Turn yourself into a flying creature for scouting, or a whale above an enemy.

---

### Level 8 — CHA to 18

**Feat: Half-feat with +1 CHA (17 → 18, spell DC 13 → 14, spell attack +5 → +6)**

Best options:
| Feat | Benefit | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Actor** | +1 CHA, advantage on Deception/Performance to impersonate | Thematically perfect for the mischief king. Impersonate anyone flawlessly with Silver Tongue + Expertise. |
| **Skill Expert** | +1 CHA, 1 new proficiency, 1 new Expertise | More mechanical power. Triple expertise (Deception, Persuasion, + one more). |

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Greater Invisibility** — Cast on Silas for permanent advantage (= guaranteed Sneak Attack) and enemies have disadvantage to hit him. Concentration but incredibly slot-efficient since it lasts the whole fight.

---

### Level 9 — The Crown Jewel (5th-Level Spells)

**New Features:**
- **Additional Expertise** (base Bard 2024): Gain Expertise in 2 more skills.

**Spells:**
- Learn: **Synaptic Static** — THE debuff king spell.
  - 20-ft radius, INT save
  - 8d6 psychic damage
  - On fail: subtract **d6 from attack rolls, ability checks, AND concentration saves** for 1 minute
  - **No concentration. No save to end early.** Just lingers.
  - Stack this on top of Hypnotic Pattern, Bane, or anything else. Pure debuff layering.

---

### Level 10 — Magical Secrets (Base Bard 2024)

**Feature: Magical Secrets** — From now on, whenever you add new prepared spells, you can choose from **Bard, Cleric, Druid, and Wizard** spell lists. Replacements can also come from those lists.

**Top picks from other lists:**
| Spell | Source | Why |
|-------|--------|-----|
| **Counterspell** | Wizard | Shut down enemy casters. 3rd-level reaction. "No." |
| **Wall of Force** | Wizard | 5th-level, indestructible dome/wall. Split encounters in half. No save, no HP, can't be dispelled. |
| **Spirit Guardians** | Cleric | 3rd-level, concentration. AoE damage aura + halved speed. If you're ever in a tight space. |
| **Slow** | Wizard | If you haven't picked it up yet, now's the time. |
