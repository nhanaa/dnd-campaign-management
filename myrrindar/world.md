# World — Myrrindar: Winds of Warding

## System
- **System:** D&D 5e (2024 PHB)
- **DM:** Seerosaur
- **Setting:** Myrrindar (fully homebrew world)

## Pre-Campaign Timeline

- **3/15/26**: Invited to campaign. Pax initially considered fighter/melee, settled on Bard.
- **3/19/26**: Discussed backstory with DM — Fairy from a settlement, captured as a child, escaped to the streets of Skybreeze.
- **4/4/26**: Named the fairy settlement **Neulvyn**.
- **4/20/26**: Finalized Mist's backstory — captured by fae-traffickers while exploring outside Neulvyn with friends; sold to a noble collector in Skybreeze; escaped during a scandal by tricking a stranger into releasing him; survived on the streets; mentored briefly by an elven bard named **Eldrin** who taught him music and magic before leaving Skybreeze. Entire arc spans the last 2-3 years.

## Key Locations

- **Myrrindar** — A continent inhabited by all manner of mystical races. Major cities: Iliora (woodland, elves), Dun Caeldor (snowy fortress, dwarves), and Skybreeze (capital, all races). Not much is known beyond the northern mountain ranges where Dun Caeldor lies — expeditions beyond that point never return.
- **Skybreeze** — Capital city on the east side of Myrrindar. Defined by the ring of magical wardstones around the city edge that ward off an Arcane Storm that threatened the city ~87 years ago. A Wardstone Festival is held annually. Governed by a Merchant Council. Districts: Noble District, Dock District, Church District, Market District (weapons/armor), The Commons (adventuring gear).
- **Neulvyn** — Fairy settlement where Mist was born (player-named)
- **Vale Tempest Hideout** — Secret underground complex accessed via a hidden door in an alley in central Skybreeze. Contains: lounge, secret room with Wardstone map, amulet assembly/enchanting room (staffed), brewery (unused), dining room, kitchen. Party discovered and partially cleared it in Sessions 2-3.
- **Stillwind Lab** — Ancient arcane laboratory northwest of Skybreeze, a couple hours walk. Used to manufacture constructs/automatons. Abandoned 87 years ago during the Arcane Storm. Still has active defenses: crystal-headed constructs (4 ft., piercing resistant), wall-mounted arcane turrets, fire and lightning traps, arcane-locked doors. Lit by blue torches. **Vault chamber cleared in Session 5** — Vault Guardian and two arcane turrets destroyed; three pedestals empty (Whisper Prism + Bag of Beans removed; Mysterious Orb depleted in place); chest looted. Lab now safely traversable.
- **The Crowned Pheasant** — Upscale tavern in the Skybreeze Noble District. Stone floors, stained-glass windows, very fancy. Evrin holds court here at a private table. The barkeep wears a long-sleeved white shirt, red vest, spectacles, and bow tie. Pre-paid suites available for Evrin's contractors. Code phrase to summon Evrin: ask the barkeep to *"purchase a seagull."*
- **Temple of Bahamut (Skybreeze)** — Bahamut temple in Skybreeze. Clerics there know of Avon from Sevra. Not yet visited.

Wears an enchanted amulet (Enchantment magic, silver shield with black runes) placed by the Vale Tempest that suppresses his true thoughts. Without it, he questions the Wardstone narrative and experiences severe mental distress. Freed by party in Session 3 with amulet left on.
- **Jensen** — Leader behind the Wardstone sabotage at the Festival (Session 1). Claims the wardstones are "sealing" something beneath Skybreeze. Escaped via smoke bomb. Currently at large.
- **Rufus Dawnstrider** — Dock Foreman. Vale Tempest target, wears an enchanted amulet ("moderate success, reapplication recommended"). Mist knows him personally from the streets.
- **Valo, Regina, Bildren** — City guards. Vale Tempest targets with amulets ("limited success, low awareness, stronger amulets recommended").
- **Blitt** — Dragonborn, Vale Tempest member (Green Robe). Killed by party in Session 3.
- **Avarian** — Drow, Vale Tempest member (Green Robe). Killed by party in Session 3.
- **Garrett** — Vale Tempest member (Green Robe). Killed by party in Session 3.
- **Fancy Robe Woman** — Unnamed Vale Tempest leader (or high-ranking member). Enchants the amulets. Still alive and unaware of what happened to the others (as of end of Session 3).
- **Evrin** — Patron of the party. Black velvet tuxedo, speaks with practiced upscale diction (surprising for a former criminal). Was in Silas's old crew — third-ranking, looked up to Silas, "almost left with him" when Silas departed. Holds court at the Crowned Pheasant in the Noble District. Pitches a populist reformer-criminal vision: *"We don't shy away from dirty jobs, but ultimately I'd like to turn my organization into something more legitimate. Lift the bottom up, not just the rich."* Visibly concerned about Silas's disappearance (Mist + Loren caught the hide on Insight 17/21). Code phrase to summon him: leave a message with the Crowned Pheasant barkeep asking to *"purchase a seagull."* Took the Whisper Prism in a metal-lined box at end of S5. Pre-paid suites at the Crowned Pheasant for the party. Pays fairly. Wants the party as long-term operatives — Mist negotiated a signing bonus (Persuasion 25) for joining; Talya in; Akasha extended-alliance-only; Avon silent.
- **Loren Lyster** *(Session 5 cameo)* — Human Barbarian (Path of the Zealot). 4'10", blonde-haired. Professional contractor employed by Evrin's organization, primarily security work. Joined the Vault Guardian fight late, killed the construct in one swing (Reckless Greataxe + Savage Attacker + max Divine Fury Radiant = 19 damage). Refused a treasure cut. Read on Evrin: dirty jobs aren't noble, but he's honest in his dealings and pays fairly. Departed after escorting party to the Crowned Pheasant. Implied she'd return if hired by Evrin again.
- **Sylvia Plath** — Traveling musician. Loren previously did security for her. Mist knows of her professionally — well-known in Skybreeze, with a thread of jealousy in Mist's reaction to her name.
- **Eldrin** — Elven bard who mentored Mist on the streets of Skybreeze ~1-2 years ago. Taught him music and how to channel fae magic with intention. Left Skybreeze at some point; destination and reason unknown. Seen by Mist as the closest thing to family since Neulvyn — and the second person to vanish from his life.
- **Mist's Unnamed Noble Captor** — Wealthy Skybreeze noble who purchased Mist from fae-traffickers and kept him as part of a private collection of fairies and mythical creatures. "Cared for" Mist in the sense of feeding and clothing him, but kept him caged. Caught in an unspecified scandal that collapsed his household; current fate unknown. Identity and house to be determined by DM.
- **Carrie-Anne** — Priestess of Bahamut at the Skybreeze temple. Recognized Avon by reputation from Sevra. Invited him to visit the temple. Bashful and reverent toward him. The Skybreeze clerics have "heard of his tales."

## Items

- **Whisper Prism** — Divination magic. Emits a cacophony of indecipherable whispers within ~10 feet. The whispers seem to focus on certain holders (focused on Akasha when she grabbed it) but never become legible. Delivered to Evrin at end of Session 5 in a metal-lined box. Even with the box closed at a tavern table, surrounding patrons heard the whispers when it was momentarily uncovered (one woman started crying).
- **Bag of Beans** — Magical (Evocation/Conjuration mix). Seven beans of varying colors inside a small leather bag (~3.5 lb total). Lore: planting one bean and watering it produces an effect; dumping the bag empties causes them all to explode. Mist pocketed in S5; pending experimentation.
- **Mysterious Orb (depleted)** — Conjuration magic. Was a vision-projector that triggered when Avon's Identify and Mist's touch landed simultaneously, transporting Mist + Avon into the frozen tableau (see below). Now an empty crystal sphere — the swirling mist inside is gone. Currently with the party.
- **The Frozen Tableau (vision)** — Mist and Avon's S5 vision. Two-part frozen-in-time scene: an outdoor battle (a fighter mid-leap stabbing a defenseless target; arrows mid-flight at another victim's back) and a sheared-in-half building containing a ritual (three robed figures, one kneeling in prayer, another about to bring a morning star down on the kneeler's skull). A stone pedestal holds two objects, with a third — a **red crystal in ornate gold housing** — fallen on the floor. Magic does not work in the tableau. Figures can be physically transposed. Avon has Insight-19 mental notes for library research. Architecture, clothing, and faces all foreign — no recognition by either character.

## Factions

### Fae Traffickers
- Organized group specializing in capturing fairies and other magical creatures to sell to wealthy collectors.
- Captured Mist as a child outside Neulvyn along with a group of his friends (~2-3 years ago).
- Operational details unknown — equipped to ambush fae, knew Neulvyn's location. Likely still active.
- Mist's captured friends' fates are unknown; they may still be in the traffickers' hands or sold to other collectors across Myrrindar.

### Vale Tempest
- Secret society dedicated to "protecting the truth" about the Wardstones
- Believes the Wardstones protect Skybreeze from the Arcane Storm — considers any other narrative dangerous "lies"
- Uses enchanted amulets to mind-control key citizens (researchers, dock workers, guards) into maintaining the Wardstone narrative
- Members wear green robes; leaders have blue accents; insignia: clenched fist with wind around it; leader's symbol: thunderbolt within a cage
- Has an amulet assembly operation in their hideout with a recent breakthrough making amulets stronger
- Party killed several members in Session 3; the Fancy Robe woman and at least 2 others remain

## Story

### Session 1 — Wardstone Festival
The party met at Skybreeze for individual reasons. During the annual Wardstone Festival, an unknown faction (led by Jensen) attempted to sabotage a wardstone. The party stopped a distraction in the center square, then intercepted the actual disruption ritual in the northeast. Jensen escaped via smoke bomb. Two party members were downed. The party recovered at a nearby Meadery with help from a local cleric.

### Session 2 — The Hidden Door
The party agreed to pay back Evrin's debt and retrieve a Whisper Prism. They met with Elias Whitmore, noticed his amulet glowing and suppressing his thoughts. Silas lifted the amulet. Following Elias and a green-robed escort into an alley, the party discovered a secret door to a hideout. Inside: a room with a map of Skybreeze's wardstones (lines pointing to the city center), non-working amulets, and Vale Tempest members. Session ended with Blitt approaching the party in the secret corridor.

### Session 3 — The Veiled Truth (4/13/26)
Party confronted Vale Tempest members in the hideout. Won a combat in the lounge (2 KO'd, 2 surrendered). Interrogated the Blue Robe lieutenant who revealed the organization's name (Vale Tempest) and their mission to suppress "lies" about the Wardstones. Silas stealthily removed Elias's amulet — Elias started questioning the Wardstone narrative but collapsed in agony. Akasha wild-shaped into a mouse to scout the amulet assembly room. Silas found a journal listing five enchanted targets in the city. Party voted to kill the incapacitated Vale Tempest members, freed Elias (with amulet), and withdrew. Leveled up to 3.

### Session 4 — The Stillwind Lab (4/20/26)
Party decided to pursue the Whisper Prism for Evrin, laying low after the Vale Tempest killings. At the tavern, Avon met Carrie-Anne (Bahamut priestess who recognized him from Sevra) and Silas confronted one of Evrin's agents watching the group. Researched the Stillwind Lab at the library — Mist charmed a scholar for intel, Avon found a map. Traveled to the lab, fought 3 crystal constructs and 2 turrets (5 rounds, no KOs). Navigated trapped hallways deeper into the lab — Akasha solved an arcane rune door but triggered a lightning trap (15 damage). Reached the vault chamber with three pedestals and a 10-foot guardian construct. Session ended on a cliffhanger: "Prepare to be annihilated."

**Post-session:** Silas's player (Drew/Aenian) left the campaign that evening after a Discord rules dispute with the DM over 2024 stealth RAW (whether leaving cover breaks the Invisible condition conferred by Hide). In-fiction handling of Silas's departure is TBD. Party enters Session 5 at 4 players — combat is still solid (Avon is the true tank via heavy armor + shield + STR, Talya handles skirmisher melee), but the party loses dedicated skill coverage for lockpicking, trap detection, and stealth. Mist or Talya can absorb the stealth/sleight-of-hand lane if needed.

### Session 5 — The Whisper Prism (4/27/26)
The Vault Guardian and two arcane turrets fell over 5 rounds. **Loren** (a Barbarian cameo, contractor for Evrin) burst in mid-combat and killed the Vault Guardian in Round 3 with a max Divine Fury Radiant greataxe to the neck (19 damage). Akasha killed the Top Turret with a club + sickle 1-2 punch. Talya killed the Lower Turret with two acid Hadouken punches. Mist's Heat Metal sustained the chip damage; Mist's Silvery Barbs reaction nullified a Vault Guardian critical hit on Avon. Friendly-fire moment: Mist's crossbow shot at the Top Turret revealed a previously-unknown reactive 10-foot pulse — Avon and Akasha both took 10 damage on failed DEX saves; Avon's Cure Wounds the same round (13 HP on Akasha) prevented her from dropping. Silas vanished into a magic circle at the start of combat — Avon's active Detect Magic spotted the trigger tile a split-second too late.

Three pedestal items found: the **Whisper Prism**, a **Bag of Beans** (Mist pocketed; History 18 identified), and a **Mysterious Orb**. When Avon used Channel Divinity to cast Identify on the orb and Mist touched it at the same moment, both vanished into a **frozen tableau** — a snapshot of an unknown ritual + battle, every figure mid-action and silent, where no spell worked. Avon (Insight 19) committed faces, weapons, architecture, and a centerpiece **red crystal in ornate gold housing** to memory. They escaped by physically transposing the morning-star-victim figure. The orb is now empty.

**Loren** escorted the party to the **Crowned Pheasant** in the Noble District. **Evrin** waited at a private table in a black velvet tuxedo, took the prism in a metal-lined box, and pitched a long-term contract framed by a populist speech: *"We don't shy away from dirty jobs, but I'd like to turn my organization into something seen as legitimate. Lift the bottom up, not just the rich."* Mist's **Persuasion 25** earned a *"I like your style, kid"* — and pried out a signing bonus for the long-term contract specifically (Evrin: *"I would not pay you a copper more than what we agreed... however, what your friend is suggesting is a more long-term agreement, and in that case a signing bonus would be more than acceptable"*). Akasha refused to renegotiate the *original prism deal* with *"a deal is a deal."* Mist (Insight 6, moved by the speech) signed on for the long-term contract and explicitly cashed in the bonus. Talya signed on; Akasha extended alliance but declined formal membership; Avon stayed silent. Code phrase to summon Evrin: *"purchase a seagull"* to the Crowned Pheasant barkeep. Suite pre-paid for long rest.

Citizens of Skybreeze are now publicly recognizing the party for the **Wardstone Festival defense** (Session 1) — friendly waves, thanks in the streets. Hideout actions remain secret. Mist privately warned Avon to keep the orb-vision a secret from Loren and Evrin — the party investigates the frozen tableau themselves first.
