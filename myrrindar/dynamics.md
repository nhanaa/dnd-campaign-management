### Synergy Notes

- **Dissonant Whispers + Melee Allies**: Forced movement from Dissonant Whispers provokes opportunity attacks from Talya and Silas — position them adjacent first.
- **Bane + Cleric/Druid Save Spells**: Bane reduces enemy saves by 1d4, boosting the hit rate of Avon and Akasha's save-or-suck spells.
- **Two Healers**: Avon (Cleric) is likely the primary healer; Mist's Healing Word is emergency backup — don't overlap.
- **No dedicated tank**: Party is squishy. Mist's debuffs (Vicious Mockery disadvantage, Bane, Tasha's) help compensate.
- **Eloquence Unsettling Words**: At Lv3, spending a Bardic Inspiration use to subtract from an enemy's save pairs perfectly with the Cleric and Druid throwing save-based spells.
