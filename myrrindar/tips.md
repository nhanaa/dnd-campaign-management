# Tips & Tactics — Mist (Fairy Bard)

## Core Strengths

- **Flight**: 30 ft. fly speed is huge at low levels — stay airborne out of melee reach
- **Social Dominance**: +7 Deception and Persuasion with Expertise; +5 Intimidation
- **Bardic Inspiration**: 3/Long Rest, Bonus Action — hand these out early in combat
- **Encouraging Song**: Musician feat lets you give Heroic Inspiration to 2 allies on rest

## Action Economy

### Combat Round Template
1. **Action**: Cast a spell (Vicious Mockery for safe damage + disadvantage, or a leveled spell)
2. **Bonus Action**: Bardic Inspiration to an ally, or Healing Word if someone drops
3. **Movement**: Fly up/away to stay safe — Small size + flight = hard to pin down

### Key Combos
- **Vicious Mockery + Flight**: Stay airborne, impose disadvantage on enemy attacks from range
- **Tasha's Hideous Laughter**: Incapacitate + prone; flying enemies fall. Use on single big threats
- **Dissonant Whispers**: Forced movement provokes opportunity attacks from allies — position allies first
- **Bane**: Debuff up to 3 enemies' attacks and saves; great for multi-enemy fights
- **Healing Word**: 60 ft. range Bonus Action heal — the best emergency pickup in the game

## Spell Slot Management

- Only 3 first-level slots — be selective
- Vicious Mockery is a strong at-will option (damage + disadvantage), don't feel pressured to spend slots
- Save at least 1 slot for Healing Word as emergency healing

## Positioning

- **Fly above melee**: Most enemies can't reach you without ranged attacks
- **Stay within 60 ft.**: That's your Bardic Inspiration and most spell ranges
- **Squeeze into small places**: Fairy trait — use terrain to your advantage (personality trait too!)
- **Light armor only**: Flight doesn't work in medium/heavy armor

## Social Encounters

- Lead social interactions with +7 Deception/Persuasion
- Prestidigitation for flair and minor illusions during performances
- Charm Person for 1-hour advantage on CHA checks against a target (they know afterward)

## Party Synergy Combos

- **Unsettling Words (Lv3) + Avon/Akasha save spells**: Bonus Action to subtract a d6 from enemy's next save, then Cleric or Druid drops a save-or-suck. Devastating combo.
- **Dissonant Whispers + Talya/Silas**: Forced movement provokes opportunity attacks. Position Monk and Rogue adjacent first, then blast with Dissonant Whispers.
- **Bane + Party Saves**: Bane reduces enemy attack rolls AND saves by d4 — helps the whole squishy party survive.
- **Faerie Fire (Lv3, free) + Silas Sneak Attack**: Grants advantage on attacks against affected targets — guarantees Rogue's Sneak Attack even without an adjacent ally.
- **Healing Word + Avon**: Two characters with emergency healing means the party is very hard to wipe. Coordinate — don't double-heal the same turn.

## Weaknesses to Watch

- **Low HP** (13): One solid hit can drop you — stay out of reach
- **Low STR** (-1): Don't get grappled; fly away from anything that grabs
- **Low CON** (+0): Concentration saves are risky — consider War Caster or Resilient (CON) later
- **Concentration heavy**: 3 of 5 spells require concentration; can only maintain one at a time
- **No tank in party**: Nobody is built to absorb hits — debuffs and control are the party's defense
