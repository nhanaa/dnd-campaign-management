## Session Summary

The coastal cave cleanup stretched longer than the party expected. Rune decapitated the sea hag as a trophy, Kane ate the leftover stew and discovered a human finger in it, and the group found the stripped-down remains of the *Bunch o' Knuckle* crew alongside a waterlogged chest with 600 gold. Virel identified the cauldron as a magical stew-maker worth an absurd amount to a starving town. Thors dragged it back to Easthaven on the boat.

At the town hall, Speaker Dennis Whalen begged to buy the cauldron to feed his people. After a tense negotiation, the party accepted ten family gemstones at 500 gp each — one down, nine in two days, a signed contract, and guards posted on the cauldron day and night. Dzaan's wizard bag was finally delivered: a scroll of Fireball, a gray Bag of Tricks, and spell components. Rune took possession of the bag.

The evening unraveled the party more than the loot celebration. Kane and Rune went ice fishing and had the first real heart-to-heart of the campaign — about Selûne, the Order of the Fang, and how it feels to be alone. Virel quietly revealed her interest in forensics and plague-doctor work, born from studying the murder evidence. Meanwhile, Rune and Kane both rolled Insight and realized Virel had fundamentally changed since the party entered The Caer — her alignment had shifted. The necklace from the soothsayer was still on her. Avarath found the frozen Easthaven Ferry at the docks but couldn't spot anything useful alone.

In the morning they boarded the ferry together. Kane wild-shaped into a giant spider; Thors swung Rune (with Brute's blindsight) and Virel (with Detect Magic) around the deck like metal detectors. Rune found a map in the cabin with **Sun Blight Fortress** marked — and Brute's blindsight picked up an invisible duergar right next to him. Combat was brief: the dwarf used Reduce and tried to run, got tagged by Virel's held Fire Bolt (10) and Kane's Giant Spider bite (15) for 25 damage, and Rune restrained him with a longbow + Ensnaring Strike the next round. Thors knocked him unconscious non-lethally with a sling.

Back at the White Lady Inn, they hauled the dwarf to the wine cellar. Virel — now openly enjoying it — heated a corkscrew in a Bonfire cantrip and drove it into the prisoner's eye. Kane healed the prisoner between torture sessions to keep Virel's work going (Healing Word x2: 11 HP, then 10 HP). The prisoner was **Durth, Xardorok's son**. He refused to beg, refused to plead, just kept asking to be killed. Avarath tried persuading him ("Haven't you suffered enough?"), tried reasoning about fatherhood — the prisoner answered that he didn't want love, he wanted respect, and today he'd lost it. He eventually gave them what they already knew: Sun Blight Fortress, the dragon, the heart. Rune auto-crit an arrow into his skull and ended it. Another arrow was already drawn as the screen faded — pointed at no one yet. Session ended on that fracture.

## Key Events

### Act 1 — The Cave Cleanup
- Rune searched the hag and skeleton (rolled 10 — nothing). Decapitated the hag, hung the head from his belt as proof.
- Kane (still in raptor form) ate the hag's leftover stew. Investigation with advantage — 19. Found a spicy broth with a human finger in it. "Good thing I'm a raptor, or this might be cannibalism."
- Thors and Avarath searched the room of stripped bones; found a jacket patched "Bunch o' Knuckleheads" — confirming the fishermen's fate.
- Found a rotting, waterlogged chest under the bones. Rune sleight-of-handed it open: **600 gp**.
- Rune rolled a 26 Investigation on the hag's body — no items, but clocked the cauldron as magical.
- Virel cast **Identify**: Cauldron is a rare wondrous item; pour water + stir 1 min → stew feeds 4 people/gallon, 3x/day, 30-gallon capacity. Can feed the whole town daily.
- Water Weird left behind — would have frozen or evaporated with no water source.
- Short rest. Thors dragged the cauldron out of the cave and onto the boat.

### Act 2 — Speaker Whalen and the Sale
- Met **Speaker Dennis Whalen** at the Easthaven town hall — human, looks 50 but is in his 30s, dark circles, scraggly beard, big fur coat.
- Whalen asked to buy the cauldron. Offered 10 family gemstones @ 500 gp each = **5,000 gp** total; one now, nine in two days.
- Avarath: "We need some insurance first." Thors's Insight confirmed Whalen's intention was genuinely to feed the town.
- Thors (former caravan guard) insisted on a contract. Whalen wrote it up on town-insignia paper and called **Prudence** (mousy assistant) in to witness.
- Avarath read it over: "copacetic." All signed.
- Captain Arlath delivered Dzaan's wizard bag: spell components, **scroll of Fireball**, **gray Bag of Tricks** (1/day, d8 creature summoned for a day). Rune took it.
- 600 gp split evenly: 120 each.

### Act 3 — Evening in Easthaven
- Virel browsed the magic shop. D100'd three 1st-level scrolls; took **Alarm** for 100 gp (only valid option — no Silvery Barbs allowed). Asked about Drizzt; shopkeeper sells his books but never met him.
- Kane and Rune went ice fishing. Survival success — 3 large trout. Extended RP:
  - Kane revealed he's from the **Order of the Fang**, sent to Icewind Dale alone by druid chief Arendelle to investigate the cult.
  - Rune shared he's been mostly alone for years; visibly envious of Kane's lasting connection to Selûne.
  - Kane: "You're never alone when you have religion, Rune."
- Avarath walked the docks. Connected "ferry" from the séance clue to **Easthaven Ferry** — a frozen boat at the docks right in front of the inn. Failed Perception, failed Investigation. Returned empty-handed.
- Back at the inn, Kane helped Bartaban scale the fish and donated them for dinner. Dropped vague hints to Bartaban about "new food incoming" — Bartaban politely stopped asking.
- Virel asked if Easthaven had a surgeon or plague doctor; told to ask Arlath in the morning about the local coroner.

### Act 4 — The Alignment Confrontation
- Rune brought up the 5,000 gp deal — "deeply uncomfortable taking 5,000 in family gemstones off the speaker." Tried trading the Fireball scroll to Virel to drop her share. She refused.
- Rune Insight on Virel: 13. Kane Insight with advantage: 15. Both concluded Virel has **changed**.
- DM confirmed: Virel failed a WIS save studying the evidence after leaving The Caer. Her alignment has shifted. The **soothsayer's necklace** is still on her.
- Rune cast Detect Magic on Virel. She noticed with Perception 19. Confirmed the necklace's evocation aura.
- Party mostly joked through it (Lord of the Rings: "take the ring from her and throw it in Mount Doom"). Avarath was lost until Rune filled him in. Kane signaled Thors he'd be willing to grapple her on cue.
- No one acted. Everyone turned in.

### Act 5 — Long Rest
- Virel chose to learn **Alarm** from her scroll rather than study evidence (latter would have been disadvantage given her alignment shift).
- Full long rest completed.

### Act 6 — The Ferry (Morning)
- Rune asked Avarath to show everyone the ferry in daylight.
- Virel Investigation with help-advantage from the party: found nothing from the dock.
- Party boarded. Kane wild-shaped into a **Giant Spider**. Climbed the mast 20 ft for overwatch.
- Virel Detect Magic (30 ft radius). Thors held position over the hatch.
- Rune opened the cabin door — inside were bedrolls, a large **map of Icewind Dale**, and markings including **Sun Blight Fortress** in the mountains.
- As Rune reached for the map, Brute's blindsight detected something invisible right beside him. Combat.

### Act 7 — The Torture
- Prisoner revealed to be **Durth** — the white-bearded duergar with three braids, Xardorok's son, the one they'd been hunting.
- Virel took charge. Heated a corkscrew in a **Create Bonfire** and drove it into his eye. Kane cast **Healing Word** mid-torture to keep him alive for more.
- Avarath tried persuasion twice ("Haven't you suffered enough?" / "If your dad would kill you, does he really love you?"). Both failed.
- Rune stood by the door, bow nearby, refusing to participate and openly challenging the rest of the party.
- Thors turned away from the scene — "I wouldn't say okay with it, but it doesn't seem right."
- Durth eventually admitted what they already knew: "You know where the fortress is. That's where dad is. That's where the dragon is. That's where the heart is."
- Rune fired an auto-crit longbow into Durth's skull — DM's ruling because Durth was bound and wanted to be killed. Transcript math: ~22 damage. Enough to drop him past Healing Word's reach. Unrevivable without Resurrection.
- Session ended with another arrow already nocked — pointed at no one yet.

## Combat Encounters

### Encounter — The Invisible Duergar (2 rounds)

**Initiative:** Rune 23 → Thors 17 → Virel 17 → Kane 14 → Avarath 5 → Duergar nat 1.

**Setup:** Kane (already in Giant Spider form) pre-webs the floor in front of the cabin door. Rune approaches, opens the door, spots the Sun Blight Fortress map. As he reaches for it, Brute's blindsight pings something invisible right next to him. "Don't you dare." — the duergar swings with disadvantage, rolls 5/4, misses. He turns visible: bald, white beard with a center braid.

**Round 1:**
- **Rune.** Action: pulls the map off the wall. Move: flees the cabin. Duergar's OA attack roll 15 — misses. Announces "we have company."
- **Thors.** Holds action at the hatch.
- **Virel.** Moves 30 ft for line of sight. Holds action — "cast a Fireball when I see him" (joked; she doesn't have Fireball at level 2 — held attack resolves as Fire Bolt cantrip).
- **Kane (Giant Spider).** Climbs 20 ft down the mast, moves another 20 ft toward the door. Holds action to bite any hostile in melee.
- **Avarath.** Moves toward the commotion. Perception — no sounds below deck. Holds position.
- **Duergar.** Bonus: **Reduce** (Tiny). Action: Dash. Tries to flee through Kane's web — DC 13 Dex save with +8 (Tiny bonus), rolls 10, total 18, **passes**. Triggers the held attacks:
  - **Virel's Fire Bolt** (disadvantage on Tiny). Nat 20 (crit negated by disadvantage). **10 fire damage** (max d10).
  - **Kane's Giant Spider bite** (disadvantage). Hits on 15. **8 piercing + 7 poison = 15 damage**.
  - Duergar takes 25 total, still alive, finishes movement.
- **Avarath.** Opportunity attack with disadvantage on the Tiny fleeing dwarf — Maul rolls 11, miss.

**Round 2:**
- **Rune.** Action: Longbow at Tiny target (disadvantage) — 25/24, hits on 24. **7 piercing.** Bonus action: **Ensnaring Strike**. Duergar Strength save with disadvantage (Tiny), DC 13 — **nat 1**. Restrained. Additional **3 piercing**. Turn total: **10 damage**.
- **Avarath.** "I don't think we should kill him. Keep him alive." No attack.
- **Thors.** Sling shot, non-lethal. Restrained (advantage) + Tiny (disadvantage) cancel to normal roll per DM. Rolls 15, hits. **5 bludgeoning.** Duergar drops from 4 HP to 0 — unconscious, alive.

**Resolution:** Combat ends. ~50 seconds later the dwarf returns to normal size, still unconscious. Kane wraps him in webs. Party hauls him to the White Lady Inn. Bartaban sees a giant spider carrying a webbed dwarf and politely says, "I'll just be upstairs then."

**Spell slots used:** Rune 1x 1st (Ensnaring Strike). No killing blow — deliberate non-lethal capture.

### Encounter — The Wine Cellar Interrogation

Not a combat encounter in the mechanical sense, but the session's most significant engagement.

- Durth wakes at 1 HP. Virel casts **Create Bonfire** (cantrip) and heats a metal corkscrew in it.
- Virel rolls Intimidation 17. Durth refuses: "You really think I'm going to give you information about my own father?" — party realizes this is Xardorok's son.
- Virel drives the corkscrew into Durth's eye. **Blinded** condition applied. Follow-up Intimidations fail (8, 11).
- Kane un-wild-shapes and casts **Healing Word** — heals 11 HP. Durth now at 12 HP.
- Virel twists the corkscrew: **5 piercing** (DM called it a dagger proxy). Durth down to 7 HP.
- Avarath Persuasion 10 — fails. "Haven't you suffered enough?"
- Durth's thesis line: "I don't want his love, I want his respect. And today I've lost it."
- Rune Persuasion 4 — fails. Durth: "You have the map. Look at it yourself."
- Virel twists again: **5 more piercing**. Durth at **2 HP**.
- Kane casts **Healing Word** second time — heals 10 HP. Durth now at 12 HP.
- Virel Intimidation 17 (with advantage) — breaks him. "I'll tell you one thing."
- Durth's terms: "You promised to put an arrow in my head as soon as I say it." Rune nods.
- Information: "You know where the fortress is. That's where dad is. That's where the dragon is." Thors: "And that's where the heart is."
- **Auto-crit** ruling from DM: Durth is tied up AND wants to be killed. Rune fires longbow. Transcript math: **13 + 9 ≈ 22 damage**. Enough to clear Durth's revivable-damage threshold. Kills him beyond what Healing Word can recover. Cannot be revived without Resurrection.

**Spell slots used:** Kane 2x 1st (Healing Word). Virel cantrip (Create Bonfire). Rune no slot on the killshot.
