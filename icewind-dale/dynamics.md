## NPC Relationships

- **Hlin Trollbane** — Hired the party to investigate the murders. Trusts them with the job. 100 gp each on completion
- **Duvessa Shane** — Cooperative; provided evidence against Sephek. Sympathetic to the investigation
- **Roark** — Friendly innkeeper at The Uphill Climb. Has been a reliable source of local information. Hopes the party's actions stabilize Caer-Dineval
- **Captain Arlath** — Professional relationship. Hired the party to find the missing *Bunch o' Knuckle* crew. Paid out after completion. Delivered Dzaan's bag as promised
- **Bartaban** — Owner of the White Lady Inn. Grateful for saving his bar; gave free rooms. Source of historical info (Drizzt, local gossip). Session 7: saw Kane (as a giant spider) drag a webbed dwarf into his bar and politely went upstairs without asking questions
- **Speaker Dennis Whalen** — Town Speaker of Easthaven. Bought the Cauldron of Plenty for 5,000 gp in gemstones (1 down, 9 in 2 days). Signed contract. Wants to feed the town. Wants the sale kept quiet. Transactional but genuine
- **Prudence** — Whalen's mousy young assistant. Witnessed the cauldron contract. Low-stakes but observed the party's leverage over Whalen
- **Avarice** — Transactional. Arcane Brotherhood wizard offering info in exchange for a future (undefined) favor. Contact via raven. Currently "stuck in the basement" of The Caer
- **Garrett** — Grateful for rescue. Denied knowledge of Torg's Caravan

### Adversaries & Unknowns
- **Sephek Caltro** — Murder suspect. Party has evidence but hasn't confronted him yet

- **Kadroth** — Cult leader in The Caer. Dismissed the party; unclear if hostile or indifferent

## Plot Threads

### Active Quests
1. **Murder Investigation** — Hired by Hlin Trollbane. Three murders linked to Sephek Caltro of Torg's Caravan. Evidence gathered but suspect not yet confronted. Reward: 100 gp each
2. **Destroy the Heart / Sun Blight Fortress** — The soothsayer's dying prophecy. Xardorok has "the heart of a dragon" and plans to destroy Ten-Towns. Durth confirmed the heart and dragon are at **Sun Blight Fortress** in the mountains. The heart must be destroyed. This is the module's endgame destination
3. **Virel's Alignment / The Necklace** — The soothsayer's evocation-magic necklace has shifted Virel's alignment to evil. Party confirmed this in Session 7 but did not act. Rune + Avarath openly uncomfortable; Kane signaled to Thors he'd grapple her on cue. Unresolved — likely comes to a head next session
4. **5,000 gp Owed** — Speaker Whalen owes 9 more gemstones (4,500 gp) in 2 days as payment for the Cauldron of Plenty. Signed contract exists. Good-faith payment or setup?
5. **Dispose of Durth's Body** — Rune killed Durth in the White Lady Inn's wine cellar. Corkscrew still in his eye. Cannot stay hidden. Bartaban is upstairs
