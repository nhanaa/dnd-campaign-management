## Party Composition Analysis

**Frontline:** Avarath (Paladin), Thors (Fighter) — two heavy melee characters
**Midline:** Rune (Ranger), Kane (Druid) — flexible damage and utility
**Backline:** Virel (Wizard), Rathis (Rogue) — damage and skill coverage

### Role Coverage

- **Tank/Defender:** Avarath (AC 17, Lay On Hands), Thors (Fighter)
- **Healing:** Avarath (Lay On Hands, Cure Wounds), Kane (Druid spells)
- **Damage:** Thors, Avarath (Smite), Rathis (Sneak Attack), Rune
- **Utility/Control:** Virel (Wizard), Kane (Druid), Rune (Ranger)
- **Skills/Scouting:** Rathis (Rogue), Rune (Ranger)

### Synergies to Explore

- Avarath's **Bless** benefits the whole party — particularly Thors and Rathis for attack rolls
- **Topple (Maul)** knocking enemies prone gives advantage to Rathis for Sneak Attack and Thors for melee
- Avarath's **Fey Step (Winter)** can frighten a target before repositioning — good setup for the party
