# World — Rime of the Frostmaiden

## System
- **System:** D&D 5.5e (2024 PHB)
- **Module:** Rime of the Frostmaiden
- **DM:** HeyDrewHayes
- **Start Date:** April 2026

## Key Locations

### Ten-Towns

- **Icewind Dale** — Frozen tundra region gripped by an unnatural, endless winter
- **Ten-Towns** — Collection of settlements in Icewind Dale; each town appeases Auril differently (light, food, or human sacrifice by lottery)

#### Targos
- **Luskan Arms Inn** — Sturdy walls, rotten floorboards, rat problem. Party's starting location
- Starting point of the murder investigation; the shipbuilder (3rd victim) was killed here

#### Bryn Shander
- Largest and most intact of the towns visited; protected by a large circular wall
- **Speaker:** Duvessa Shane — provided evidence against Sephek Caltro
- Torg's Caravan left town in the middle of the night (suspicious)

#### Caer-Dineval
- Named for **The Caer**, one of the last true castles in Icewind Dale
- **The Uphill Climb** — Inn run by Roark; party's base of operations in the area
- **The Caer** — Fortress now occupied by a cult of Levistus. Banner: two black crossed swords (replacing the normal white tower over red fish). Town Speaker Cranic has not been seen since the cult arrived; communicates only through signed letters
- **Dinev's Rest** — Abandoned building with a crooked dragon weathervane. Was a duergar staging point; destroyed by fire after the party cleared it
- Athelas Daenan chose to remain here to watch the cult (departed party Session 4)

#### Caer-Konig
- Town with a crumpled, ruined castle
- **Frozenfar Expeditions** — Guide outfit run by Atenas Swift; provided info on Garrett's mountain route

#### Easthaven
- Site of the public execution of the wizard Dzaan (burned at the stake for murdering adventurers)
- Sacrifices people by lottery (often rigged against vagrants)
- Durth (duergar, white beard in three braids) — one of Xardorok's sons — was last reported here
- **The Rusty Anchor** — Shop where party sold gear
- **Ferryan Feress** — Another shop visited
- **White Lady Inn** — Former mansion converted to an inn. Owner: Bartaban (elderly man). Drizzt once stayed here, wrote in a book, asked about the White Lady legend. Rinaldo the halfling ran the séance here
- **Easthaven Ferry** — Large boat frozen into the ice at the docks near the White Lady Inn. Matches the séance clue "Ferry." Not abandoned — used as a covert base by invisible duergar. Party boarded in Session 7; cabin contained bedrolls (more than one occupant), a map of Icewind Dale marking Sun Blight Fortress and a duergar outpost near Kelvin's Cairn. Durth (Xardorok's son) was found invisible inside and captured. Other duergar likely return to this base.

### Wilderness & Points of Interest

- **Keegan's Hut** — Between Targos and Bryn Shander. Home of Keegan, who directed the party to find Garrett on the mountain
- **Mountain near Caer-Konig** — Yeti territory. Contains a cave system with a massive chasm (~80 ft. deep) crossed by a stone bridge. Site of the yeti encounter and rescue of Garrett's climbing party
- **Upside-Down Tower** — Rumored ancient magical tower that fell from the sky and buried itself underground. Connected to the fallen wizard city. Dzaan was allegedly searching for it before his execution
- **Coastal Caves (northeast of Easthaven)** — Four cave mouths in 80-foot cliffs along the coast. The Bunch o' Knuckle crew's rowboat was found here. Contains:
  - Water-level cave with a frozen river path and a Water Weird guarding a frozen waterfall (likely frozen/destroyed once the water froze over)
  - Former hot spring with four frozen frost giants (drowning death ritual), open to the sky with Auril's Rime visible above
  - A sea hag's lair with a cauldron and a frost giant skeleton servant (both destroyed by the party)
  - A pile-of-bones room where the Bunch o' Knuckle crew was stripped of meat and eaten; contained a waterlogged chest with 600 gp
  - Three higher caves (20-30 ft up) connected by an ice bridge — unexplored
- **Sun Blight Fortress** — Duergar fortress in the mountains around Icewind Dale. Xardorok's base. Contains the dragon and "the heart." Location revealed on a map recovered from the Easthaven Ferry (Session 7). Durth described it as "where dad is, where the dragon is, where the heart is." This is the module's endgame destination.
- **Duergar outpost (near Kelvin's Cairn)** — Marked on the same map; unvisited. Likely a staging point for the duergar invasion.

## Lore

### Auril's Endless Winter
- An unnatural, endless winter grips Icewind Dale
- Each town appeases Auril differently: some sacrifice light, some food, some people
- Human sacrifices occur by lottery on the new moon; lotteries are often rigged to fall on vagrants

### Levistus — The Chained One
- Arch-Devil of the 5th level of the Hells, also known as The Frozen Prince
- Worshipped by the cult occupying The Caer in Caer-Dineval
- The soothsayer received visions from Levistus about Xardorok's plot

### The Fallen Wizard City
- An ancient magical city once held aloft by wizards above Icewind Dale, long before Ten-Towns existed
- The city eventually fell; its remnants are buried and scattered across the dale
- One specific rumor: a magical tower fell from the sky upside down and burrowed into the earth underground
- Dzaan was searching for this tower before his execution

### Auril's Domain
- Auril lives far north across the Sea of Moving Ice in some kind of fortress or temple
- The endless winter started two years ago — one day the sun simply didn't rise
- An aurora-like barrier (Auril's Rime) covers the sky, blocking sunlight
- A Tempest Cleric might know more about how to reach or stop her
- The **Island of Solstice** is connected to Auril somehow — significance unknown

### The Cauldron of Plenty
- Rare wondrous item recovered from the sea hag's lair in the coastal caves
- Pour water and stir for 1 minute → transforms into hearty hot stew
- Feeds 4 people per gallon, 3 times per day; holds 30 gallons
- Enough food for ~120 people daily
- Now in Speaker Whalen's custody in Easthaven, guarded day and night. Whoever feeds Easthaven controls Easthaven

### Frost Giant Death Rituals
- Elderly frost giants perform drowning rituals rather than die of old age or sickness
- They seek deep water in hot springs or caves to end their lives
- Four such giants were found frozen beneath a former hot spring in the coastal caves

### Drizzt Do'Urden in Icewind Dale
- Drizzt visited the White Lady Inn years ago
- He stayed for a while, writing extensively in a book and asking about the White Lady legend
- Disappeared one night; Bartaban hasn't seen him since

### The Duergar Threat
- Xardorok leads duergar forces from the Underdark into Icewind Dale
- Goal: destroy the surface towns so duergar can live above ground
- Xardorok possesses "the heart of a dragon" — central to the plan
- Duergar can turn invisible; a staging group of five was found in Dinev's Rest awaiting orders to attack
- The soothsayer's dying words: "Destroy the heart"

### The Three Murders — RESOLVED (Session 8)
- Three murders in one month across Ten-Towns, all with the same MO: piercing blade, wounds frozen over, no weapon recovered
- Victims: a halfling trapper (Easthaven), a dwarven glassblower (Bryn Shander), a shipbuilder (Targos — ice dagger in heart). Plus Kane Whitefang (Session 8)
- Killer: **Sephek Caltro** — undead vessel of Auril. Claims her "blessing." Kills people who paid off town officials to avoid the sacrifice lottery
- Motive: victims cheated the Auril sacrifice system by bribing officials to be excluded from the lottery. Sephek killed them as alternative sacrifices to the Frostmaiden
- Sephek captured alive, turned in to Easthaven guards. Bounty collection from Hlin Trollbane in Targos pending

### Errtu
- Balor demon that terrorized Icewind Dale over a century ago
- A ship figurehead depicting Errtu was found in the Easthaven town hall, made of the same dark evocation stone as the soothsayer's necklace
- The White Lady ghost (from the séance) was bound to this figurehead
- Connection between Errtu, the evocation stone, and the cult of Levistus is unknown

## Story

### Session 1 — The Job and the Road
Party met at the Luskan Arms Inn in Targos. Hlin Trollbane hired them to investigate three connected murders and the suspect Sephek Caltro. On the road, Boy the dog led them to Keegan, who asked them to find the missing guide Garrett on the mountain near Caer-Konig. In Bryn Shander, Duvessa Shane provided evidence against Sephek. Torg's Caravan had left town suspiciously in the night. Session ended with three crag cats encircling the party on the road to Caer-Dineval.

### Session 2 — The Mountain Rescue Begins
Defeated the crag cats. Arrived in Caer-Dineval — noticed the wrong banner flying over The Caer. Stayed at The Uphill Climb. Virel studied the murder notes (consistent piercing weapon, frozen wounds). Traveled to Caer-Konig, met Atenas Swift, and climbed the mountain. Found Garrett's abandoned camp with five starving sled dogs. Survived an avalanche. Found Garrett's bloodied body and killed a crag cat attacking him. Garrett described a yeti attack and three missing climbers. Party camped in a cave with a stone bridge over an 80-foot chasm.

### Session 3 — Yeti Cave and The Caer
Found the remains of Mukingo (goliath — dead), rescued the halfling and tiefling (Asterisks). Killed a mother and male yeti. Descended the mountain and returned to Caer-Konig, then Caer-Dineval. Virel's research found no motive in the murders; Torg, Sephek, and Vex were all named. Roark warned the party about the cult in The Caer. Party entered the fortress, met Thoob, and were taken to the soothsayer — an elderly dwarven woman who prophesied the party would "save them," warned about Xardorok, and said "Destroy the heart" before dying. She left behind a magical necklace (evocation, causes dread). Kadroth, a tiefling cult leader, questioned the party about Levistus and dismissed them.

### Session 4 — Avarath Joins, Duergar Discovered
After leaving The Caer, the party met Avarath Solvane, who had been trying to intimidate his way into the castle. Athelas chose to stay behind in Caer-Dineval to watch the cult. Roark mentioned Xardorok and Dinev's Rest. The party investigated Dinev's Rest — an abandoned building with signs of covert use. Inside, they fought five duergar (invisible) and interrogated a survivor. The duergar revealed: Xardorok ordered them to wait for "the attack," the duergar came from the Underdark to destroy Ten-Towns, Xardorok has "the heart of a dragon," and one of his sons (Durth, white beard in three braids) is in Easthaven. Dinev's Rest burned down afterward.

### Session 5 — Easthaven, Dzaan, and the Séance
Roark noted black smoke and large winged creatures leaving The Caer. Party traveled to Easthaven and witnessed Dzaan's public execution. Rune recalled the legend of the fallen wizard city and the upside-down tower. The gargoyle Gurgle delivered a message from Avarice (Arcane Brotherhood) — she offered an exchange of information for a future favor and warned about Vellynne. Captain Arlath hired the party to find the missing crew of the *Bunch o' Knuckle*. That night, a halfling invited the party to a séance about the White Lady (a woman who died on the lake, treasure sank, husband suspected of murder). The séance produced frost-written messages: "Cauldron," "Caves," "invisible dwarves," "ferry," and "something else here." A specter attacked and was destroyed. Session ended with the terrified halfling on the floor.

### Session 6 — The Hag's Cauldron
Evening at the White Lady Inn: Avarath learned about Auril's winter from a guard (lives across the Sea of Moving Ice, find a Tempest Cleric). Rune pressed the same guard about the trapper murder — nat 20 Insight revealed the guard is hiding something about Torg's Caravan; the guard threatened Rune via Message. Rune recalled the "Island of Solstice" (nat 20 Religion). Virel studied the murder evidence and suffered an unknown magical effect (failed CHA save). Virel asked Bartaban about Drizzt — he stayed at the inn years ago, wrote in a book about the White Lady, then vanished. Next morning, the party took a longboat northeast to search for the Bunch o' Knuckle crew. Found their rowboat near coastal caves with four cave mouths in 80-foot cliffs. Inside: fought a Water Weird at a frozen waterfall (Kane's leg grabbed through the ice), discovered four frozen frost giants in a former hot spring (drowning death ritual), and encountered a sea hag cooking stew in a cauldron with a blood trail behind her. When Thors refused her hospitality, she transformed and summoned a frost giant skeleton. Avarath killed both — Divine Smite on the hag, then a critical hit with his maul (19 bludgeoning doubled to 38 via vulnerability + 19 radiant Divine Smite = 57 damage) shattered the CR 6 skeleton. The party was not expected to survive. Session ended with the cave system not fully explored.

### Session 7 — The Cauldron, The Ferry, and The Son
Party finished looting the coastal caves: found a waterlogged chest with 600 gp, Dzaan's missing wizard bag (scroll of Fireball, gray Bag of Tricks, spell components), and confirmed the *Bunch o' Knuckle* crew were stripped of meat by the sea hag. Virel identified the sea hag's cauldron as the magical **Cauldron of Plenty**. Dragged it back to Easthaven. Speaker **Dennis Whalen** desperately offered 5,000 gp in family gemstones to buy it — signed contract witnessed by Prudence. Received first gemstone; nine more promised in 2 days.

That evening, Virel revealed she wants to be a plague doctor / forensic specialist. Rune and Kane went ice fishing; Kane revealed his **Order of the Fang** affiliation (sent alone by druid chief Arendelle to investigate the cult). Rune cast Detect Magic on Virel — confirmed the soothsayer's necklace is still altering her. Kane's Insight 15 confirmed her alignment has shifted. The party joked through it with Lord of the Rings references; no one acted.

Avarath walked the docks alone and connected the séance's "fairy" clue to the frozen **Easthaven Ferry**. In the morning, the full party boarded the ferry (Kane as a Giant Spider for overwatch). They found a map of Icewind Dale marking **Sun Blight Fortress** (Xardorok's base) and an invisible duergar. The duergar used Reduce to try to flee; Virel's Fire Bolt (10) + Kane's Giant Spider bite (15) + Rune's Ensnaring Strike restraint brought him down. Thors knocked him unconscious non-lethally with a sling.

Back at the White Lady Inn, the party hauled the prisoner to the wine cellar. Virel led the interrogation, heating a corkscrew in Create Bonfire and driving it into the prisoner's eye. Kane Healing Word'd the prisoner twice to keep him alive for more torture. Avarath pleaded with him; Rune stood by the cellar door refusing to participate, bow ready. The prisoner was **Durth, Xardorok's son**. He revealed Sun Blight Fortress is where Xardorok is, where the dragon is, and where the heart is — then demanded Rune execute him. Auto-crit longbow to the skull. Durth dead, unrevivable. Session ended with Rune nocking a second arrow — pointed at no one, yet.

### Session 8 — Ghosts, Thieves, and the Frost Maiden's Blade
Party confronted Virel about the necklace — Avarath identified it as a curse (nat 20 Medicine) requiring Greater Restoration (5th level). At the town hall, found the **White Lady ghost** bound to a ship figurehead depicting **Errtu** (Balor demon), made of the same dark evocation stone as the soothsayer's necklace. Avarath attacked first; ghost resisted everything except Divine Smite. Kane summoned Reginald the Octopus as a distraction; the ghost possessed Reginald and tried to flee; Avarath killed the octopus (opportunity attack) and Rune killed the ghost (opportunity attack longbow). DM confirmed the encounter was completely unrelated to any active quest.

Below the town hall, found Speaker Whalen and guards locked in cells — **Prudence** had betrayed them, calling in accomplices who stole the **Cauldron of Plenty** and fled toward Targos. Whalen can't pay the remaining 9 gemstones without the cauldron (contract clause requires it present in 48 hours). A guard overheard the thieves mention Targos.

That night: Virel researched murder evidence and discovered victims were people who paid off officials to avoid the sacrifice lottery. Rune stole the cursed necklace from Virel's bag while she tranced (passed CHA save DC 10). **Sephek Caltro** attacked Kane in his sleep with an ice sword — 11 piercing (Relentless Endurance saved him to 1 HP) then 5 more (unconscious). Kane Wild Shaped into Dinosuchus (raptor call woke the party). Sephek Misty Stepped past Thors and killed Kane with a max-damage roll (d10=10, +3=13) — permanently dead, past negative max HP. Thors interposed with Tavern Brawler + Heroic Inspiration. Avarath Fey Stepped in and Toppled Sephek prone. Rune climbed out a third-floor window (Acrobatics 18). Virel cast Sleep (Sephek failed WIS save). Three rounds plus surprise.

Sephek interrogated: undead (necromancy), claims Auril's "blessing," kills lottery-dodgers, ice sword is separate. Virel's Detect Magic also revealed the necklace on Rune's belt — "You thief! You half-baked potato!" Justice debate: gagged, hooded, turned in to Easthaven guards with caster's muzzle. Thors carried Kane's Dinosuchus body to the town hall. Kane's player rolling a Warlock.
