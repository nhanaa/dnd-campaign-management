## Aesgor's Role in the Party
- **Primary Healer/Support** — Healing Word, Cure Wounds, Bless
- **Secondary Tank** — AC 18, medium armor + shield, decent HP
- **Explorer/Navigator** — Eye of the Navigator, Survival/Perception advantage in wilderness, swim speed
- **Scout (with Aware)** — Once/long rest burst of Perception/Investigation power

## Key NPCs

- **Hrolf** — Veteran Hallharn warrior. Same tribe as Aesgor (and Marzena). Damaged internally — no proficiency bonus on attacks, gains exhaustion when pushing himself. Authority has eroded as the raiding party has been whittled down; after "double nowhere" he effectively defers to the group. Prayed to Hel personally after the zombie attack.
- **Siddhe** — Displaced child from another world. Traveling with the party. Aesgor has promised to find her way home. Innately able to stabilize downed allies; revived Glug in the beach fight by literally jumping on her chest and bit-slapping her awake.

## Faction Relations

### Raider Clans
- **Hallharn** — Aesgor's clan. Known for Mother's Brew mead (sweet fruit honey mead).
- Other clans TBD as encountered.

### The Baendur
- Forest/hill-dwelling peoples. Historically subjugated by the Witchking, weakened after raiders overthrew him.

### Ironwood Witches
- Agents of the Witchking. Seeking to reassemble the Rainbow Spear.

### Unknown Enemy Clan
- Attacked the Hallharn on the beach outside Kekel (session 2).
- Clan shield carries a stylized stalking predator — *tiger-shaped*, but not definitely a tiger. New, unrecognized faction.

### The Stone Court (Gods)
- Petrified Vanir, now divine. Aesgor draws power from the entire court.
- **Aldyhn** — Leader of the Vanir, Aesgor carries a yarn effigy to Aldyhn.

## Plot Threads

- **Who raises the dead?** Zombies are not supposed to exist in Grimnir. The zombie raider at "double nowhere" was missing all its fingernails — tortured before death. Hrolf prayed directly to Hel. Something or someone is deliberately reanimating bodies.
- **Ragnarok's harvest.** Valkyries are actively collecting the slain from pyres to reinforce the armies of the final battle. Aesgor is the only one with clear sight of them; Gaynor understood the implication instantly. Ragnarok may be closer than the Hallharn admit.
- **Tree-Hammer's gray feather.** A silver-sea afterlife vision and an unprompted word — *luau*. Tree-Hammer has an unexplained link to an afterlife-adjacent realm. Pair with Aesgor's white raven feather from Kekel.
- **The iron bust (lips sewn shut).** Carried from Kekel. Still unexplained.
- **Siddhe's home.** Endless green farmland — not Grimnir. Aesgor has sworn to return her. Hard story anchor for Aesgor.
- **Finding the real "Nowhere."** Still somewhere east. Hrolf is no longer confident in his directions.
- **The unknown clan's intent.** Who sent them? Why hit this shore? Possibly linked to the Ironwood Witches / Witchking, or a new third faction entirely.
