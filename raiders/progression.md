# Aesgor — Progression Plan

## Build Identity: Frontline Battle Cleric

Wade into melee with Spirit Guardians and Spiritual Weapon doing the heavy lifting. High AC, CON save proficiency from Fighter dip, and spell-focused damage. Weapon attacks (rapier when found) are the fallback when slots are spent.

## Typical Combat Turn (Level 5+)

1. **Pre-combat or Round 1:** Cast Spirit Guardians (action, concentration)
2. **Each subsequent turn:**
   - Spirit Guardians deals 3d8 to nearby enemies (automatic, no action)
   - **Action:** Toll the Dead (1d12 vs damaged targets) or Dodge or melee attack
   - **Bonus Action:** Spiritual Weapon attack (1d8+4) OR Healing Word if someone's down
3. **Emergency:** Steadfast (BA, spend Hit Die + CON to self-heal, 1/rest)

## Key Decision Points

### Level 4: Observant (Locked In)
- WIS 17 → 18
- Passive Perception 19 (24 with Aware active)
- Spell Save DC 14, Spell Attack +6
- Lip reading

### Level 6: Fighter 1 Dip (Locked In)
- CON save proficiency — concentration checks go from +2 to +5 (scales with proficiency)
- Defense fighting style — AC 19
- Second Wind — bonus action 1d10+1 heal, no spell slot
- Delays Cleric 6 (Wrathful Flood) by 1 character level

### Level 9 (Cleric 8): ASI/Feat — TBD
Options to consider when we get there:
- **Resilient (CON)** — CON 14→15, stacks with Fighter proficiency (already have it). Skip this.
- **+2 WIS** (18→20) — max WIS, DC 15, +7 spell attack. The safe pick.
- **War Caster** — advantage on concentration (stacks with CON prof), OA cantrips. Still strong even with CON proficiency.
- **Sentinel** — punish enemies trying to leave your Spirit Guardians. Thematic for a frontliner.
- **Lucky** — universally good. 3 rerolls/day.

## Weapon Plan
- **Current:** Warhammer (+2 attack, 1d8/1d10 bludgeoning, STR-based)
- **Goal:** Rapier when found (+4 attack, 1d8+2 piercing, DEX-based)
- Weapon is the fallback option — spells (Toll the Dead, Spiritual Weapon) are primary damage
- With Divine Strike at Cleric 8: rapier swing becomes 1d8+2 piercing + 1d8 (divine) once/turn

## Spell Priorities by Level

### Level 1 (2 slots)
- **Always prepare:** Healing Word, Bless
- **Flex:** Cure Wounds (touch heal), Shield of Faith (+2 AC)
- **Domain (free):** Expeditious Retreat, Fog Cloud

### Level 3 (3 slots, 2nd-level)
- **Must-have:** Spiritual Weapon
- **Strong:** Aid (bonus HP for 3 people, 8 hours), Lesser Restoration
- **Domain (free):** Augury, Pass Without Trace

### Level 5 (4/3 slots, 3rd-level)
- **Must-have:** Spirit Guardians
- **Strong:** Revivify (need 300 gp diamond — ask DM early), Dispel Magic, Beacon of Hope
- **Domain (free):** Haste (buff the fighter/barbarian), Clairvoyance
