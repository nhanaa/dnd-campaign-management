# Aesgor — Progression Plan

## Build Identity: Frontline Battle Cleric (Pure Cleric 16)

Wade into melee with Spirit Guardians + Spiritual Weapon doing the heavy lifting, then transition into tier-3 caster dominance with Holy Aura, Blade Barrier, and Divine Word at high levels. No multiclass — every character level is a Cleric level, so caster progression never slips.

## Typical Combat Turn

### Level 5+ (Spirit Guardians online)
1. **Pre-combat or Round 1:** Cast Spirit Guardians (action, concentration)
2. **Each subsequent turn:**
   - Spirit Guardians deals 3d8 to nearby enemies (automatic, no action)
   - **Action:** Toll the Dead (1d12 vs damaged targets) or melee attack
   - **Bonus Action:** Spiritual Weapon attack (1d8+4) OR Healing Word if someone's down
   - **Reaction (post-L8):** War Caster OA — Toll the Dead at anyone leaving the aura
3. **Emergency:** Steadfast (BA, spend Hit Die + CON to self-heal, 1/rest)

### Level 15+ (Holy Aura online)
1. **Turn 1:** Holy Aura (action, concentration, 30 ft aura)
2. **Turn 2+:**
   - Allies in aura have advantage on saves AND enemies attack them at disadvantage
   - **Action:** Toll the Dead (3d12 vs damaged) or Sunburst for AoE clears
   - **BA:** Spiritual Weapon (1d8+5) — still running alongside Holy Aura
3. **Burn 5th+ slots:** Flame Strike, Blade Barrier, Fire Storm for AoE. Mass Cure Wounds or Heal for emergency healing.

## Key Decision Points

### Level 4: Observant (Locked In)
- WIS 17 → 18
- Passive Perception 19 (24 with Aware active)
- Spell Save DC 14, Spell Attack +6
- Lip reading
- Locked in — too much value on a scout/vigilant character with Aware

### Level 8: War Caster (Locked In)
- **Advantage on concentration saves** — better than CON proficiency alone (at DC 15, ~82% vs ~45% without)
- Cast spells with somatic components while holding warhammer + shield (no sheathing needed)
- **Opportunity Attacks become cantrips** — Toll the Dead (d12+) or Sacred Flame as a reaction when an enemy leaves Spirit Guardians or tries to flee
- Replaces what the old Fighter 1 dip was protecting — without sacrificing a Cleric level

### Level 12: Resilient (CON) (Locked In)
- CON 14 → 15 (no mod bump, but fills the odd score)
- **CON save proficiency** — stacks with War Caster for near-unbreakable concentration
- Combined concentration save at DC 15: advantage + prof = ~91% retention
- This is what finally puts the "tank my concentration spell" question to bed for the rest of the campaign

### Level 16: ASI/Feat — final pick (TBD)
Ranked options:

- **+2 WIS (18→20)** — DC 17, spell attack +9, +1 prepared spell, +1 to WIS saves and all the skill checks. Safe, universally strong. **Default pick.**
- **Lucky** — 3 rerolls per long rest. Universally strong, can save a failed concentration save the one time War Caster + Resilient rolls snake eyes. Strong competitor to +2 WIS.
- **Sentinel** — enemies stopped on OA, OA on disengage, OA when ally in 5 ft is hit. Thematic for a frontliner with Spirit Guardians, lets you lock down targets. Weaker than WIS bump statistically but highly flavorful.
- **Fey Touched** (+1 WIS 18→19, Misty Step 1/LR free, bonus-prep Bless) — mobility feat; at L16 you'd rather have WIS 20 than 19.

## Weapon Plan
- **Current:** Warhammer (+2 attack, 1d8/1d10 bludgeoning, STR-based)
- **Goal:** Rapier when found (+4 attack, 1d8+2 piercing, DEX-based)
- Weapon is the fallback option — spells (Toll the Dead, Spiritual Weapon) are primary damage
- With Divine Strike at Cleric 8: rapier swing becomes 1d8+2 piercing + 1d8 (divine) once/turn
- At Cleric 14: Divine Strike upgrades to 2d8, making weapon swings genuinely competitive

## Spell Priorities by Level

### Level 1 (2 slots)
- **Always prepare:** Healing Word, Bless
- **Flex:** Guiding Bolt (4d6 + advantage setup), Sanctuary, Inflict Wounds
- **Domain (free):** Expeditious Retreat, Fog Cloud

### Level 3 (3 slots, 2nd-level)
- **Must-have:** Spiritual Weapon
- **Strong:** Aid (bonus HP for 3 people, 8 hours), Lesser Restoration, Spiritual Weapon
- **Domain (free):** Augury, Pass Without Trace

### Level 5 (4/3 slots, 3rd-level)
- **Must-have:** Spirit Guardians
- **Strong:** Revivify (need 300 gp diamond — ask DM early), Dispel Magic, Beacon of Hope, Mass Healing Word
- **Domain (free):** Haste (buff the fighter/barbarian), Clairvoyance

### Level 7 (4/3/3/1 slots, 4th-level)
- **Must-have:** Banishment (CHA save or gone for 1 min), Death Ward (cheat death once)
- **Strong:** Guardian of Faith (spirit warrior, 20 radiant to anyone within 10 ft), Freedom of Movement
- **Domain (free):** Control Water, Locate Creature

### Level 9 (4/3/3/2/1 slots, 5th-level)
- **Must-have:** Flame Strike (4d6 fire + 4d6 radiant AoE, half on save)
- **Strong:** Mass Cure Wounds, Hold Monster, Dawn (AoE radiant + moves each turn), Greater Restoration
- **Domain (free):** Commune (ask god 3 yes/no questions), Passwall

### Level 11 (4/3/3/3/2/1 slots, 6th-level)
- **Must-have:** **Heal** (70 HP + cure blindness/deafness/paralysis/disease), **Blade Barrier** (concentration wall, 5d10)
- **Strong:** Harm (14d6 necrotic single target), Planar Ally (negotiate with a celestial/elemental/fiend for service), Heroes' Feast (party immune to poison/fear, +2d10 max HP for 24 hours)

### Level 13 (4/3/3/3/2/1/1, 7th-level)
- **Must-have:** **Divine Word** (WIS save, <50 HP = deafened 1 min / <40 = blinded 10 min / <30 = stunned 1 hour / <20 = killed. Celestials/elementals/fey/fiends pushed back to home plane.)
- **Strong:** Fire Storm (7d10 AoE radiant), Regenerate, Conjure Celestial (solar/deva)

### Level 15 (4/3/3/3/2/1/1/1, 8th-level)
- **Must-have:** **Holy Aura** (30 ft, disadvantage on attacks vs allies, advantage on saves, 1 min concentration — this is your "close out a boss fight" button)
- **Strong:** Antimagic Field (shut down enemy casters cold), Sunburst (12d6 radiant + blindness in 60 ft radius)

## Concentration Math (L12+, post-Resilient)

With War Caster + Resilient (CON) + CON 15:
- CON save: **+3 mod + prof** (at L12: +4 prof = +7 total) **with advantage**
- DC 15 (damage 20+): **~91% retention**
- DC 20 (damage 40+): **~68% retention**

You can confidently hold Holy Aura / Blade Barrier / Spirit Guardians through boss nuke turns.

## Build Rationale: Why No Fighter Dip

Earlier plan dipped Fighter 1 at char-L6 for CON save prof, +1 AC (Defense), and Second Wind. In an L16 campaign the trade is bad:
- Delays every Cleric level by 1 → Holy Aura lands at L16 instead of L15 (one session of play lost)
- Costs an ASI slot across the full build (3 total vs pure's 4)
- Never reaches Divine Intervention until L11 instead of L10
- The defensive gain (CON prof) is fully replaced by **Resilient (CON)** at L12 — same feature, feat slot, zero Cleric progression lost
- The concentration gain is *surpassed* by **War Caster** — advantage on saves beats flat prof at DC 15+
- Second Wind (1d10+1 BA heal) is already covered by Steadfast + Healing Word
- +1 AC from Defense is marginal compared to what magic armor at tier 3 provides
