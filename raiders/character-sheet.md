## Racial Features (Tuss)

### Steadfast
As long as you have not taken lightning damage since your last turn, if reduced to 0 HP or as a **bonus action**, spend one Hit Die to regain HP (roll + CON mod). Recharges on short or long rest.

### True Nature
**Action** to manifest. Physical appearance changes (define details). **Action** to hide again. While active:
- **Darkvision** 60 ft
- **Powerful:** +1 damage on weapon attacks (half proficiency bonus rounded up)
- **Swift:** +5 ft walking speed
- **Unsettling:** Disadvantage on Persuasion, advantage on Intimidation

### Afflicted
After each long rest, roll d20. On 1-2: take 5 (2d4) psychic damage (ignores resistances/immunities, can't reduce below 1 HP). Max damage = short-term madness.

### Aware
Once per long rest, activate for 1 hour:
- +5 bonus to Perception and Investigation checks, with advantage
- Advantage on initiative
- Cannot be surprised
- Outdoors: a white raven appears to point out clues; indoors: a small white feather floats near clues
