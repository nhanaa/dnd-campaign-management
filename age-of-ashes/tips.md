# Playstyle Tips — Zane Alphire (Earth/Wood Kineticist)

## Action Economy Priority

### Round 1 — Opening
1. **Channel Elements** (1 action) — activate kinetic aura + 1-action Elemental Blast or Ravel of Thorns stance
2. **Armor in Earth** (1 action) — stone armor for the fight (skip if already active, lasts 10 min)
3. **Stride** or **Elemental Blast** (1 action) — position or attack

If Armor in Earth is already up:
1. **Channel Elements + Ravel of Thorns** (1 action) — aura + stance in one action
2. **Stride** (1 action) — get into position, thorns grow at end of turn
3. **Elemental Blast** (1 action) — melee with Weapon Infusion

### Standard Round — Ravel of Thorns Active
1. **Elemental Blast** with Weapon Infusion (free + 1 action) — melee reach or sweep
2. **Elemental Blast** (1 action) — MAP -5, consider agile via Weapon Infusion
3. **Battle Medicine** or **Stride** or **Blast** (1 action)

*Ravel of Thorns is passive — no action cost while in stance. Thorns reset at end of turn if you moved.*

### Burst Round — Tremor (Overflow)
1. **Weapon Infusion + Elemental Blast** (free + 1 action) — Strike first
2. **Tremor** (2 actions, overflow) — 10-ft burst, Fort save, prone on crit fail, difficult terrain. **Deactivates aura.**

*After overflow: kinetic aura is down. Next round spend 1 action on Channel Elements to restart. Plan accordingly.*

### Healing Round
1. **Battle Medicine** (1 action) — Assurance 18 guarantees success. Use Risky Surgery for +2 if the patient can take 1d8.
2. **Elemental Blast** or **Stride** (remaining actions)

### Timber Sentinel Round
1. **Timber Sentinel** (1 action) — place Protector Tree adjacent to a squishy ally
2. Two remaining actions for Blasts/Stride/Medicine

## Key Tactical Notes

### Weapon Infusion (Free Action)
- **Always use before Elemental Blast.** It's free.
- **Reach** (melee): Attack from 10 ft. Avoid getting adjacent to dangerous enemies.
- **Sweep** (melee): +1 circumstance bonus to attack against second target when hitting adjacent enemies. Good for multiple melee foes.
- **Agile** (melee): MAP -4 instead of -5, then -8 instead of -10. Better for 2+ attacks per round.
- **Forceful** (melee): +1 damage on second hit, +2 on third. Stacks with multi-attack rounds.
- **Backswing**: +1 circumstance bonus after a miss. Insurance on second Strikes.

### Armor in Earth
- Lasts 10 minutes. Put it up before fights when possible.
- At level 3+: heavy armor, AC 22, bulwark (+3 Reflex instead of DEX).
- Plate specialization: resistance to non-magical B/P/S damage. At level 7+ with weapon specialization this is relevant.
- **Does not stack with worn armor.** Worn armor is suppressed.

### Ravel of Thorns (Stance)
- Thorns fill your entire kinetic aura (10-ft emanation).
- -10 ft speed to creatures starting their turn in it. Hazardous terrain dealing damage per square.
- **Double damage on soil/water.** Outdoor fights on natural ground = double hazardous damage.
- **Thorns disappear when you move, regrow at end of turn.** Plan movement carefully — if you need to reposition, thorns reset. Sometimes staying put is better.
- Replaces kinetic aura benefits of other stances. Only one stance at a time.

### Tremor (Overflow)
- **Costs your kinetic aura.** Don't use early unless the prone + difficult terrain is worth losing your stance.
- Best use: when multiple enemies are clustered in a 10-ft burst and you've already gotten value from your stance.
- Prone on crit fail = Off-Guard for the whole party. Barbarian and Sorcerer benefit.
- Difficult terrain until start of YOUR next turn — enemies waste movement.

### Battle Medicine + Assurance + Risky Surgery
- **Assurance 18**: Automatically succeeds on DC 15 (trained) and DC 20 (expert) Treat Wounds. At level 4 with expert Medicine, this heals 2d8 HP guaranteed.
- **Risky Surgery**: +2 circumstance bonus, deal 1d8 slashing to patient. Use when you need the higher DC bracket or are rolling instead of Assurance. Net positive healing.
- **1-hour immunity per target.** Track who you've healed.
- In exploration mode: use Treat Wounds normally (no Battle Medicine needed). Assurance still works.

### Extract Element
- Use against creatures with earth or wood traits (elementals, plant creatures, earth-based monsters).
- Fort save. On failure: bypass their immunities, -1 to saves/AC vs your impulses, ignore their resistances.
- 1 action setup, lasts 5 min. Worth it against tough elemental enemies.

### Timber Sentinel
- Protector Tree: AC 10, 20 HP (rank 2). Absorbs damage for adjacent allies.
- Place next to the Sorcerer or Druid when enemies get through.
- If it survives and is on soil, it becomes a permanent mundane tree. Flavor win.
- Using Timber Sentinel again ends the previous tree. Only one active at a time.

## Positioning

- **Ideal position**: 10-15 ft from enemies, using reach via Weapon Infusion. Ravel of Thorns covers the space between you and the enemy, punishing approach.
- **Protect ranged allies**: Stay between enemies and the Sorcerer/Druid. Your thorns and difficult terrain slow anything trying to get past you.
- **Don't overextend**: You're durable (AC 22, physical resistance) but you're not a barbarian. Your value is control and healing, not tanking every hit.

## Elemental Blast Quick Reference

| Element | Damage | Type Options | Range |
|---------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Earth | 1d8 (2d8 at 5) | Bludgeoning or Piercing | Melee / 30 ft |
| Wood | 1d8 (2d8 at 5) | Bludgeoning or Vitality | Melee / 30 ft |

- **Melee**: +9 attack, 1d8 + 3 STR damage
- **Ranged**: +7 attack, 1d8 damage
- **2-action blast**: +4 CON status bonus to damage
- **Weapon Infusion**: Change to B/P/S, add weapon trait

*Vitality (wood) is effective against undead. Remember this for undead encounters.*
