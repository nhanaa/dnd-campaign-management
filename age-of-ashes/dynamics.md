## Party Gaps

- Zane's arrival fills the biggest gap: **no dedicated combat healer** before he joined
- Zane adds a second frontliner alongside the barbarian
- **No rogue/thievery** — traps and locks could be a problem
- **Social skills** unclear — Zane has no Diplomacy/Intimidation/Deception
- Party has heavy dragon themes (Draconic Sorcerer, Dragonblood Barbarian) which is interesting given the campaign opposes draconic destruction

## Party Synergy

### Zane + Barbarian
- Two melee bodies. Barbarian is the damage dealer, Zane is the controller and backup.
- Ravel of Thorns punishes enemies approaching the barbarian. Tremor prones them for the barbarian to hit while they're Off-Guard.
- Battle Medicine keeps the barbarian standing. Risky Surgery is worth it on a high-HP barbarian.

### Zane + Ranged Characters (Sorcerer/Druid)
- Zane's terrain control (Tremor difficult terrain, Ravel speed penalty, Jagged Berms cover) protects ranged party members.
- Timber Sentinel provides Protector Tree cover adjacent to squishier allies.
- Zane holds the front so ranged characters can operate safely.

## Dynamics Notes

### Session 10 — First Impressions

**Zane → Birch**: Birch vetted Zane on the Leaf-druid red line ("nothing to do with raising the dead?"). Once cleared, he warmed quickly. Birch made Zane swear secrecy about the Alseta Ring before showing him the gates. Birch is the party's social/magic anchor — Zane defers to him on diplomacy.

**Zane → Varkos**: Varkos took the lead on hiring Zane and laid out the cult threat directly, including offering a chance to back out. Direct, generous-with-loot-explanations, magpie. Zane reads him as honest but not subtle.

**Zane → Meepos**: Not yet met. Meepos was absent. Mentioned by the others as the party's arcane brain — does identifies, transferred a striking rune onto Varkos's halberd, working on the gate research full-time.

**Read on Nketiah**: Both Zane and Varkos clocked her enthusiasm as performative. She's the village linguist (a status role), wears one wooden prosthetic arm, speaks fluent common with no accent. Her father has gold (one of the "purest warriors") and has encountered the Cinder Claws — but she "didn't take notes." Watch.

**Read on Jahsi**: Straightforward. Of the Leopold clan / River clan parentage. Worships Yuelral — refused magical healing for an open wound out of religious edict. If the Wildscale Conclave needs a friendly contact in Akrivel, lean Jahsi.
