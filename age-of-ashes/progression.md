# Build Progression — Zane Alphire

## Concept

Human (Dromaar) **Earth/Wood Dual Gate Kineticist** — frontline controller and field medic. Armor in Earth provides heavy armor AC without equipment. Weapon Infusion shapes elemental blasts into melee weapons with reach or sweep. Tremor and Ravel of Thorns lock down the battlefield with difficult terrain, speed penalties, and prone. Medicine investment (Battle Medicine + Risky Surgery + Assurance) makes Zane the party's combat healer. Earth and wood are the elements of building and mending — thematic counterpoint to the Dahak fire nightmares that drive his story.

## Build Philosophy

- **Armor in Earth** = free heavy armor scaling with level. No equipment needed. Bulwark at level 3. Plate specialization for physical resistance.
- **Weapon Infusion** = flexible melee/ranged Elemental Blasts. Reach for safe frontline, sweep for multi-target, or agile for MAP reduction. Free action, no cost.
- **Ravel of Thorns (stance)** = passive aura control. -10 ft speed + hazardous terrain in kinetic aura. Punishes melee enemies approaching the party. Doesn't require actions after activation.
- **Tremor (overflow)** = burst AoE with prone on crit fail + difficult terrain. Opens fights or resets positioning. Costs the aura (overflow), so use strategically.
- **Battle Medicine + Risky Surgery + Assurance** = guaranteed DC 18 heal in combat. 1 action. Risky Surgery adds +2 to the check for bigger heals at the cost of 1d8 to the patient (net positive). Assurance removes RNG — you always succeed.
- **Timber Sentinel** = deployable cover for ranged allies. Protector Tree absorbs hits for adjacent creatures. Repositionable each round.

## Level-by-Level Plan

### Level 5 — Gate's Threshold
- **Elemental Blast**: 2d8
- **Gradual Boost**: 4th of 4 (TBD)
- **Gate's Threshold**: Gain access to composite impulses (earth+wood). Consider **Jagged Berms** (composite, earth+wood) — conjure earth mounds with wooden stakes. Cover + hazardous terrain in a 120-ft range.
- **Class Feat (Kineticist 6)**: **Jagged Berms** — up to 6 mounds providing cover, with piercing wooden stakes on adjacent squares. Excellent battlefield shaping at range. Unlimited duration.
- **Skill Feat**: **Continual Recovery** — reduces Treat Wounds immunity to 10 min. Essential for between-combat healing.
- **Ancestry Feat**: TBD (Dromaar/Orc/Human options)

### Level 6
- **Class Feat (Kineticist 6)**: If not taken at 5, **Dash of Herbs** (wood, healing) — 30-ft ranged heal + condition removal (disease, poison, sickened, confused). Heals with d10s for injuries. Enhances medic role with magical healing.
- Alternative: **Sand Snatcher** (earth) — summonable grappler that flanks and pins enemies. Uses impulse attack roll. Sustainable 1 min.

### Level 7
- **General Feat**: **Toughness** or **Incredible Initiative**
- **Class Feature**: Perception expert, weapon specialization (+2 damage to blasts)
- **Skill Feat**: **Ward Medic** — Treat Wounds on 2 patients simultaneously during exploration.

### Level 8
- **Class Feat (Kineticist 8)**: **Spike Skin** (earth) — grant resistance 4 to all physical damage to an ally. 10 min duration. Great on the barbarian frontliner.
- Alternative: **Drifting Pollen** (wood, stance) — aura inflicts Sickened + Dazzled on enemies. Fort save. Replaces Ravel of Thorns as the stance of choice when debuffing matters more than terrain control.
- Alternative: **Sanguivolent Roots** (wood, overflow) — 15-ft burst AoE that damages enemies AND heals allies in the area. Blood-drinking vines. Sustainable.

### Level 9
- **Elemental Blast**: 3d8
- **Class Feature**: Fortitude mastery, gate's threshold expands
- **Ancestry Feat**: TBD

### Level 10
- **Class Feat (Kineticist 10)**: TBD — evaluate based on campaign needs

### Level 12
- **Class Feat (Kineticist 12)**: **Rock Rampart** (earth, overflow) — Wall of Stone effect. 40-ft wall. **Rattle the Earth** (earth, overflow) — 60-ft cone earthquake. Or **Witchwood Seed** (wood, overflow) — implant seed that immobilizes and debuffs.

## Combat Role in Party

Zane fills gaps the current party lacks:
- **Frontline durability**: Armor in Earth gives heavy armor AC. Only the barbarian is currently melee.
- **Battlefield control**: Tremor (prone + difficult terrain), Ravel of Thorns (speed penalty + hazardous terrain), Jagged Berms (cover + stakes). The party has two ranged characters who benefit from enemies being slowed and blocked.
- **Combat healing**: Battle Medicine with Assurance guarantees heals without rolling. No other party member has dedicated healing.
- **Condition removal**: Dash of Herbs (at level 6) handles disease, poison, and sickened — important in Age of Ashes.
