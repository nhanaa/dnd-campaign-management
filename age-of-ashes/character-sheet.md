## Backstory

Zane grew up helping his mother, a healer, in a small settlement where nobody looked twice at a half-orc kid. She taught him everything — how to set a bone, clean a wound, which herbs stop bleeding. He'd always had nightmares about fire, vivid ones, but she told him not to worry about it. Just bad dreams. One night he dreamed their home burning and told her. She said the same thing. Three days later the settlement was raided and set ablaze. He couldn't get to her in time. After that he found a priest who recognized something in his visions — the touch of Dahak, a god of destruction. He didn't fully understand what that meant, but he stopped ignoring his dreams. He took his mother's name, picked up her kit, and started walking. Spent years moving from town to town, closing wounds for whoever needed it, not really belonging anywhere. Somewhere along the way his body started doing strange things — stones shifting out of his path, roots curling toward his hands while he slept. Earth and wood, never fire. He figured it out on his own, mostly. Two months ago the nightmares changed. Instead of shapeless fire he saw a specific place — a statue, a town square, buildings going up like matchsticks. He asked around until someone told him it sounded like Breachill. He went.

## Kinetic Gate — Dual Gate (Earth / Wood)

### Core Class Features

- **Channel Elements** (1 action): Activate kinetic aura (10-ft emanation). Can use a 1-action Elemental Blast or stance impulse as part of this action. Aura deactivates if knocked out, overflow used, or dismissed.
- **Elemental Blast** (1 or 2 actions): Attack with kinetic element.
  - **Earth**: 1d8 bludgeoning or piercing, melee or 30 ft ranged
  - **Wood**: 1d8 bludgeoning or vitality, melee or 30 ft ranged
  - Melee attack: +9 (6 prof + 3 STR), damage 1d8 + 3 STR
  - Ranged attack: +7 (6 prof + 1 DEX), damage 1d8
  - 2-action blast: +4 CON status bonus to damage
- **Base Kinesis** (2 actions): Generate, move, or suppress 1 bulk of earth or wood within 30 ft. Utility/flavor.
- **Extract Element** (1 action, level 3): Target creature with earth/wood trait within 30 ft. Fort save vs class DC. On failure: deals damage, bypasses target's immunity/resistance to your impulses, -1 circumstance penalty to saves/AC vs your impulses for 5 min.

## Impulses

### Armor in Earth (1 action, earth)
Stone encases you as armor. Uses highest armor proficiency. At level 3+: heavy armor, AC +5, DEX cap +1, bulwark, plate group. Lasts 10 min. Armor specialization: resistance to non-magical physical damage.

### Timber Sentinel (1 action, wood)
Conjure a Protector Tree within 30 ft. Rank 2 (half level rounded up). Medium tree with AC 10, 20 HP. Intercepts damage for adjacent allies. Lasts 1 min.

### Tremor (2 actions, earth, overflow)
10-ft burst within 30 ft. 2d8 bludgeoning, Fort save. Crit fail: knocked Prone. Area becomes difficult terrain until start of next turn. Deactivates kinetic aura (overflow).

### Ravel of Thorns (1 action, wood, stance)
Thorny vines in kinetic aura. Creatures starting turn in aura: -10 ft speed penalty. Hazardous terrain dealing damage per square entered. Double damage on water/soil. Thorns disappear when you move, regrow at end of turn.
