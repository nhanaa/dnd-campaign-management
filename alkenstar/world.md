## Setting: The Mana Wastes / Alkenstar

A steam-powered metropolis in the Mana Wastes — a magically unstable region between the warring nations of Nex and Geb. Firearms and clockwork technology thrive here because magic is unreliable. The city of Alkenstar is known for its Grand Duchy, its gunsmiths, and its industrial innovation.

**Themes**: Steampunk, urban fantasy, political intrigue, heists, outlaws seeking retribution.

**Premise**: The crew was crossed and left to rot. Now running from the law, they're working to bring balance — and revenge — against those who betrayed them. The campaign involves clockwork creatures, fantastic firearms, industrial dungeons, magical wilds, and social intrigue.
