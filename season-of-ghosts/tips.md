# Mo — Tactical Playbook

## Core Identity

You are a **shadow-folk skirmisher** built around two interlocking loops:

1. **Off-guard burst** — set up off-guard via initiative, flank, or self-procced (Unbalancing Blow on crits). Sneak Attack triggers on every Strike against off-guard targets.
2. **Cover-and-Hide cycle** — Dance of the Mousedeer or Slink Away creates cover → Hide → next-turn engagement from hidden = off-guard target.

The Spirit Warrior FA layers a damage-density bonus on top: **Overwhelming Combination** lets you make two Strikes for one action, both eligible for Sneak Attack.

---

## Round 1: Burst Window

**Always lead with Avoid Notice exploration mode** between encounters when terrain allows. Triggers Stealth-init at fight start.

**Initiative**: roll Stealth (+11), add **Incredible Initiative +2 circumstance** = ~+13. You will go first against most enemies.

**Surprise Attack** procs if you act before a target — that target is **off-guard** for the round 1 attack.

**Optimal opening sequence**:
| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Overwhelming Combination** (1A flourish) | Rapier Strike (1d6+4 + 1d6 sneak vs off-guard) + Fist Strike (1d6 bludgeoning + 1d6 sneak via +1 striking Handwraps) |
| **Strike** (1A) | Second rapier Strike (MAP -5 with agile-equivalent... actually rapier isn't agile, so MAP -5). Sneak Attack still applies if target still off-guard. |
| **Step / Stride / Hide** (1A) | Reposition or hide for next turn |

**Round-1 expected damage vs off-guard** (assuming hits):
- Overwhelming Combination: ~14 (rapier 1d6+4+1d6) + ~7 (fist 1d6+1d6) = **21 damage**
- Optional second Strike: another 7-14 damage
- **Burst ceiling: ~35 damage if all three Strikes connect**

## Sustained Loop (Rounds 2+)

**Without** Surprise Attack, you need a fresh off-guard source. Options in priority:

### Best (no action cost)
- **Flank with Ming Bao** — both of you proc your Backstabber/Sneak Attack synergies on a flanked target. Stay adjacent to Ming Bao when he engages.
- **Unbalancing Blow proc** — if you crit a target, they are off-guard against your attacks for next turn. Free continuation of Sneak Attack chain.
- **Ally debuff** — Mingxi or Jun-Yeong dropping prone/frightened/off-guard via spells. Strike same target.

### Cheap (1 action)
- **Dance of the Mousedeer** (Performance vs single enemy's Perception) → on success, standard cover → can immediately Hide → next turn engage from Hidden = off-guard. On crit: greater cover (+4 AC).
- **Hide / Sneak** in environmental cover or via Refined Motion in Darkness in dim light.

### Action-tax (2 actions)
- **Slink Away** (1A) → Hide + Sneak vs one observer. Cover-equivalent for that creature.
- Move to flank position.

## Defensive Decisions

### When to Nimble Dodge (reaction)
- The attack that would drop you below a damage threshold (last 10-15 HP).
- A crit threat (the d20 looks like it's near hitting on its second die).
- Prefer to save reactions for melee Strikes, not ranged (less crit-on-crit risk on ranged).

### When to use Dance of the Mousedeer defensively
- Caster targeting you with a spell — the cover may force them to retarget.
- You're the only one in melee range and need to break line-of-sight.

### When to disengage with Slink Away
- HP below 1/3.
- Boss with reactions you can't easily disengage from.
- Target needs to lose track of you so you can re-flank from new angle.

## Damage Type Coverage

You have **no native fire/holy access** (no spellcaster archetype, no spellheart). Coverage comes from cash purchases:

| Need | Source | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Fire** | Lesser Alchemist's Fire (thrown bomb) | 3 gp |
| **Fire on Strike** | Lesser Burning Talisman (single-use, +1d6 fire) | 4 gp |
| **Holy / vs undead** | Holy Water (thrown alchemical, 1d6 positive) | 3 gp |
| **Cold iron (vs jinkin/fey)** | Cold Iron Blanch (Lesser, makes weapon cold iron 10 strikes / 1 min) | 10 gp |
| **vs incorporeal undead** | Lesser Ghost Charge talisman (+2d6 force) | ~5 gp |

**Throw an alchemical** = ranged Strike via DEX. **Sneak Attack applies if target is off-guard.** Ranged Strike requirement: ranged weapon attack OR ranged unarmed attack. Bombs are ranged martial weapons.

Carry minimum: 2 Cold Iron Blanches (jinkin will be back), 2-3 Alchemist's Fires, 2-3 Holy Waters.

## Skill Tactics

### Initiative & exploration
- **Avoid Notice** when traveling. Stealth init is your goal every fight.
- **Terrain Stalker (Underbrush)** auto-Sneaks in forest underbrush — almost the entire campaign happens in Specterwood.

### Investigation
- **Religion** (+5) is your best vs-undead/spirit Recall Knowledge. Use this when fighting ghosts.
- **Occultism** (+4) for hauntings, curses, fey lore.
- **Survival** (+5) in the hinterlands and on the Pilgrim's Path to Tan Sugi.

### Social
- **Deception** (+10) for Lies and Feints. Feint creates off-guard at -10 ft range with Deception vs target Perception.
- **Performance** (+10) for Dance of the Mousedeer, plus general Make an Impression via art.
- **Intimidation** (+7) is the weakest of your social cluster. Demoralize via Intimidating Glare works without language penalty, but the +7 won't reliably beat Will DCs at level. Use Demoralize as a "free" action when nothing better presents — don't rely on it.

### Theft
- **Thievery** (+8) — Pickpocket, Pick Lock, Disable Device. Fits the urchin RP. With Thieves' Toolkit this is your bread and butter outside combat.

## Action-Economy Cheat Sheet

3-action turn templates by situation:

| Situation | A1 | A2 | A3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Round 1, target in melee** | Overwhelming Combination | Strike (rapier) | Step/Stride |
| **Round 1, target out of reach** | Stride to engage | Strike (rapier) | Strike (rapier) — MAP -5 |
| **Need re-Hide** | Strike | Dance of the Mousedeer | Hide |
| **Ranged engagement** | Throw alchemical (ranged Strike) | Throw alchemical (Strike, MAP) | Reposition |
| **HP critical** | Slink Away | (Hidden, hidden, hidden) | Drink potion / heal |
| **Crit happened, sustain** | Strike (off-guard from Unbalancing Blow) | Strike | Strike |

**Reaction**: always keep one available for Nimble Dodge.

## Long-Term Goals (L4–L12)

Tactical shifts to plan for:

- **L4** — Spirit Warrior next archetype feat. Likely *Kaiju Defense Oath* or follow-up Spirit Warrior path. Class feat picks: **Mobility** (avoid reactive strikes when moving) or **Reactive Pursuit**.
- **L5** — Sneak Attack scales to 2d6. Auto Rune adds +1 Striking to weapons (rapier becomes 2d6+4, plus 2d6 sneak). **Major damage scaling.**
- **L7** — Rogue gets Weapon Specialization (+2 damage with finesse) and Perception Mastery (you go to expert). Skill increases unlock master in some skill — **Stealth to master** is the strongest pick (Foil Senses unlocks).
- **L8** — Spirit Warrior advanced feats. Auto Rune +2 weapon potency.
- **L11** — Sneak Attack scales to 3d6. Rogue Expertise. Auto Rune +2 striking.
- **L12** — Campaign endpoint. Final boss tier.

**Skill feat priority sequence**: Pickpocket (urchin RP), Cat Fall, Battle Cry (when Intimidation hits master at L7+), Foil Senses (when Stealth hits master).

## Don'ts

- **Don't engage without off-guard** if you can avoid it — your damage drops by 1d6+ per Strike without Sneak Attack.
- **Don't tank** — 38 HP, AC 18, and CON 12 means you fold to focused fire fast. Mo dies in 2-3 hits from a moderate enemy.
- **Don't waste actions on Demoralize** unless Will DCs are low — your Intimidation +7 is the weakest of your social skills.
- **Don't move out of cover during Dance of the Mousedeer** — moving ends the effect. Use it when you'll stay in place.
- **Don't burn Slink Away as a regular action** — its 1-minute immunity means you can only use it once per fight on a given target.

## Things to Track at the Table

- **Hero Points** (3/3 currently — use one for any save you really can't afford to fail)
- **Seasonal Boon (Outgoing variant)**: once per week, a downtime activity benefiting Willowshore that you fail → succeed instead (or crit fail → fail).
- **Foundation seasonal boon**: once per season, automatic crit success on a recovery check (yours or adjacent ally's).
- **Fist + Handwraps invested** — confirm at start of session.
- **Off-guard sources active on each enemy** — track which targets are still off-guard from your last crit (Unbalancing Blow lasts until end of *your next turn*, so it's a 1-round window).
