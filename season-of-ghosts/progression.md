## Equipment Path (with Auto Rune Progression)

Auto Rune handles fundamental runes, so all spend goes to property runes, magic items, and consumables.

| Level | Auto Rune Grant | Suggested property runes / items |
|---|---|---|
| L3 | +1 weapon potency | (current — no property runes yet) |
| L4 | +1 armor potency | Wealth-by-level catch-up: spend on **Returning** (not useful, rapier isn't thrown) — skip; consider **Talisman attachment** |
| L5 | +1 striking weapon | Add **Striking** rune — auto |
| L8 | +2 weapon potency, +1 armor resilient | Property rune options unlock — **Frost** (1d6 cold, L8) for elemental coverage, or **Flaming** (1d6 fire, L8) for the DM-flagged fire damage type |
| L10 | +2 striking, +2 armor potency | **Holy** rune (L11) becomes target — closes the holy-tag-on-strikes gap |
| L12 | +3 weapon potency | Final tier item shopping |

**Critical milestone: L8 Flaming rune** = your build finally has direct fire damage on every Strike. Until then, alchemical fire bombs from cash are the bridge.

**Critical milestone: L11 Holy rune** = covers the DM's "holy on strikes" hint that we couldn't access at L3.

## Notes

- **Sneak Attack progression**: 1d6 (L1) → 2d6 (L5) → 3d6 (L11). The L5 jump is the biggest mid-game power tier.
- **Spirit Warrior Dedication ramp**: dedication → basic feats at L4 → expert proficiency feats at L8+. Plan archetype feats every other even level (L4, L6, L8, L10, L12).
- **Dance of the Mousedeer scaling**: 1 target → 2 at expert Performance → 4 at master → 8 at legendary. Master Performance is high priority.
- **Wayang ancestry feats unknown beyond L1/L3 picks**. Audit the catalog before L5/L9/L13 selections. Tian Xia Character Guide should have higher-level Wayang options.
