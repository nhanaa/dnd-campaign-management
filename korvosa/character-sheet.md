## Variant Rules in Play

- **Automatic Rune Progression**: Runes scale automatically
- **Free Archetype**: Extra archetype feat at even levels
- **Ancestry Paragon**: Extra ancestry feat at 1st and odd levels
- **Gradual Ability Boosts**: 1 boost per level (2nd–5th) instead of 4 at 5th
- **Skill Paragon (Diplomacy)**: Expert at 3, Master at 7, Legendary at 15; bonus general feat at 3/7/15 requiring Diplomacy proficiency

## Homebrew Rules

- **8-Hour Rests**: Restore to full HP
- **Collision**: Forced movement into solid objects = falling damage (remaining distance)
- **Background Lore**: Background Lore skills follow Additional Lore progression (increases at 3/7/15)
- **Plot Armor**: Players may request NPCs gain Dying instead of instant death (NPCs die at Dying 2, companions at Dying 4)
- **Incapacitation (Modified)**: Only upgrades crit fails → fails, or downgrades crit successes → successes
- **Resting in Dungeons**: One free 10-min rest between encounters; 2nd/3rd require DC 11/16 flat checks
- **Materia**: Runes are socketed crystals; 2 Actions to remove from socket
