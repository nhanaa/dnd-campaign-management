# World — Korvosa

## System
- **System:** Pathfinder 2e Remastered
- **Campaign:** Curse of the Crimson Throne (Korvosa)

## Story So Far (as of Level 4)

### Key Events
- Defeated **Gaedren Lamm**
- **Korvosa went up in flames** (city-wide unrest) due to the death of the king, the queen took the throne
- Now pursuing **Rolth** (Gaedren's son), who escaped
- Cleared a graveyard of **Derros** (Rolth's minions) — Rolth fled again
- Granted an **audience with the Queen**, who thanked the party for eradicating the Derro threat
- **Queen's Reward**: Money + one **Level 10 item** from the treasury vault — Marwen chose the **Greater Demon Mask**
- Week of **downtime**: Caliban trained with Sabina, Beast visited Bank of Abadar, Bergatha crafted, Marwen gathered intel, Rattle invested
- **Trinia Sabor** — young artist accused of assassinating the king. Party found her, interrogated her, and **hid her in Yargin's North Point safe house** rather than turning her over
- Fabricated evidence pointing to the town of **Harse** to throw off pursuit
- **Cressida Kroft** saw through the deception but tacitly approved. Gave **Vencarlo Orisini's** address as a smuggling contact
- Queen Ileosa announced capture and public execution of "Trinia Sabor" — actually a scapegoat
- **Blackjack** intervened at the execution, freed the condemned woman, declared "Down with the queen!" — riot erupted
- **Party leveled up to 4**

## NPCs

- **Vencarlo Orisini** — Swordmaster, ally. Contact provided by Cressida for smuggling Trinia out of the city
- **Sabina** — The Queen's personal guard. Now leads the **Grey Maidens**. Trained Caliban during downtime
- **King of Spiders** — Influential NPC with significant connections
- **Cressida Kroft** — Field Marshal of the Korvosan Guard. Tacit ally; hired the party to find Trinia, knows they're hiding her
- **Ishani Dhatri** — Priest/teller at the Bank of Abadar. Manages Beast's anonymous donation to Eliza Blythe; manages Rattle's investments
- **Trinia Sabor** — Young artist (Bard, Level 5 creature) accused of regicide. Party believes her innocent. Hidden in safe house
- **Yargin Balko** — Former Gaedren associate, now the party's housekeeper/assistant. Manages the safe house. Party calls him "Jargon"
- **Verik** — Butcher shop owner at All the World's Meats (wanted, 50gp bounty). Works for the **Arkonas** noble family
- **Blackjack** — Legendary vigilante. Appeared at the execution to free the condemned woman. Identity unknown
- **Eliza Blythe** — Old woman near the docks. Beast's anonymous beneficiary (800gp via Ishani)
- **Marcus Endron** — Interim leader of the Sable Company (Seneschal is missing)
- **Queen Ileosa** — Young queen (25). Cunning political operator. Executed a scapegoat publicly. Forming the Grey Maidens

## Locations

- **Korvosa** — Primary city, currently in unrest

- **Yargin's Safe House, North Point** — Hidden safe house where Trinia is being kept
- **Bank of Abadar** — Beast and Rattle both have accounts with Ishani
- **All the World's Meats (Verik's Butcher Shop)** — Arkonas-connected business, Marwen established a contact there
- **Harse** — Nearby town; fabricated as Trinia's escape destination
- **The Shingles** — Rooftop network in Midland used by daredevils and fugitives
