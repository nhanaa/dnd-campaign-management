# Build Progression — Marwen (Fioren)

## Concept

Frozen Wind Kitsune Draconic (Rime) Sorcerer — elemental blaster with intel-driven targeting (Whispers of Weakness), Frightened economy via Demoralize, and scaling signature spells. Oracle Dedication (Cosmos) for weakness identification. Primal tradition for blasting/healing/control.

## Build Philosophy

- **Whispers of Weakness → exploit** = core combat loop. 1 action to learn weakness + lowest save, then blast the right element at the right save.
- **Signature spells scale, low-rank slots become utility** — Heal (rank 1), Sudden Bolt (rank 2), Blazing Bolt (rank 4) all heighten freely. Low-rank slots used for spells that don't need heightening (Grease, Fear, Dispel Magic, Entangling Flora).
- **Demoralize engine** — Intimidating Glare (no language needed), Greater Demon Mask (+2 item bonus), Aura of Courage (allies shake Frightened faster). 1-action debuff every round.
- **Star Orb familiar** — Enhanced Familiar (4 abilities/day): Innate Surge, Cantrip Connection (Stabilize), Familiar Focus, Restorative Familiar. Passive value, no movement needed.
- **Fire coverage via Blazing Bolt** — multi-target, no MAP, signature at rank 4 (free heighten). Learn at higher ranks as needed.
- **Cold theme** — Frostbite cantrip, Rime Slick, Ice Ball. Bloodline gifts at rank 5 (Howling Blizzard) and rank 8 (Arctic Rift) are cold AoE nukes.
- **DM rulings** — Rime Slick replaces Shatter (bloodline gift, thematic swap). Flurry of Claws deals cold damage (reflavor approved). Ice Ball = Fireball reflavored to cold.

## Combat Loop

### Round 1 (Setup)
1. **Whispers of Weakness** (1 action) → learn weakness + lowest save
2. Cast the right spell: fire weakness → Blazing Bolt, electricity → Sudden Bolt, Reflex weakest → Ice Ball/Grease, Will weakest → Fear

### Subsequent Rounds
1. **Demoralize** with Intimidating Glare (1 action, no language)
2. Cantrip or spell matching the weakness (2 actions)
3. OR: Haste on Caliban/Ruin (round 1), then Demoralize + cantrip every round after

### Emergency
- **Heal** (signature, heighten freely) — touch/ranged/AoE
- **Stabilize** (cantrip, 30ft) — save dying ally without burning a slot
- **Shield** (1 action, +1 AC, Shield Block Hardness 5)
- **Restorative Familiar** (1/day, 2d8 heal from Star Orb)

## Notes

- GM allows uncommon spells freely
- Free retraining before level 6; costs minor gold + 1 week after
- Collision homebrew: forced movement into walls = falling damage (Hydraulic Push synergy)
- Dungeon resting: 1 free 10-min rest, 2nd/3rd require DC 11/16 flat checks
- Whispers of Weakness on one mob = intel on all identical mobs
- Cosmos curse (Enfeebled) = irrelevant for STR 10 caster
- Galactic Hero Points: rerolls treat die below 10 as 10
- Bloodline gifts can't be swapped (but DM allowed Shatter → Rime Slick for thematic fit)
- Blazing Bolt signature at rank 4 also grants casting at ranks 2 and 3 for free (signature rules)
