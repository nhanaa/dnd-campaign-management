# Build Progression — Marwen (Fioren)

## Concept

Frozen Wind Kitsune Draconic (Rime) Sorcerer — cold blaster primary, Occult debuff nuke secondary, Frightened economy master. Uses Primal tradition for daily blasting/healing, Oracle dip for blaster optimization (Whispers of Weakness + Foretell Harm), and Bard Dedication's Occult access for fight-ending debuffs at key moments.

## Build Philosophy

- **Primal spells** = bread and butter (cold blasting, Heal, Fear, Slow, Fireball, utility)
- **Oracle Dedication (Cosmos)** = Whispers of Weakness (auto-learn weakness + lowest save) + Foretell Harm (free AoE bonus damage). Cosmos curse (Enfeebled) is irrelevant for STR 10 caster.
- **Bard Dedication (Occult)** = gateway to devastating Will-save debuffs not on Primal list (Synesthesia, Vision of Death, Warp Mind). No compositions — Courageous Anthem conflicts with Caliban's Bless.
- **Rogue Dedication** = light armor, Surprise Attack, Skill Mastery
- **Greater Demon Mask** = +2 Intimidation + 1/day rank 3 Fear (DC 29). Powers Frightened economy.
- **Sanguine Pendant** (buy later ~1,000 gp) = +2 Intimidation/Arcana + free focus spell. Scales better late game.
- **Skills** = Bon Mot (Diplomacy), Demoralize (Intimidation), No Cause for Alarm — action-1 engine
- **Personal cold staff** (future crafting goal) = expand Primal cold repertoire
- **Fire coverage** = learn Fireball at level 5. No item needed.
- **Resistance coverage** = Overwhelming Energy at level 10 (ignore resistance = your level)

## Three Archetypes (Free Archetype)

### 1. Oracle Dedication — Cosmos (Blaster Optimization)

Whispers of Weakness + Foretell Harm. The two tools that make every blast count.

**Curse**: Enfeebled = cursebound value. Irrelevant with STR 10.
**Cursebound management**: One use per mob fight (free Refocus resets). Both for boss fights (long rest after).

**Retrain**: Currently have Bard Dedication at level 3. Retrain to Oracle Dedication during downtime (ask GM — standard retraining costs minor gold + 1 week per feat. Free retraining at level 6+).

| Level | Free Archetype Feat | What It Gives |

## Cursebound Usage Pattern

| Situation | Use | CB Cost | Reset |
|-----------|-----|---------|-------|
| **Pack of identical mobs** | **Whispers** — learn weakness + save for ALL of them, then AoE blast the right element | CB → 1 | 1 Refocus |
| **Mixed trash mobs** | **Foretell Harm** — blast + free AoE bonus damage | CB → 1 | 1 Refocus |
| **Single weird enemy** | **Whispers** — what IS this thing? Then blast appropriately | CB → 1 | 1 Refocus |
| **Boss** | **Whispers + Foretell Harm** — full combo, maximum damage | CB → 2 | Long rest |
