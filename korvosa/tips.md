# Tips & Tactics — Marwen

## Combat Modes

### Mob Clear (Identical Pack)
1. **Whispers of Weakness** (1 action, CB→1) — learn weakness + lowest save for ALL of them
2. **AoE blast** targeting the right element at the right save (2 actions)
3. **Foretell Harm** (free action, CB→2 only if boss — skip for mobs) — every target takes 2× rank bonus damage

### Mob Clear (Mixed Trash)
1. **AoE blast** (2-3 actions)
2. **Foretell Harm** (free action, CB→1) — free bonus damage on every target hit
3. One Refocus between fights fully resets

### Boss Fight (Full Combo)
1. **Whispers of Weakness** (1 action, CB→1) — auto-learn weakness + lowest save
2. **Bon Mot** or **Demoralize** (1 action) — stack debuffs on the boss
3. **Targeted blast or Occult nuke** (2 actions) — hit the weakness/worst save
4. **Foretell Harm** (free action, CB→2) — bonus damage on top
5. Let Pol delete the debuffed boss

### Flanker Gets Close
- **Demoralize** (1 action) — at Master Intimidation + Demon Mask +2, high crit chance
- With **Terrified Retreat** (level 7+): crit = Frightened 2 + Fleeing. They run.
- Or **Flurry of Claws** — ranged 2-Strike focus spell. Critted for 47 each killing 2 strong mobs.

### Slot-Free Rounds (Conserving Resources)
- **Dragon Breath** (level 6+) — cold AoE cone, auto-heightens, recharges on Refocus
- **Flurry of Claws** — ranged 2-Strike, auto-heightens, recharges on Refocus
- **Frostbite / Electric Arc** — cantrips for chip damage
- **Demon Mask Fear** (1/day) — rank 3 Fear DC 29, no spell slot spent

## Combat Loop

### Round 1 (Alpha Strike)
1. **Incredible Initiative** → go first
2. **Surprise Attack** (Rogue, level 14+) → enemies who haven't acted are off-guard (-2 AC)
3. **Whispers of Weakness** (1 action, Oracle) → auto-learn all weaknesses + lowest save. No roll.
4. **Cast the right spell** (2 actions) targeting their weakest save with the right element
5. **Foretell Harm** (free action, Oracle) → all targets take 2× spell rank bonus damage next turn

### Standard Round
1. **Action 1** (skill debuff/support — pick one):
   - **Bon Mot** (Diplomacy) → -2 to target's Will saves and Perception for 1 min
   - **Demoralize** (Intimidation + Demon Mask +2) → Frightened (penalty to everything)
   - **No Cause for Alarm** (Diplomacy) → strip Frightened from up to 4 allies
   - **Scare to Death** (level 15+, Legendary Intimidation) → enemy might literally die
2. **Actions 2-3** (spell):
   - **Primal blast** (daily workhorse): Frostbite, Sudden Bolt, Fear, Fireball, Slow
   - **Occult nuke** (boss moment): Vision of Death, Synesthesia, Warp Mind
   - **Focus spell** (free resource): Dragon Breath (AoE cone), Flurry of Claws (ranged Strikes)
3. **Foretell Harm** (free action) if you cast a damaging spell — stacks on AoE

### The Debuff Stack
Bon Mot → Demoralize → Fear/Synesthesia is the dream sequence:
1. Bon Mot: -2 to Will saves (sets up everything)
2. Demoralize: Frightened (penalty to everything including saves)
3. Fear or Occult nuke: targets the now-weakened Will save

## Key Combos

- **Whispers → right element AoE → Foretell Harm**: The bread and butter. Learn weakness, blast it, free damage on top.
- **Demon Mask Fear (1/day)**: Open a fight with rank 3 group Fear at DC 29 — no spell slot. Frighten the whole encounter for free.
- **Dragon Breath + Foretell Harm** (level 6+): Free cold cone + free bonus damage. Zero spell slots. Recharges on Refocus.
- **Bon Mot + Synesthesia** (level 14+): -2 Will from Bon Mot, then Synesthesia targeting Will. Boss is crippled, Pol deletes.
- **Demoralize + Terrified Retreat** (level 7+): Crit success on Demoralize = Frightened 2 + Fleeing. Flankers run away.
- **Hydraulic Push + Collision**: Slam enemies into walls for forced movement damage (homebrew rule).
- **Whispers → targeted spell**: Auto-learn weakness + lowest save. Cast the spell that exploits them. No roll needed.
- **Fear + No Cause for Alarm**: Inflict Frightened on enemies, strip it from allies. Control both sides of the Frightened economy.
- **Flurry of Claws**: Ranged focus spell, 2 Strikes, auto-heightens. Proven damage dealer (critted for 94 total at level 3). Only downside: targets AC.
- **Reach Spell + any blast** (level 6+): +30 ft range for safety. Cast from further back in the dungeon.

## Spell Slot Management

- **Primal slots** = daily workhorses. Heal, Fear, Slow, Fireball, Sudden Bolt. Use freely.
- **Occult slots** = nuclear option. 1 per rank. Save for bosses or fight-defining moments.
- **Focus spells** (Flurry of Claws, Dragon Breath at 6+) = free damage every fight. Refocus between encounters.
- **Demon Mask Fear** = free rank 3 Fear 1/day. Use to open fights or handle mob packs without spending slots.
- **Sanguine Pendant** (when purchased) = 1 extra focus spell/day via Blood's Call. Use on Dragon Breath for free AoE or Flurry for burst.

## Positioning

- **Stay behind the frontline** (Pol/Caliban). You're a ranged caster.
- **Reach Spell** (level 6+) adds +30 ft to spell range — more breathing room.
- **Use Shield cantrip** as a reaction when hit — +1 AC for free.
- **Dungeon flanking**: DM likes rear ambushes. Stay in the middle of the party, not the back. Let Rattle & Ruin's companions cover the rear.
- **Flight** (if you get Fly spell later) makes you nearly untouchable by melee.

## Kitsune Tricks

- **Change Shape** before social encounters → different face for different contacts
- **Change Shape + Demon Mask underneath** = drop the disguise, reveal the mask, Demoralize. Peak drama.
- **Star Orb** = ranged unarmed attack option
- **Shapechanger's Intuition** = bonus to detect shapeshifters/illusions

## Party Synergy

- **Pol** (Barbarian): You debuff bosses (Synesthesia = -3 AC), Pol murders them. Core combo.
- **Caliban** (Warpriest): He buffs with Bless (+1 attack), you debuff with Frightened (-X to everything). They stack (different targets: party vs enemy).
- **Rattle & Ruin** (Summoner): Eidolon + companions provide flanking bodies that benefit from your debuffs.
- **Bergatha** (Witch): She handles social face + Arcane casting. You handle operative intel (Whispers, Diplomacy) + Primal/Occult casting. Three traditions covered.
