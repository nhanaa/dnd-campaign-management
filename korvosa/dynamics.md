- **Vencarlo Orisini** — Swordmaster, ally. Contact for smuggling Trinia out of the city
- **Sabina** — Queen's personal guard, now leads the Grey Maidens. Trained Caliban
- **King of Spiders** — Powerful NPC with wide influence in Korvosa. Mutual alliance with party
- **Queen Ileosa** — Cunning political operator. Rewarded party for Derro threat, but executed a scapegoat. Party is increasingly suspicious
- **Cressida Kroft** — Field Marshal, Korvosan Guard. Tacit ally. Knows the party has Trinia hidden. Hired them for investigations
- **Ishani Dhatri** — Abadar priest. Manages Beast's anonymous donation and Rattle's investments
- **Trinia Sabor** — Artist accused of regicide. Party believes innocent. Hidden in Yargin's safe house, North Point
- **Yargin Balko** — Party's housekeeper/assistant ("Jargon"). Manages safe house and helps Bergatha with crafting
- **Verik** — Butcher shop owner at All the World's Meats, works for Arkonas. Marwen established an information broker relationship
- **Blackjack** — Legendary vigilante. Marwen suspects Vencarlo is Blackjack (blind sense motive — uncertain). Tossed Caliban a dagger with his address after the execution rescue.
- **Grau Soldado** — Korvosan Guard sergeant. Cleaned up, sober. Brought party to his sick niece Brianna. Owes the party a favor.
- **Tayce Soldado** — Grau's sister-in-law, single mother/washerwoman in Trails End. Bergatha paid for Brianna's cure. Offers dinner/hospitality.
- **Brianna Soldado** — Tayce's 12-year-old daughter, sick with unknown plague. Ishani casting Remove Disease.
- **Trisha** — The scapegoat from the execution. Named by the party. Now in Harse with ranchers alongside Trinia, Beast, and Yargin.
- **Were-rat Network** — Rattle's mafioso organization. "King of Shinies." Operates in Korvosa's underground. Intel source on Red Mantis and Urgathoa cultists.
- **R.D.** — Unknown figure meeting with Rolth. Two initials only. Identity unknown.

## Plot Threads

- **Rolth & R.D.**: Rolth meeting with unknown figure "R.D." (two initials). New lab in a warehouse on the main island. 500 platinum funding from unknown nobility. Red Mantis assassins (~30-36) and Urgathoa cultists operating in sewers.
- **The Plague**: Unknown disease in Trails End. Quarantine ship (black sails, yellow lights) sunk in harbor ~3 days ago. Brianna Soldado first known case. Ishani casting Remove Disease. May already be spreading.
- **Grey Maidens**: Queen's new elite all-female guard, publicly deployed. Led by Sabina. Recruiting attractive women from Korvosan Guard. Arbiters approve; commoners do not.
- **Harse Safe House**: Trinia, Trisha, Beast, and Yargin safely in Harse with ranchers. Yargin runs supply. Can be called to testify.
- **Vencarlo/Blackjack**: Marwen thinks Vencarlo is Blackjack (blind sense motive — uncertain). Not shared with party. Vencarlo's informants also searching for Rolth's warehouse.
- **Rattle's Empire**: Were-rat mafioso network growing. Rental property in Midland. Drug distribution with Bergatha. "King of Shinies."
- **Queen Ileosa**: Locked in palace for 2 weeks after Blackjack's intervention. Grey Maidens deployed. Approval split between commoners (against) and elites (for).
- **Cressida Kroft**: Overworked, losing female guards to Grey Maidens. Knows about Red Mantis and Urgathoa. Cleared party of treason. Party has trust but with reservations (especially Rattle).
- **Arkonas Noble Family**: Interest in the throne. Verik is their agent. Spider King connection.
- **Sable Company**: Leaderless (Seneschal missing). Interim leader Marcus Endron. Constitutional power to override monarchy.
- **Hell Knights**: Openly hostile to Queen Ileosa, preparing to leave the city.
