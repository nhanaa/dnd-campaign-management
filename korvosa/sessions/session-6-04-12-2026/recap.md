## Session Summary

Following their audience with Queen Ileosa and the reward for returning the brooch, the party enjoyed a week of downtime in Korvosa. Beast quietly arranged to send his 800 gold to an old woman named Eliza Blythe through the Abadar priest Ishani, Caliban trained under Sabina and learned troubling details about the Grey Maidens and the Curse of the Crimson Throne, Bergatha threw herself into alchemical crafting, Rattle recruited a wolf companion named Lobo and invested his entire fortune with Ishani, and Marwen visited the butcher shop owner Verik to gather intelligence for the Spider King.

The downtime was shattered by news that a young artist named Trinia Sabor had been accused of assassinating the king. Cressida Kroft, believing Trinia was being scapegoated, hired the party to find her before the mob could. Through a combination of disguises, invisibility, Bergatha's water elemental familiar, and Rattle's telekinetic hand, the party located Trinia in her apartment, interrogated her, and — after a heated party vote — chose to hide her in one of Jargon's old safe houses rather than turn her over to anyone. They staged her apartment to look like a rushed escape, fabricated a journal entry pointing to the town of Harse, and deceived Cressida (who saw through it but tacitly approved, giving them Vencarlo Orisini's address as a potential contact for smuggling Trinia out).

Two days later, the queen announced she had captured "Trinia Sabor" and would execute her publicly — confirming Bergatha's prediction that the crown would simply find another scapegoat. Beast punched a wall in Cressida's office in helpless fury. The session ended with the party attending the execution as the legendary vigilante Blackjack intervened at the last moment, freeing the prisoner and declaring "Down with the queen!" — plunging the city into another riot.

## Key Events

### Act 1 — Downtime in Korvosa

- **Beast at the Bank of Abadar**: Beast visited Ishani Dhatri and arranged to anonymously send his 800 gold to **Eliza Blythe**, an old woman living near the docks. Ishani set up a payment plan of 10 gold every two weeks. Beast asked Ishani not to reveal where the money came from.
- **Caliban's Training with Sabina**: Week-long bootcamp at Castle Korvosa. Sabina revealed:
  - The queen is forming the **Grey Maidens**, an all-female elite guard unit
  - The Royal Guard and Korvosan Guard have growing tensions
  - Sabina is poaching guards from Cressida Kroft's ranks
  - The **Curse of the Crimson Throne**: no monarch has died of natural causes or produced an heir in 300 years
  - Sabina has no useful intel on **Rolth** — he's Cressida's jurisdiction
- **Bergatha's Crafting**: Alchemical items including lethargy potions, juggernaut mutagens, drakeheart mutagens, life-boosting oils, and a shiver cake (for "emergencies"). Spider venom, invisibility potions, and fear flower nectar were already in the party stash from prior crafting
- **Marwen's Intelligence Gathering**: Visited butcher shop owner **Verik** (wanted, 50gp bounty). Verik confirmed he works for the **Arkonas** noble family and hinted they have interest in the throne. Marwen relayed this intel to Beast, Bergatha, and Rattle (deliberately excluding Caliban as "too good-natured")
- **Rattle's Investments**: Replaced boar companion Tusk with a wolf named **Lobo**. Invested full 1,035 gold with Ishani at Bank of Abadar. Threatened Ishani: "If you fuck with my money, you will die faster."

### Act 2 — The Hunt for Trinia Sabor

- News broke that **Trinia Sabor**, a 22-year-old painter, was accused of poisoning the king's tea during her portrait commission
- A guard who "witnessed" it confessed and then died by suicide pill — Bergatha immediately called it a scapegoat
- **Cressida Kroft** privately briefed the party: she believes Trinia is innocent, the queen is consolidating power, the **Grey Maidens** are being formed to replace the guard
- Cressida gave the party Trinia's address: **42 Moon Street, Midland**
- Party voted unanimously to find Trinia themselves

### Act 3 — Securing Trinia

- The party infiltrated Trinia's apartment building using a multi-layered approach:
  - Bergatha and Marwen: disguised as visitors with flowers and cake (the cake was drugged with shiver)
  - Beast: invisible, climbing walls
  - Caliban: stealthing via Follow the Expert
  - Rattle: flying surveillance outside, sniffed for paint to identify the right window
- **Bergatha's water elemental familiar Lagunae** scouted under the door, confirmed Trinia inside, and unlatched the window
- **Rattle used telekinetic hand** to silently unlock the three chain locks from outside
- The party entered, interrogated Trinia (she claims complete innocence — was just painting), searched her apartment (no evidence of wrongdoing), and **voted 3-2 to question Trinia themselves** before bringing her to Cressida (Bergatha, Rattle, Marwen for questioning first; Beast, Caliban for going straight to Cressida). The later decision to keep Trinia in a safe house was a group consensus after Trinia herself refused to go to any authority
- Bergatha disguised Trinia (dyed hair brunette, changed complexion, dressed as a boy) and gave her a **Potion of Emergency Escape**
- Escorted to **Jargon's safe house in North Point**

### Act 4 — The Deception

- Rattle staged Trinia's apartment to look like a rushed escape
- Bergatha had Trinia write a fake journal entry pointing to the town of **Harse**, then used charcoal rubbing to create "discovered" evidence
- Party reported to Cressida: "She fled to Harse, here's the journal evidence"
- **Cressida saw through the lie** but tacitly approved, saying "If you're hiding her... keep it that way"
- Cressida gave them **Vencarlo Orisini's** address as a contact for potentially smuggling Trinia out of the city
- Party received **50 gold each** for the information
- **Party leveled up to 4**

### Act 5 — The Execution and Blackjack

- Two days later: the queen announced she had captured "Trinia Sabor" and would execute her at noon
- Beast **punched a wall** in Cressida's office: *"Fucking hell! Useless! Do one good thing, try and do the right thing, and it's just... what the fuck is it all for?"*
- The party debated saving the condemned woman but concluded they lacked the power to intervene against the crown's full military display
- Bergatha comforted Beast: *"This will not amount to nothing. This is the beginning of something."*
- Cressida urged the party to attend the execution to prevent riots
- The execution was staged as a **grand spectacle** — confetti, champagne, nobility in gowns. Queen Ileosa appeared in a lavish green and white silk dress, attended by **Sabina** and the new **Grey Maidens** in full plate with crimson plumes
- The woman brought to the guillotine was clearly **not Trinia** — just a superficially similar scapegoat
- Just before the axe fell, **Blackjack** appeared — throwing a dagger into the executioner's hand, freeing the prisoner, and declaring: *"Let us usher in justice, but let that be justice for Korvosa, not the shambles you call a monarchy. Long live Korvosa, down with the queen!"*
- **Session ended on cliffhanger**: riot erupting, party in the thick of it
