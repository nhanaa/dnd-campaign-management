Session Summary

Following their audience with Queen Ileosa, the party enjoyed a week of downtime. Beast quietly arranged to anonymously send 800 gold to an old woman named Eliza Blythe, Caliban trained under Sabina and learned troubling details about the Grey Maidens, Bergatha threw herself into alchemical crafting, Rattle recruited a wolf named Lobo and invested his entire fortune, and Marwen gathered intelligence from the butcher shop owner Verik for the Spider King.

The downtime was shattered by news that a young artist named Trinia Sabor had been accused of assassinating the king. Cressida Kroft hired the party to find her before the mob could. Through disguises, invisibility, a water elemental familiar, and a telekinetic hand, the party located Trinia, interrogated her, and chose to hide her in a safe house rather than turn her over. They fabricated evidence pointing to the town of Harse and deceived Cressida — who saw through it but tacitly approved.

Two days later, the queen announced she had captured “Trinia Sabor” and would execute her publicly — confirming Bergatha’s prediction. The session ended with the legendary vigilante Blackjack intervening at the last moment, freeing the prisoner and declaring “Down with the queen!” — plunging the city into another riot.

Key Events

Act 1 — Roleplay / Downtime

Downtime in Korvosa

  • Beast at the Bank of Abadar — Visited Ishani Dhatri and anonymously arranged to send 800 gold to Eliza Blythe, an old woman near the docks. Payment plan of 10gp every two weeks. Asked Ishani not to reveal the source.
  • Caliban’s Training with Sabina — Week-long bootcamp at Castle Korvosa. Learned about the Grey Maidens (all-female elite guard), Royal Guard tensions with the Korvosan Guard, the Curse of the Crimson Throne (300 years of monarchs dying unnaturally), and that Rolth is Cressida’s jurisdiction.
  • Bergatha’s Crafting — Lethargy potions, juggernaut mutagens, drakeheart mutagens, life-boosting oils, and a shiver cake for “emergencies.”
  • Marwen’s Intelligence Gathering — Visited butcher shop owner Verik (wanted, 50gp bounty). Learned he works for the Arkonas noble family who have interest in the throne. Intel shared with Beast, Bergatha, and Rattle — deliberately excluding Caliban.
  • Rattle’s Investments — Replaced boar companion Tusk with wolf Lobo. Invested full 1,035 gold with Ishani. Threatened: “If you fuck with my money, you will die faster.”
Act 2 — Investigation

The Hunt for Trinia Sabor

  • News broke: Trinia Sabor, a 22-year-old painter, accused of poisoning the king’s tea during her portrait commission.
  • A guard “witness” confessed and died by suicide pill. Bergatha immediately called it a scapegoat: “That’s a very neat bow on that loose end.”
  • Cressida Kroft briefed the party: she believes Trinia is innocent, the queen is consolidating power, the Grey Maidens are being formed. Gave the address: 42 Moon Street, Midland.
  • Party voted unanimously to find Trinia themselves.
Act 3 — Investigation / Stealth

Securing Trinia

  • Multi-layered infiltration of Trinia’s apartment building:
    • Bergatha & Marwen: disguised as visitors with flowers and cake (the cake was drugged)
    • Beast: invisible, climbing walls
    • Caliban: stealthing via Follow the Expert
    • Rattle: flying surveillance, sniffed for paint to identify the right window
  • Bergatha’s familiar Lagunae scouted under the door and unlatched the window.
  • Rattle used telekinetic hand to silently unlock three chain locks.
  • Interrogated Trinia — she claims innocence, no evidence found. Party voted 3-2 to question her before turning her over (Bergatha, Rattle, Marwen vs. Beast, Caliban). Later consensus: keep her in Jargon’s safe house in North Point.
  • Bergatha disguised Trinia (dyed hair brunette, changed complexion) and gave her a Potion of Emergency Escape.
Act 4 — Deception / Intrigue

The Deception

  • Rattle staged the apartment as a rushed escape.
  • Bergatha had Trinia write a fake journal entry pointing to Harse, then used charcoal rubbing to create “evidence.”
  • Reported to Cressida: “She fled to Harse.” Cressida saw through it but tacitly approved: “If you’re hiding her… keep it that way.”
  • Cressida gave them Vencarlo Orisini’s address for smuggling Trinia out. Party received 50gp each.
  • Party leveled up to 4.
Act 5 — Political Crisis

The Execution and Blackjack

  • Two days later: the queen announced she found “Trinia Sabor” and would execute her at noon.
  • Beast punched a wall in Cressida’s office: “The people in power still find some young innocent girl to put the blame on and kill publicly. How fucking typical.”
  • The execution was staged as a grand spectacle — confetti, champagne, nobility in gowns. Queen Ileosa in lavish green-and-white silk, attended by Sabina and the new Grey Maidens in crimson-plumed plate.
  • The condemned woman was clearly not Trinia — a superficially similar scapegoat.
  • Blackjack intervened — dagger in the executioner’s hand, freed the prisoner, declared: “Long live Korvosa, down with the queen!”
  • Riot erupted. Session ended on cliffhanger.
