Session Summary

The session opened mid-combat at the execution square, where the party fought Palace Guards and the Executioner to aid Blackjack’s rescue of the scapegoat. Bergatha’s Mist blanketed the battlefield in concealment, and her Floating Flood crushed guards’ heads in a devastating sustained assault. Marwen used Whispers of Weakness twice — first on a Palace Guard to identify electricity weakness (leading to an Electric Arc crit for 25 damage), then on the Executioner to identify fire weakness, setting up a Blazing Bolt crit for 39 damage. Rattle magnetically stripped the guards of platinum and daggers, and Caliban’s Shielding Taunt and Champion’s Reaction kept allies alive.

With the city calming after the queen’s retreat into her palace, the party enjoyed two weeks of downtime. Rattle built a criminal empire, recruiting were-rats as mafioso and earning the title “King of Shinies.” Bergatha ran a burgeoning drug operation. Marwen traded intelligence with the King of Spiders, learning that Rolth is meeting with a mysterious figure known only as “R.D.,” has set up a laboratory in a warehouse on the main island, and that Red Mantis assassins — roughly 30–36 of them — are operating in the city’s sewers. Caliban identified the skull symbol spotted by Rattle’s were-rat informants as the unholy symbol of Urgathoa.

The party followed Blackjack’s address to Vencarlo Orisini’s apartment, where the scapegoat had been dropped off. Marwen’s blind sense motive told him Vencarlo might be Blackjack. The party escorted Trinia, Trisha, Beast, and Yargin to Harse for safekeeping. Upon return, Grey Maidens were patrolling the streets — the queen’s new all-female elite guard under Sabina. The session ended with a dire hook: Grau brought the party to his sick niece Brianna in Trails End, and Bergatha’s critical success connected the unknown disease to a quarantine ship sunk in the harbor days ago — a plague may already be spreading through Korvosa.

  • Followed Blackjack’s address to an apartment near Orisini Academy on Garrison Hill
  • Vencarlo Orisini answered — has the scapegoat (party named her Trisha). Claims Blackjack dropped her off.
  • Marwen’s sense motive was a blind roll — the DM told him he thinks Vencarlo is Blackjack, but it’s unclear if this is genuine or crit-fail false confidence. Caliban and Rattle bought his story. Bergatha didn’t bother checking.
  • Vencarlo offered 400gp to escort Trisha to Harse. Party decided to move Trinia and Trisha, Beast, and Yargin together.
  • Disguised both women as men (“Tristan” and “Trent”) via Potion of Passing Fancy. Left at night posing as refugees.
  • All four safely delivered to ranchers in Harse. Yargin runs supply between Harse and Korvosa. Trinia and Trisha can be called back to testify against the queen.
  • Marwen shared Rolth warehouse intel with Vencarlo, withholding sources: “Let’s just say I have my ways.”
Act VI — Plot Hook — Grey Maidens & the Plague

A Sickness Stirs

  • Returned to Korvosa — Grey Maidens now publicly patrolling. Queen’s decree: elite all-female military led by Sabina Marin.
  • Grau Soldado arrived at Zellara’s estate — cleaned up, sober, in proper armor. His niece Brianna (12) is sick with an unknown disease in Trails End.
  • Caliban examined Brianna: unknown disease, highly contagious, potentially deadly, never seen before — red pox, veins bulging, constant coughing.
  • Ishani Dhatri was already there but couldn’t heal without 20gp. Bergatha paid (plus 1gp interest). Ishani will cast Remove Disease tomorrow.
  • Tayce Soldado (Brianna’s mother) mentioned: a black-sailed ship with yellow lantern lights entered the harbor ~3 days ago and was sunk by trebuchets in the night.
  • Bergatha (critical success, society): yellow lights = quarantine signal. The ship was a plague vessel. Timeline correlates with Brianna’s illness.
  • Session ended on the plague hook — disease may already be spreading through Korvosa.

  • Caliban: Shielding Taunt. Two strikes — hit for 19 damage.

Round 4 — Cleanup and Escape

  • Blackjack: Knocked out a guard. “I see that I am not the only true hero of Korvosa today.”
  • Rattle & Ruin: “Fuck it. We ball.” Live Wire + Ripper — Ripper killed the final guard (12 + 17 damage).
  • Blackjack: Final salute — silhouette in the mist. Princess-carried Trisha to the rooftops. Tossed Caliban a dagger with an address.
  • Extraction: Bergatha used Potion of Emergency Escape (130-ft sprint). Party fled to Zellara’s estate.
