## Session Summary

The session opened mid-combat at the execution square, where the party fought Palace Guards and the Executioner to aid Blackjack's rescue of the scapegoat. Bergatha's Mist blanketed the battlefield in concealment, and her Floating Flood crushed guards' heads in a devastating sustained assault. Marwen used Whispers of Weakness twice — first on a Palace Guard to identify electricity weakness (leading to an Electric Arc crit for 25 damage), then on the Executioner to identify fire weakness, setting up a Blazing Bolt crit for 39 damage. Rattle magnetically stripped the guards of platinum and daggers, and Caliban's Shielding Taunt and Champion's Reaction kept allies alive. Blackjack escaped with the scapegoat, tossing Caliban a dagger with an address before leaping away.

With the city calming after the queen's retreat into her palace, the party enjoyed two weeks of downtime. Rattle built a criminal empire, recruiting were-rats as mafioso and earning the title "King of Shinies." Bergatha ran a burgeoning drug operation selling shiver. Marwen traded intelligence with the King of Spiders, learning that Rolth is meeting with a mysterious figure known only as "R.D.", has set up a new laboratory in a warehouse on the main island, and that Red Mantis assassins — roughly 30-36 of them — are operating in the city's sewers. Caliban identified the skull symbol spotted by Rattle's were-rat informants as the unholy symbol of Urgathoa.

The party followed Blackjack's address to Vencarlo Orisini's apartment, where the scapegoat had been dropped off. Marwen's blind sense motive told him Vencarlo might be Blackjack — though whether that's a genuine read or crit-fail false confidence remains unknown. The party escorted Trinia, Trisha, Beast, and Yargin to Harse for safekeeping. Upon return, Grey Maidens were patrolling the streets — the queen's new all-female elite guard under Sabina. The session ended with a dire hook: Grau brought the party to his sick niece Brianna in Trails End, and Bergatha's critical success on a society check connected the unknown disease to a quarantine ship that was sunk in the harbor days ago — a plague may already be spreading through Korvosa.

## Combat Encounters

### The Execution Rescue — 4 Rounds

**Enemies**: 4 Palace Guards (halberds, reactive strikes readied for Blackjack), 1 Executioner (axe, Mark for Death, grapple)
**Allies**: Blackjack (minor fast healing, Potion of Leaping, feint-based fighting)
**Terrain**: Execution platform with surrounding crowd; Grease (Marwen) and Mist (Bergatha) on the field

#### Round 1 — The Opening
- **NPCs**: Blackjack stabbed a Palace Guard (hit, modest damage). Executioner grappled Blackjack, missed a headbutt, used Mark for Death. Guards readied Reactive Strikes against Blackjack and attacked with halberds.
- **Rattle & Ruin**: Tandem Movement to reposition Rattle and Ruin. Act Together — Ruin's **dragon breath** (DC 20 Reflex): Executioner **failed** (2d6 poison damage), both Palace Guards succeeded. Protect Companion on Ripper. Ripper strode closer. Breath weapon recharge: 4 rounds.
- **Bergatha**: Cast **Mist** (2 actions) — all creatures in the area became concealed, obscuring the battlefield.
- **Marwen**: Cast **Grease** (2 actions) on two Palace Guards — both saved initially but must recheck each round. Attempted to Demoralize the Executioner — out of range. Switched to a Palace Guard — DM critically failed the save; guard became Frightened. (DM introduced **Galactic Hero Points** rule during this exchange — Marwen did not spend a hero point here.)
- **Caliban**: Grew Large from Fury Cocktail (10-ft reach). **Shielding Taunt** on Executioner + 2 guards. Struck a guard — missed (rolled 6).

#### Round 2 — Magnetic Heist and Lightning
- **Palace Guard**: Stepped toward Ruin, slashed — miss. Follow-up fist strike hit (passed concealment flat check).
- **Executioner**: Pulled knife from ground, stepped (passed Grease check), swung at Blackjack — missed due to concealment.
- **Palace Guard**: Fell **prone on Grease** (Reflex fail). Crawled out + stood up (2 actions wasted). Hit Ruin for **16 damage**.
- **Caliban**: Used **Champion's Reaction (Glimpse of Redemption)** on the 16-damage hit against Ruin — the Palace Guard chose to take **Enfeebled**, Ruin received **6 DR** (2 + Caliban's level).
- **Blackjack**: Broke out of grapple. Stepped east, nodded to party. Struck twice — both hit.
- **Rattle & Ruin**: Rattle moved (1 action). Act Together — Rattle used **Magnetic Attraction** (Staff of Metal): stripped guards of **25 platinum pieces**, daggers, and **6 pairs of manacles** (halberds too bulky). Ruin used **Poison Claw Strike** (hit with concealment complications). Ripper attacked — miss.
- **Bergatha**: Commanded **Lagunae** into water (1 action). Cast **Floating Flood** (2 actions) — initial hit on one Palace Guard (DC 20 Reflex, cold damage).
- **Marwen**: **Whispers of Weakness** on a Palace Guard — learned **electricity weakness** and Reflex as weakest save (became Cursebound 1). Cast **Electric Arc** (heightened cantrip) targeting 2 Palace Guards — one **crit failed**: **25 damage**. The other took 8 damage.
- **Caliban**: **Shielding Taunt** (3 enemies). **Hero Pointed** a strike — hit for **10 damage**. Attempted to **Demoralize the Executioner** — failed ("He does not care, he's too focused on Blackjack").

#### Round 3 — Fire and Flood
- **Palace Guards**: One at death's door continued fighting (taunted, off-guard). Hit Blackjack for 13 — Shielding Taunt **downgraded a crit to a hit**. Another tried to re-grapple Trisha — concealment check failed twice. A third attacked Caliban: enfeebled turned a crit into a hit, **14 damage** → **Caliban Shield Blocked to 7**. One guard slipped on **Grease** again.
- **Blackjack**: Strode (took **2 Reactive Strikes** — one hit, one missed due to concealment). **Feinted** a guard (success). First strike blocked by concealment. Final strike hit for **30 damage**.
- **Executioner**: Brought down axe — miss. Follow-up strike — miss. "Currently injured, but not bloodied."
- **Rattle & Ruin**: Act Together — Rattle shot **Acid Arrow** at Executioner (outside mist, no concealment): **hit for 13 damage + 1d6 persistent acid** ("clothes start to burn away, you can start to see the face behind the mask"). Ruin chomped a guard on death's door. Commanded **Ripper**: hit for 12 + 17 damage.
- **Bergatha**: Sustained **Floating Flood** through multiple guards in a devastating sequence:
  - Guard 1: Failed Reflex — **killed**. DM: *"You draw first blood, Virgitha."*
  - Guard 2: Failed Reflex — **22 damage**
  - Guard 3: Failed Reflex — near-max damage, killed
  - *"The roiling water crushes it like a soda can underwater... plops back up and crushes its head too."*
  - *"Bergeth is a fucking murderer."* → *"I'm just a woman. Oh, no."*
- **Marwen**: **Whispers of Weakness** on the **Executioner** — learned **fire weakness** (robes combustible) and Reflex weakest save. Cast **Blazing Bolt** (2-action, targets 2):
  - Executioner: Rolled poorly, **Hero Pointed** the reroll → **CRIT for 39 damage**. *"Third-degree burn spreading throughout his chest."*
  - Palace Guard: Hit for **18 damage** — guard at death's door.
- **Caliban**: **Shielding Taunt** on remaining enemies. Two strikes — hit for **19 damage**, putting a guard at death's door.

#### Round 4 — Cleanup and Escape
- **Blackjack**: Knocked out a Palace Guard near him. Addressed the party: *"I see that I am not the only true hero of Korvosa today."*
- **Rattle & Ruin**: *"Fuck it. We ball."* Act Together — Rattle used **Live Wire** (electricity) on a guard. Ruin struck (concealment issues). Commanded **Ripper**: hit for **12 damage**, struck again for **17 damage** — **Ripper killed the final guard**.
- **Blackjack**: Gave a final salute — silhouette in the mist. Princess-carried Trisha and leaped to the rooftops via Potion of Leaping. Tossed **Caliban** a dagger (handle-first) with a note containing an address.
- **Party extraction**: Caliban pocketed the dagger. Bergatha used **Potion of Emergency Escape** (+40 ft speed → 130 ft in 2 actions). Party fled back to Zellara's estate.

**Outcome**: All Palace Guards killed. Executioner severely damaged (39 crit + 13 acid + persistent acid + 2d6 poison — fate not explicitly stated). Scapegoat rescued by Blackjack.
**Hero Points spent**: Marwen 1 (Blazing Bolt reroll → crit), Caliban 1 (strike reroll → hit for 10)
**Loot from combat**: 25 platinum pieces, 6 pairs of manacles, daggers (Rattle via Magnetic Attraction)
